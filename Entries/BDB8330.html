BDB8330 [H8311]

Biblical Hebrew

[שָׂרַע] verb extend (Arabic point directly at, also enter upon a path (Frä213 thinks originally divide, then stretch out, make straight), projecting roof, long-nosed (torto naso ᵑ9 Lev 21:18 ); Ethiopic dispose, arrange; Sabean שרע arrangement, security HomChr 124; — but Aramaic explanation as mutilated Lev 21:18; Lev 22:23 PS2744, compare 𝔊 ὠτότμητος); —

Qal Passive participle שָׂרוּעַ extended, i.e. too long (in a limb or member), of man Lev 21:18, beast Lev 22:23 (see II. חרם ).
Hithpa`el stretch oneself: Infinitive construct הִשְׂתָּרֵעַ Isa 28:20 (see מַצַּע p. 427:a).
