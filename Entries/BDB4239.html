כָּתַב223 verb write (Late Hebrew id.; Phoenician כתב; Aramaic כְּתַב, Nabataean כתב, see especially Nö in EutNab. p. 43; thence Arabic Ethiopic book, DiLex. 852 (as loan-words, Frä249 DHMVOJ i (1887), 29); MV Buhl compare Arabic draw or sew together, conjoin (letters), etc., compare FlZMG 1873, 427) —

Qal Perfect ׳כ Josh 8:32 +, כָּתַבְתָּ Jer 36:6 +, suffix וּכְתַבְתָּם consecutive Deut 6:9; Deut 11:20, etc. (Perfect 27 t.); Imperfect יִכְתֹּב Isa 44:5, וַיִּכְתֹּב Exod 24:4 +, etc. (Imperfect 39 t.); Imperative כְּתֹב Isa 8:1 + 5 t., כְּתָבֿ Exod 34:27 + 2 t.; suffix כָּתְבָהּ Isa 30:8, כָּתְבֵם Prov 3:3; Prov 7:3 plural כִּתְבוּ Deut 31:9 + 3 t .; Infinitive absolute כָּתוֺכ Jer 32:44 cstr כְּתוֺב Ps 87:8, לִכְתֹּב Deut 31:24; Josh 18:8 suffix כָּתְבוֺ Jer 45:1 Participle active כֹּתֵב Jer 36:18, plural כֹּתְבִים Jer 32:12; Neh 10:1 passive (113 t.), כָּתוּב Josh 1:8 +, feminine כְּתוּבָה 2Sam 1:18 +, plural כְּתוּבִים 1Kgs 15:7 +, כְּתֻבִים 1Kgs 11:41 +; feminine plural כְּתוּבוֺת 2Chr 34:24 —
1. write:
\ \ a. with accusative, words Exod 34:27, commandments Exod 24:12, etc.; accusative of congnate meaning with verb מכתב Exod 39:30, compare כַּמִּכְתָּב Deut 10:4 with כְּ, giving purport of writing, also Esth 8:8 but object also a book, סֵפֶר Exod 32:32; Deut 24:1 מְגִלָּה Jer 38:6, compare participle pass, וְהִיא כְּתוּבָה פָּנִים וְאָחוֺר Ezek 2:10 ( of מגלה; i.e. written on both sides); a letter, סֵפָר 2Sam 11:14; 2Kgs 10:1 + 4 t., אִגֶּרֶת 2Chr 30:1 a divorce-certificate סֵפֶר כְּרִיתֻת Deut 24:1, etc.; even לֻחֹת כְּתֻבִים tablets inscribed by the finger of God Exod 31:18 compare Deut 9:10 מִשְּׁנֵי עֶבְרֵיהֶם ׳כ ׳ל Exod 32:15 tables inscribed on both sides (compare Ezek 2:10 above) Here belongs (probably) ׳וְזֶה יִכְתֹּב יָדוֺ לי Isa 44:5 and this one shall inscribe his hand, 'Yahweh's', so ᵐ5 Hi Kn Che Br Du Di, Ges Ew De, shall sign (with) his hand unto ׳י.
\ \ b. more often write something on, or in (עַלֿ 85 t., בְ 44 t., אֶלֿ 3 t.) a tablet, roll, book, etc. (object various, as above): — (I) עַלֿ ׳כ followed by סֵפֶר = book Deut 17:18 + 55 t., especially in phrase ׳כתובים עלסֿפר דברי וגו 1Kgs 11:41 + often Kings Chronicles; עַלמְֿגִּלָּה ׳כ Jer 36:2; Jer 36:28; Jer 36:32; Jer 38:29 עַללֿוּח(ותׅ ׳כ Exod 34:1 + 7 t., עַלאֶֿבֶן Deut 27:3 + 2 t., עַלגּֿלָּיוֺן Isa 8:1 עַלמְֿזֻזוֺת בַּיִת Deut 6:9; Deut 11:20 עַלמַֿטֶּה on a rod Num 17:17; Num 17:18 עַלעֵֿץ Ezek 37:16 (twice in verse), עַלצִֿיץ (high priest's plate) Exod 39:30 even (only Chronicles) עַלֿ followed by name of book or its contents: — עַלדִּֿבְרֵי נָתָן ׳כ 2Chr 9:29 compare 2Chr 33:19 ׳עַלמִֿדְרַשׁ וגו 2Chr 24:27 עַלהַֿקִּינוֺת 2Chr 35:25 figurative, עַללִֿבָּם ׳כ Jer 31:83 I will write them upon their heart; compare עַללֿוּחַ לִבֶּ֑ךָ ׳כ Prov 3:3. (2) אֶלֿ ׳כ appears for עַלֿ ׳כ only Jer 36:2 compare Ezek 2:10; Jer 51:60. (3) בְּ ׳כ followed by ספר = book Josh 24:26 (E) + 35 t.; = letter, bill, deed, 1Kgs 21:9 + 5 t.; בִּמְגִלַּת סֵפֶר כָּתוּב עָלָ֑י Ps 40:8 it is prescribed to me, על as 2Kgs 22:13 (
N.B. עלֿ ׳כ not used with סֵפֶר = letter, and בְּ ׳כ not used with לוּחַ, אבן or עֵץ).
\ \ c. other uses of perp. with ׳כ are: — בְּ instrumental, בְּחֶרֶט אֱנוֺשׁ ׳כ Isa 8:1, בְּעֵט בַּרְזֶל ׳כ Jer 17:1 כְּתֻבִים בְּאֶצְבַּע אֱלֹהִים Deut 9:10; Exod 31:18 אֶלֿ ׳כ unto, of person to whom letter, etc., is addressed 2Sam 11:14; 2Kgs 10:6; Esth 9:13 עַלֿ substantive for אֶלֿ (late) 2Chr 30:1; Ezra 4:7; Esth 8:8 the fuller construction is ספר וישׁלח (אֶלֿׅ ׳כ 2Kgs 10:1; Esth 8:10; Esth 9:20 +; אֶלֿ ׳כ write down for the benefit or use of some one, Judg 8:14 לְ to or for, Deut 17:18; Deut 24:1 +; note especially אֶכְתָּו֯בלֿוֺ רֻבֵּו֯ תּוֺרָתִי Hos 8:12, i.e. either, I write (keep writing) for them ever so many (We, רֹב, compare Ew and most רִבּוֺ(אׅ a myriad) of my directions, or 'Though I write,' 'Were I to write,' etc.; ׳כ followed by ל with infinitive of purpose 2Chr 32:17; Esth 8:5 with מִן in phrase ׳מִפִּי פ ׳כ write from the mouth of any one i.e. from dictation, Jer 36:2; Jer 36:32; Jer 38:6; Jer 38:17; Jer 38:27; Jer 45:1.
2. = write down describe in writing, אֶתהָֿאָרֶץ ׳כ Judg 18:4; Judg 18:6; Judg 18:8 (3 t. in verse); Judg 18:9.
3. = register enroll Isa 10:19 (= record the number), 1Chr 24:6 especially passive הַכְּתוּבִים Num 11:26 those enrolled compare Neh 12:22 בְּשֵׁמוֺת ׳הַכּ 1Chr 4:41 those recorded by name; in prediction, אֶתהָֿאִישׁ ׳כ הַזֶּה עֲרִירִי Jer 22:30 register this man as childless; with eschatological reference, כָּלהַֿכָּתוּב לַחַיִּים Isa 4:3 all those enrolled (i.e. appointed) unto life; and, more explicitly, כָּלהַֿנִּמְצָא כָּתוּב בַּסֵּפֶר Dan 12:1 יִסְפֹּר בִּכְתוֺבּ עַמִּים ׳י Ps 87:6 ׳י shall reckon, when he registers (note absence of suffix) the peoples, etc.
4. = decree, תִּכְתֹּב עָלַי מְרֹרוֺת Job 13:26 thou (׳י) decrest against me bitter things. Niph`al (chiefly late, especially Esther), Imperfect יִכָּתֵב Esth 1:19 +, 3 feminine singular תִּכָּ֫תֶב זֹאת Ps 102:19 3 masculine plural יִכָּתֵ֑בוּ Jer 17:13 + 3 t., יִכָּֽתְבוּן Job 19:23 Participle נִכְתָּב Esth 3:12 + 2 t.; —
1. be written, subject words Job 19:23, book Mal 3:16 be written בְּסֵפֶר Esth 2:23; Esth 9:23, in, or among ב the laws Esth 1:19 once, with עַלֿ, עַלסִֿפְרְךָ כֻּלָּם יִכָּתֵ֑בוּ Ps 139:16 in thy ׳י's book all of them (my members) are written, i.e. written down, recorded (see below); absolute כְּתָב אֲשֶׁר נִכְתָּב בְּשֵׁם הַמֶּלֶךְ Esth 8:8 a writing which is written in the king's name, compare (impersonal) Esth 3:12 impersonal also וַיִּכָּתֵב כְּכָלאֲֿשֶׁר צִוָּה Esth 3:12; Esth 8:9 ׳יִכָּ followed by לְ = for Ps 102:19 followed by לְ + infinitive (really = a subject-cl., — the contents of the letter) Esth 3:9; Esth 8:5.
2. be written down, recorded, Ezra 8:34 enrolled, בִּכְתָב בית ישׂראל לֹא יִכָּתֵ֑בוּ Ezek 13:9 in the enrolement of the house of Israel they are not enrolled (eschatolog.); וְעִםצַֿדִּיקִים אַליִֿכָּתֵ֑בוּ Ps 69:29 (|| חַיִּים יִמָּחוּ מִסֵּפֶר) — בָּאָרֶץ יִכָּתֵ֑בוּ Jer 17:13 is difficult; Gie reads, plausibly, מֵאֶרֶץ יִכָּרֵ֑תוּ (compare Ps 34:17; Ps 101:8, etc.) Pi`el frequently, Perfect and Participle, only כִּתֵּ֑בוּוּמְכַתְּבִים עָמָל Isa 10:1 and busy writers that make a business of writing oppression (i.e. register unjust sentences, compare Qal 4; || הַחֹקֲקִים חִקֲקֵיאָֿוֶן).
