BDB2083 [H1928]

Biblical Hebrew

הֲדַרְעֶ֫זֶר proper name, masculine a king of Aram (צוֺבָה) defeated by David 2Sam 10:16 (twice in verse); 2Sam 10:19 = 1Chr 19:16; 1Chr 19:19 also 1Chr 18:3; 1Chr 18:5; 1Chr 18:8; 1Chr 18:9; 1Chr 18:10 & הֲדַרְעָ֑זֶר 1Chr 18:7 1Chr 18:10 all erroneous for הֲדַדְעֶזֶד (q. v.), although 𝔊 throughout Ἁδρααζαρ.
