Biblical Hebrew

תְּכוּנָה noun feminine arrangement, preparation, fixed place; —

1. arrangement, disposition תְּכוּנָתוֺ Ezek 43:11 , i.e. the arrangement of the house (strike out Co with 𝔊 Manuscripts)
2. preparation, וְאֵין קֶצֶה לַתְּכוּנָה Nah 2:10 and no end to the preparation (i.e. things prepared, supply, store).
3. fixed place, i.e. dwelling-place, of God (= מָכוֺן) תְּכוּנָתוֺ Job 23:3.
