BDB5955 [H5818]

Biblical Hebrew

עֻזִּיָּהוּ, עֻזִּיָּה proper name, masculine (my strength is ׳ י; compare Old Hebrew עזיו Lzb338); —

1. Οζειας, but also Αζαριας: king of Judah = ׃עְַזַרְיָהוּ
a. עֻזִּיָּהוּ 2Kgs 15:32 ; 2Kgs 15:34 ; 2Chr 26:1; 2Chr 26:3; 2Chr 26:8; 2Chr 26:9; 2Chr 26:11 ; 2Chr 26:14 ; 2Chr 26:18 (twice in verse); 2Chr 26:19; 2Chr 26:21; 2Chr 26:22; 2Chr 26:23; 2Chr 27:2; Isa 1:1; Isa 6:1; Isa 7:1.
b. עֻזִּיָּה 2Kgs 15:13 ; 2Kgs 15:30 ; Hos 1:1; Amos 1:1; Zech 14:5.
2. עֻזִּיָּהוּ father of an officer of David 1Chr 27:25.
3. עֻזִּיָּה Levite name 1Chr 6:9.
4. id.: priest with foreign wife Ezra 10:21.
5. id.: name in Judah Neh 11:4 (also Neh 11:5 𝔊 for חזיה). — See also יַעֲזִיָּהוּ.
