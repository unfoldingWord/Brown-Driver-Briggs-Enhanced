Biblical Hebrew

נַחְשׁוֺן proper name, masculine son of Aminadab, of Judah, brother-in-law of Aaron Exod 6:23; Num 1:7; Num 2:3; Num 7:12; Num 7:17; Num 10:14 (all P ), Ruth 4:20 (twice in verse); 1Chr 2:10; 1Chr 2:11 𝔊 Ναασσων (DHMZMG xxxvii (1888), 15 compare Sabean tribal name נחסן).
