BDB2362 [H2146]

Biblical Hebrew

זִכָּרוֺן, זִכָּרֹן noun masculine Eccl 1:11 memorial, remembrance (compare LgBN 199, 200) — absolute זִכָּרוֺן Josh 4:7 +; זִכָּרֹן Exod 28:12 (twice in verse); Exod 28:29 construct זִכְרוֺן Eccl 1:11 + 2 t.; suffix זִכְרוֺנֵךְ Isa 57:8 plural הַזִּכְרֹנוֺת Esth 6:1 suffix זִכְרֹנֵיכֶם Job 13:12 —

1. memorial, reminder:
a. memorial-day Exod 12:14 ( P ).
b. memorial-usage Exod 13:9 ( J E ).
c. memorial-objects, altar-plates Num 17:5 ( P ); stones in Jordan Josh 4:7 ( J E ); crowns in temple Zech 6:14 ׳ ז in Isa 57:8 is symbol of strange god (Di), or perhaps phallus-image, as sign of harlot (Che), compare > Du (who proposes זְכָרוֺן, from זָכָר).
d. memorial-record; in a book Exod 17:14 ( E ); compare סֵפֶר זִכָּרוֺן Mal 3:16, הַזִּכְרֹנות דברי הימים ׳ ס Esth 6:1 memorial, as proof of citizenship Neh 2:20 (|| חֵלֶק, צְדָקָה); reminder of Israel, ׳ לִפְנֵי י, of כֶּסֶף הַכִּפֻּרִים Exod 30:16 spoils of war Num 31:54 inscribed stones of ephod Exod 28:12; Exod 28:29, called ׳ אַבְנֵי ז Exod 28:12 = Exod 39:7 blowing of trumpets Num 10:10 (foregoing all P ), compare זִכְרוֺן תְּרוּעַה Lev 23:24 ( H ; where, however, no ׳ לפני י, see Di); מִנְחַת (ה)זכרון Num 5:15; Num 5:18 ( P ); memorial-sentence, apophthegm Job 13:12.
זִכְרֹנֵיכֶם מִשְׁלֵיֿ אֵפֶר Job 13:12 your memorial words are ashen sayings (i.e. worthless), compare Bu Du; Margolis (privately) suggests that 𝔊 seems to have read יִמָּשֵׁל לְאֵפֶר [? < רָנְּכֶם ] רָנְיְכֶם your exultation shall be like ashes.
2. remembrance Eccl 1:11 (construct before preposition Ges«GKC:130»§ 130, 1), Eccl 1:11; Eccl 2:16.
