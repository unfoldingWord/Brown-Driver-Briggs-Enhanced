Biblical Hebrew

[בָּזַר] verb scatter (Arabic , Aramaic בְּדַר) —

Qal Imperfect Dan 11:24 בִּזָּה וְשָׁלָל וּרְכוּשׁ לָהֶם יִבְז֑וֺר plunder and spoil and possessions he will scatter among them (subject Antiochus Epiph.)
Pi`el Perfect בִּזַּר, id., Ps 68:31, but read Imperative (בַּזַּר) בַּזֵּר ( 𝔊 ᵑ6 ᵑ9 so most; De follows MT), subject reference to ׳ י, object עַמִּים.
