Biblical Hebrew

אֲרִיאֵל

1. proper name, feminine (probably, see Ew) Ariel ( lioness of El) name applied to Jerusalem Isa 29:1; Isa 29:2 (twice in verse); Isa 29:7 (so Ges Ew Che Di and others; Tagum De Brd Or and others say hearth of El, compare אֲרִאֵיל).
2. proper name, masculine a chief man among returning exiles Ezra 8:16.
3. אֲרִאֵל מוֺאָב 2Sam 23:20 = ׳ אֲרִיאֵל מ 1Chr 11:26 taken by 𝔊 RV DrSm and others as proper name, masculine, read two sons of Ariel of Moab; RSSemitic i. 469 compare אראל MI12 , altar-hearth (so Sm & So, and DrSm xci, see below אֲרִיאֵיל) and translation מוֺאָב ׳ א altar-hearths of Moab.
4. Ezek 43:15 & Qr Ezek 43:15 Ezek 43:16 see below אֲרִיאֵיל.
Du Isa 29:1 (q. v.) makes
1. = אֲרִיאֵיל, from אָרָה + ל afformative, hearth (not + אֵל hearth of El).
