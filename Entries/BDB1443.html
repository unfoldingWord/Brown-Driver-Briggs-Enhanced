BDB1443 [H1382]

Biblical Hebrew

גִּבְלִי adjective [of a people] of foregoing with article = collectivea noun Josh 13:5 והארץ הַגִּבְלִי, but read ׳ גְבוּל הַגּ, compare Di. 1Kgs 5:32 הַגִּבְלִים (but probably text error; Th reads וַיַּגְבִּלוּם and they bordered them, made a border for them (the stones); 𝔊 ἔβαλον, compare also Klo).
