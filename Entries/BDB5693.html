2. legal document, כְּרִיתֻת ׳ ס certificate of divorce Deut 24:1; Deut 24:3 and (figurative) Jer 3:8 ; Isa 50:1 הַמִּקְנָה ׳ ס deed of purchase Jer 32:11; Jer 32:12 (strike out article), Jer 32:12 Jer 32:14 Jer 32:16, compare ׳ בַּסּ Jer 32:10, הַסְּפָרִים Jer 32:14 (si vera lectio; strike out ᵐ5 Gie compare Gf), סֵפֶר Jer 32:16b; Jer 32:44 ׳ ס = indictment Job 31:35 (object of כָּתַב; ׳ כָּתַב בְּ = sign Jer 32:10; Jer 32:12; Jer 32:44 ).
3. book, or scroll, in which something is written to preserve it for future use Exod 17:14 ( E ), Josh 18:9 ( J E ), 1Sam 10:25; Isa 30:8; Job 19:23 +; in form of roll, compare וְנָגֹ֫לוּ כַּסֵּפֶר הַשָּׁמַיִם Isa 34:4, הֶחָתוּם ׳ הַסּ Isa 29:11; Isa 29:12 (see Jer 36, etc., below); particular:
