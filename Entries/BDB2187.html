Biblical Hebrew

הַמְּדָתָא proper name, masculine father of Haman (Persian mâh , moon (mâha, month, SpiegAPK 236) + data, given? so (written hamaodata Öttli; 𝔊 Ἁμαδαθου (always genitive); in Greek perhaps Μαδάτης [Μαδάτας, Μαδέτης], compare XenCyr, see 3. 41 Diodxvii,67 PottZMG 1859, 424); — הַמְּדָתָא הָֽאֲגָגִי Esth 3:1; Esth 3:10; Esth 8:5; Esth 9:24 without האגגי Esth 9:10.
