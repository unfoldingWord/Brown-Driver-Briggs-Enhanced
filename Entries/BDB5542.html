BDB5542 [H5489]

Biblical Hebrew

II. סוּף proper name [of a location] (si vera lectio) named in defining location of Deuteronomic law-giving Deut 1:1 (מול סוּף, where מוֺל by dissimil. for מוּל); but read perhaps מִיַּם סוּף ( 𝔊 πλησίον ρῆς ἐρυθρᾶς, 𝔊 L + θαλάσσης, ᵑ9 contra mare rubrum), see I. סוּף .
