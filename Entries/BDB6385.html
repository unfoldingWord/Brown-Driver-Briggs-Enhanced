Biblical Hebrew

מַעֲרָכָה noun feminine row, rank, battle-line; —

1.
a. ׳ מ battle-line 1Sam 4:2; 1Sam 4:12; 1Sam 4:16 (twice in verse); ׳ וַיָּ֑רָץ הַמּ 1Sam 17:22 and he ran to the battle-line, so 1Sam 17:28 (on reference here to Israel's line of. HPS), ׳ לפְרַאת מ ׳ מ 1Sam 17:21 ׳ עֹדְרֵי מ 1Chr 12:39 ( van d. H. v.1Chr 12:38); nearly= battle הַחַיִל ׳ הַאיּצֵא אֶלהַֿמּ 1Sam 17:20.
b. plural ranks = army; construct ׳ מַעַרְכוֺת יִשׂ 1Sam 17:10; 1Sam 17:45, ׳ מַעַרְכֹת יִשׂ 1Sam 17:8 ׳ מ אֱלּהִים חַיִּים 1Sam 17:26 1Sam 17:36 פְּלִשְׁתִּים ׳ מ 1Sam 23:3 and 1Sam 17:23 Qr , so 𝔊 A (v. omitted by B ) Vulgate ᵑ7 ; singular 𝔊 L ᵑ6 (> Kt מערות).
2. נֵרוֺת הַמַּעֲרָכָה Exod 39:37 lamps of the row, i.e. arranged in a row.
3. order, arrangement: ׳ בַּמּ Judg 6:26 in the (proper or usual) arrangement of an alter, compare עָרַךְ 1.a. Num 23:4 of altar.
