Biblical Hebrew

יוֺעָשׁ proper name, masculine 𝔊 Ἰωας (׳ י hath aided (?; compare Arabic ); compare Sabean אלעֿותֿ DHM in MV) —

1. a Benjamite, son of Becher 1Chr 7:8.
2. an officer under David 1Chr 27:28.
