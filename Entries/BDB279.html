Biblical Hebrew

אַחְלַי Ps 119:5 and אַחֲלֵי 2Kgs 5:3 (perhaps from אָח III. and לַי = Aramaic לְוַי = ׃לוּ the varying punctuation is due doubtless to the word being treated by the punctuators as a substantive = wish , with suffix & in construct stative, 'my wish is that,' etc.; compare Ki on Ps 119 תְּחִנּוֺתַי וּבַקָּשׁוֺתַי), ah that! ( 𝔊 ο[φελον).
