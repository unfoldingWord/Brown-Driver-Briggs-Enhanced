Biblical Hebrew

חֶצְרוֺן, חֶצְרֹן proper name [of a location and person] (compare Sabean חצֿרן proper name, of a location or tribe HalNo. 661 DHMZMG 1883,15) —

1. proper name [of a location] 𝔊 Ασωρων, Ασερων, Εσρωμ:
a. חֶצְרוֺן Josh 15:3 place in extreme south of Judah, + אַדָּ֫רָה = חֲצַראַֿדָּר (q. v.) Num 34:4.
b. קְרִיּוֺת חֶצְרוֺן הִיא חָצוֺר Josh 15:25 another town of Judah in south.
2. proper name, masculine 𝔊 Ασρωμ(ν), Εσρων, etc.:
a. חֶצְרֹן son of Reuben Gen 46:9; Exod 6:14; Num 26:6 = חֶצְרוֺן 1Chr 5:3.
b. son of Peres and grandson of Judah Gen 46:12; Num 26:21 = חֶצְרוֺן Ruth 4:18; Ruth 4:19; 1Chr 2:5; 1Chr 2:9; 1Chr 2:18; 1Chr 2:21; 1Chr 2:24 (twice in verse); 1Chr 2:25 ; 1Chr 4:1.
