2. last king of Israel 2Kgs 15:30; 2Kgs 17:1; 2Kgs 17:3; 2Kgs 17:4; 2Kgs 17:6; 2Kgs 18:1; 2Kgs 18:9; 2Kgs 18:10, 𝔊 Ωσηε.

4. an Ephraimite chief under David 1Chr 27:20, 𝔊 Ωση(ε).
5. a chief under Nehemiah Neh 10:24, 𝔊 Ωσηθα, Ωσηε.
