הוֺשֵׁעַ proper name, masculine (salvation; on form, compare הַשְׁמֵד הַמְשֵׁל on pronunciation see HptZA ii, 261 Anm. 2 JagerBAS i, 468) —

1. original name of Joshua according to Num 13:8; Num 13:16 ( P ), ᵐ5 Αυση, compare Deut 32:44 ( J ; probably error, see Dr), ᵐ5 Ιησους, see יְהוֺשׁוּעַ above
2. last king of Israel 2Kgs 15:30; 2Kgs 17:1; 2Kgs 17:3; 2Kgs 17:4; 2Kgs 17:6; 2Kgs 18:1; 2Kgs 18:9; 2Kgs 18:10, ᵐ5 Ωσηε.
3. the prophet Hosea Hos 1:1; Hos 1:2 (twice in verse), ᵐ5 Ωσηε.
4. an Ephraimite chief under David 1Chr 27:20, ᵐ5 Ωση(ε).
5. a chief under Nehemiah Neh 10:24, ᵐ5 Ωσηθα, Ωσηε.
