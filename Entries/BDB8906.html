BDB8906 [H8162]

Biblical Hebrew

שַׁעַטְנֵז noun masculine mixed stuff (perhaps of Egyptian origin; Kn derives from Coptic saht, woven, + nudj, false (PeyronLex 224. 133), and thinks originally שַׁעַטְנֹז; 𝔊 has κίβδηλος spurious); — a kind of cloth forbidden for garments; defined Deut 22:11 by צֶמֶר וּפִשְׁתִּים יַחְדָּו, and Lev 19:19 ( H ) by כִּלְאַיִם.

שֵׁעִיר see שֵׂעִיר .
