I. מַחְלִי proper name, masculine

1. Levite, son of Merari Exod 6:19; Num 3:20; 1Chr 6:4; 1Chr 6:14; 1Chr 23:21 (twice in verse); 1Chr 24:26; 1Chr 24:28; Ezra 8:18 (probably), 𝔊 Μοολλει.
2. son of Mushi, and grandson of Merari 1Chr 6:32; 1Chr 23:23; 1Chr 24:30, 𝔊 Μοολει, Μοολ(λ)ι.
