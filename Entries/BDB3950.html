Biblical Hebrew

יְכוֺנְיָה proper name, masculine (׳ י is firm, enduring); — Jer 27:20 = יְכָנְ֫יָהוּ Jer 24:1 = יְכָנְיָה Jer 28:4; Jer 29:2; 1Chr 3:16; 1Chr 3:17; Esth 2:6 = כָּנְיָ֫הוּ Jer 22:24; Jer 22:28; Jer 37:1 ( 𝔊 Ιεχονιας throughout), all of last king but one of Judah, see יְהוֺיָכִין above
