III. יֶ֫תֶר proper name, masculine (abundance; on form and meaning of this and the following proper name see LagBN 52, 198) —

1. father of Moses' wife Exod 4:18 a ( E ; probably text error, elsewhere יִתְרוֺ , q. v.)
2. eldest son of Gideon Judg 8:20, 𝔊 Ιεθερ.
3. father of Amasa 1Kgs 2:5; 1Kgs 2:32; 1Chr 2:17, 𝔊 Ιεθερ; = יִתְרָא 2Sam 17:25, 𝔊 Ιοθερ, Ιοθορ; 𝔊 L Ιεθερ.
4. men of Judah, 𝔊 Ιεθερ:
a. 1Chr 2:32 (twice in verse).
b. 1Chr 4:17.
5. a man of Asher 1Chr 7:38 ( = יִתְרָן 1Chr 7:37), 𝔊 Ιεθερ, A Ιεθηρ, 𝔊 L [1Chr 7:39 ] Εθραν.
