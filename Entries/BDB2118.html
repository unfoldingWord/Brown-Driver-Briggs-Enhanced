Biblical Hebrew

יְהוֺרָם, יוֺרָם, יֹרָם proper name, masculine (׳ י is exalted; compare מַלְכִּירָם; further, Phoenician בעלרם, רמבעל; Sabean אלרם DHMZMG 1876, 686) —

1. ׳ יְהוֺ ( 𝔊 Ἰωραμ) king of Judah, son of Jehoshaphat 1Kgs 22:51; 2Kgs 1:17; 2Kgs 8:16; 2Kgs 8:25; 2Kgs 8:29; 2Kgs 12:19; 2Chr 21:1; 2Chr 21:3; 2Chr 21:4; 2Chr 21:5; 2Chr 21:9; 2Chr 21:16; 2Chr 22:1; 2Chr 22:6; 2Chr 22:11 = ׳ יוֺ 2Kgs 8:21; 2Kgs 8:23; 2Kgs 8:24; 2Kgs 11:2; 1Chr 3:11.
2. ׳ יְהוֺ ( 𝔊 Ἰωραμ) king of Israel son of Ahab 2Kgs 1:17; 2Kgs 3:1; 2Kgs 3:6; 2Kgs 9:15; 2Kgs 9:17; 2Kgs 9:21 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 9:22; 2Kgs 9:23; 2Chr 22:5; 2Chr 22:6; 2Chr 22:7 = ׳ יוֺ 2Kgs 8:16; 2Kgs 8:25; 2Kgs 8:28 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 8:29 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 9:14 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 9:16 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 9:29; 2Chr 22:5; 2Chr 22:7.
3. ׳ יְהוֺ priest in time of Jehoshaphat 2Chr 17:8 ( 𝔊 Ἰωραμ, Ἰωραν).
4. ׳ יוֺ son of Tou, king of Hamath 2Sam 8:10 ( 𝔊 Ἰεδδουραν) probably error for הֲדוֺרָם || 1Chr 18:10.
5. יֹרָם a Levite 1Chr 26:25 ( 𝔊 Ἰωραμ).
