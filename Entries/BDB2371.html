I. [זָלַל] verb shake (Arabic slip, agitate, shake; be agitated, quake, especially of earth-quake) —

Niph`al Perfect נָזֹ֫לּוּ shake, quake Isa 63:19 of mountains at ׳ י's presence (Ges De Che Di Du; 𝔊 ᵑ9 as if from נזל flow down); so Isa 64:2 (but here probably not original Che Di Du); read נָזֹלּוּ likewise Judg 5:5 ( 𝔊 Thes Stu Be Bl MV SS; ᵑ0 נָָֽזְלוּ, from נזל).
