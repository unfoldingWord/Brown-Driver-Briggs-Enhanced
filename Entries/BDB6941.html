צֶ֫דֶק noun masculine Isa 1:21 rightness, righteousness; — ׳ צ Lev 19:36 + 87 t.; צִדְקִי Isa 41:10 + 8 t., etc.; —

1. what is right, just, normal; rightness, justness, of weights and measures, אֵיפָה , אֶבֶן שְׁלֵמָה וָצֶדֶק Deut 25:15 a perfect and a just weight, ephah; ׳ מאֹזְנֵי צ; ׳ אַבְנֵי צ, ׳ אֵיפַת צ, ׳ הִין צ, ׳ בַּת צ Lev 19:36 ( H ) Job 31:6 ; Ezek 45:10 ׳ מַעְגְּלֵי צ right paths Ps 23:3 ׳ זִבְחֵי צ right peace-offerings Deut 33:19; Ps 4:6; Ps 51:21.
2. righteousness, in government:
a. of judges, rulers, kings, ׳ שׁפט בצ Lev 19:15( H ); ׳ שׁפט צ Deut 1:16; Prov 31:9 ׳ משׁפט צ Deut 16:18 ׳ רדף צ Deut 16:20 ׳ חוֺקֵק צ Prov 8:15 ׳ מלך לצ Isa 32:1 ׳ דבּר צ Ps 58:2 also Prov 25:5; Ps 94:15; Eccl 5:7.
b. of law, as מִשְׁפָּטִים Isa 58:2; Ps 119:7; Ps 119:62 ; Ps 119:75; Ps 119:106; Ps 119:160 ( ᵐ5 , but ᵑ0 מִשְׁפַּט), Ps 119:164 as עֵדוֺת Ps 119:138 Ps 119:144 as מִצְוֺר Ps 119:172.
c. of Davidic king, Messiah Isa 11:4; Isa 11:5; Isa 16:5; Ps 45:5, ׳ דין דצ Ps 72:2.
d. of Jerusalem, as seat of just government, עִיר הַצֶּדֶק Isa 1:26 city of righteousness; ׳ נְוֵה צ Jer 31:23; Jer 50:7 (possibly these reflect an original god צֶדֶק, see מַלְכִּי צֶדֶק); יָלִין בָּהּ ׳ צ Isa 1:21 righteousness used to lodge in her; compare ׳ מְקוֺם הצ Eccl 3:16 the place of righteousness.
e. of God's attribute as sovereign Job 36:3, husband of Israel Hos 2:21 ׳ צ his personified agent Ps 85:11; Ps 85:12; Ps 85:14, foundation of his throne Ps 89:15 = Ps 97:2 in his government Ps 9:9; Ps 65:6; Ps 96:13 = Ps 98:9, promise Isa 45:19, administration of justice Job 8:3; Jer 11:20; Ps 7:18; Ps 48:11; Ps 50:6 = Ps 97:6, vindication of his people Ps 9:5; Ps 35:24; Ps 35:28 raising up Cyrus Isa 45:13, calling his servant Isa 42:6 אֱלֹהֵי צִדְקִי Ps 4:2 God of my righteousness (who vindicates me); his ׳ צ is everlasting Ps 119:142.
3. righteousness, justice, in a case or cause Job 6:29; Job 8:6; Job 29:14; Ps 35:27; Isa 59:4 God ׳ שׁפט כצ Ps 7:9 judges according to righteousness; ׳ גמל כצ Ps 18:21 ׳ השׁיב כצ Ps 18:25 ׳ שׁמע צ Ps 17:1 ׳ הוציא צ Ps 37:6.
4. rightness, in speech, ׳ דבּר צ Ps 52:5 (opposed to שֶׁקֶר); ׳ יַגִּיד צ Prov 12:17 כל אמרי פי ׳ בצ Prov 8:8 ׳ שִׂפְתֵי צ Prov 16:13 .
5. righteousness, as ethically right Job 35:2; Ps 17:15; Ps 45:8; Prov 1:3; Prov 2:9; Eccl 7:15; Jer 22:13; Ezek 3:20; Hos 10:12 (read ׳ פְּרִי צ, so ᵐ5 We Now); עלמים ׳ צ Dan 9:24 ׳ עשׂה צ Isa 64:4; Ps 119:121 ׳ פעל צ Ps 15:2 ׳ למד צ Isa 26:6; Isa 26:10 ׳ בקּשׁ צ Zeph 2:3 ׳ רדף צ Isa 51:1 ׳ ידע צ Isa 51:7.
6. righteousness as vindicated, justification in controversy with enemies and troubles, deliverance, victory, prosperity:
a. of God as covenant-keeping, in redemption, בִּימִין צִדְקִי Isa 41:10, || יֵשַׁע Isa 45:8; Isa 51:5, compare Isa 42:21 ׳ בשּׂר צ Ps 40:10 ׳ אִמְרַת צ Ps 119:123.
b. in name יהוה צִדְקֵנוּ, of Messianic king (vindicating people's cause and giving victory) Jer 23:6 of city Jer 33:16.
c. of people as enjoying ׳ צ of salvation, || יְשׁוּעָה Isa 62:1 || כָּבוֺד Isa 58:8; Isa 62:2 ׳ כהנים יִלְבְּשׁוּ צ Ps 132:9 ׳ שַׁעֲרֵי צ Ps 118:19 ׳ אֵילֵי הצ Isa 61:3.
d. of Cyrus, יִקְרָאֵהוּ לְרַגְלוֺ ׳ צ Isa 41:2 (who) in victory calleth him at every step (CheHpt on whose steps attends victory, so Di Du).
