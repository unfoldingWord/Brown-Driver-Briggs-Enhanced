קָרֹב, קָרוֺב78 adjective near; — absolute קָרֹב Exod 12:4 +, וֺב- 2Sam 19:48 +; plural קְרֹבִים 1Kgs 8:59, קְרוֺבִים Isa 33:13 +; feminine plural קְרֹבוֺת Ezek 22:5, etc.; — near (often opposed to רָחוֺק):

1. of city Gen 19:20 (+ לָנוּס שָׁ֫מָּה), Jer 48:24, ׳ הַקּ Deut 21:3 the nearest (+אֶל), compare (of elders) Deut 21:6 of land 1Kgs 8:46 = 2Chr 6:36 road Exod 13:17 = contiguous, כֶּרֶם 1Kgs 21:2 (+ אֵצֶל בֵּיתִי).
2. of person:
a. + אֶל person Gen 45:10; Deut 13:8; Deut 22:2; 1Chr 12:41 ( van d. H. v40); אֶל ׳ הַקּ Exod 12:4 the nearest to; with suffix קְרֹבוֺ Exod 32:27, i.e. his neighbour.
b. absolute (opposed to or + רחוק, oft,= near and far, i.e. all without distinction), Ezek 6:12; Isa 57:19; Prov 27:10 plural Isa 33:13; Jer 25:26; Dan 9:7; Esth 9:20; Exod 22:5 ׳ ק of distress, personified, Ps 22:12.; — Ezek 23:5; Ezek 23:12 Co Berthol read קְרוּאִים famous; Toy רַבִּים (compare Jer 39:13); Hpt in Toy Krae conjectures קְרוֺדִים (Assyrian ‡urâ du , warrior).
c. of near relationship, + אֶל person2 Sam Jer 19:43; Lev 21:2; Lev 21:3; Lev 25:25 (all H ), Num 27:11 ( P ); ל pers Neh 13:4; Ruth 2:20 + מִן compare Ruth 3:12 of intimacy, קְרֹנוֺ Ps 15:3 (|| רֵעֵהוּ), קְרוֺבַי Job 19:14 (|| מְיֻדָּעַי) Ps 38:12 (|| אֹהֲנַי וְרֵעַי); near, in office, Esth 1:14 .
d. near to ׳ עַם קְרֹבוֺ י Ps 148:14 (Ri Bae קְרכָו the people of those near him).
e. near to ׳ י in ceremonial function, with אֶל Ezek 43:19 , with ל Ezek 42:13 , suffix Lev 10:3.
f. of God (׳ י), + אֶל person Deut 4:7; Deut 30:14 ל pers Ps 34:19; Ps 145:18 abs Jer 12:2; Isa 50:8; Isa 55:6; Ps 119:151 אֱלֹהֵי מִקָּרֹב Jer 23:23 a God from near by (opposed to מֵרָחוֺק ׳ א ); ׳ ק of ׳ צֶרֶק י Isa 51:5, ׳ יֶשַׁע י Ps 85:10 (+ ל of person); — ׳ ק שִׁמְךָ Ps 75:2, read ׳ קֹרְאֵי בְּשׁ Dy Che Gr Dr, compare Bae 𝔊 SyeVer Du.
g. of word, + אֶל person Deut 30:14 prayer, + ׳ אֶליֿ 1Kgs 8:59 .
3. of time: יוֺם אֵידָם ׳ ק Deut 32:35 especially of ׳ יוֺם י Zeph 1:7; Zeph 1:14 (twice in verse); Ezek 30:3 (perhaps del as 𝔊 Co Krae), Ezek 30:3; Isa 13:6; Obad 1:15; Joel 1:15; Joel 2:1; Joel 4:14, compare Ezek 7:7 + infinitive אֵירמֿוֺאָב לָבוֺא ׳ ק Jer 48:16, compare Isa 13:22 ; Isa 56:1 ׳ מְחִתָּה ק Prov 10:14 imminent ruin; מִקָּרוֺכ Deut 32:17 recently, so Ezek 11:3 𝔊 ᵑ6 Co Siegf Berthol (for Mt בְּקָרוֺב) Ezek 7:8 soon; Job 20:5 of the briefest; — מִפְּנֵי חשֶׁח ׳ אוֺר ק Job 17:12 is dubious; Thes (my) light is near to (prope abest a, as Arabic , but not proven in Hebrew) darkness; Di light (they say) is near (breaking away) from darkness , will soon leave it behind; But אוֺר קְרוֺבָם פֶּןיֶֿחְשַׁךְ the light of their intimate friend shall not (?)grow dark.
