BDB2300 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2302

[זוּב] verb flow, gush (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic דּוּב, דִּיב, , all used often in sense 4 below; Arabic (medial ) melt, dissolve, flow) — only Qal Imperfect יָזוּב Lev 15:25 etc.; Participle זָב Jer 49:9f. זָבָ֫ה Lev 15:19 etc.; —

1. flow, gush (poetry), of water from the smitten rock Ps 78:20 (|| שָׁטַף), Ps 105:41 (|| הָלַךְ), Isa 48:21 (|| נָזַל).
2. Participle frequently in (אֶרֶץ) זָבַת חָלָב וּדְבַשׁ, always of promised land, Exod 3:8; Exod 3:17; Exod 13:5 (all J) Exod 33:3; Num 13:27; Num 14:8; Num 16:13; Num 16:14 (all J E) Deut 6:3; Deut 11:9; Deut 26:9; Deut 26:15; Deut 27:3; Deut 31:20; Josh 5:6 (D) Lev 20:24 (H) Jer 11:5; Jer 32:22; Ezek 20:6; Ezek 20:15 (in last two insert ארץ ᵐ5 Co); זָב עִמְקֵךְ Jer 49:4 (fertile valley of Ammon).
3. figurative flow away = pine away (from hunger) Lam 4:9.
4. flow, of issue from woman (all P), Lev 15:25 יָזוּב זוֺב דָּמָהּ (see ווֺב); especially have an issue (flux, gleet), of woman Lev 15:25 and Lev 15:19 (תִּהְיֶה זָבָה); usually of man, 2Sam 3:29 (|| מְצֹרָע); elsewhere P, Lev 15:2 זָב מִבִּשָׂרוֺ; with cognate accusative Lev 15:33 ordinarily Participle זָב one that has an issue Lev 15:4; Lev 15:6; Lev 15:7; Lev 15:8; Lev 15:9; Lev 15:11; Lev 15:12; Lev 15:13; Lev 15:32 (all P), Lev 22:4 (H; || צָרוּעַ); also Num 5:2 (P; || id.)
