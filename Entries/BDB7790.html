Biblical Hebrew

רוּד verb wander restlessly, roam (compare Arabic ( ) go to and fro [NöZMG xxxvii (1883), 539], Ethiopic run upon, invade, attack); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular עִד רָד עִם אֵל Hos 12:1, figurative, corrupt, 𝔊 apparently עַתָּ יְדָעָם, We עִדַּר דַּעַת lacks knowledge, Now יָדֻעַ, BewerJBL xxi (1902), 108 f. עֹד יְדָעָם; 1 plural רֵדְנוּ Jer 2:31 figurative of Israel; read וְרַדְתִּי perhaps Judg 11:37 literal, see ירד 1.g.
Hiph`il shew restlessness: Imperfect 2 masculine singular כַּאֲשֶׁר תָּרִיד Gen 27:40 (dubious Nöl.c. 540); 1 singular אָדִיד בְּשִׂיחִי Ps 55:3 I shew restlessness (?) in my murmuring.
