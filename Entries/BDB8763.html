Biblical Hebrew

I. [שִׁלֵּשׁ]

Pi`el denominative do a third time, devide into three parts, etc.; — Perfect 2 masculine singular וְשִׁלַּשְׁתָּ֫ Deut 19:3 divide land into three parts (so Imperfect וַיְשַׁלֵּשׁ אֶתהָֿעָם [for ᵑ0 וַיְשַׁלַּח] 2Sam 18:2 𝔊 L Bu); 1Sam 20:19 stay three days (compare Dr); 1Sam 20:20 We Dr and others (compare 𝔊 ) read ׳ אֲשַׁלֵּשׁ בַּ (for ׳ שְׁלשֶׁת הַ) = I will shoot on the third day with the arrows (HPS question); Imperfect 3 masculine plural וַיְשַׁלֵּ֑שׁוּ 1Kgs 18:34, and Imperative masculine plural שַׁלֵּ֑שׁוּ 1Kgs 18:34 do a third time.
Pu`al Participle מְשֻׁלָּשׁ three years old Gen 15:9 + 1Sam 1:24 (read with 𝔊 Th We etc. בְּפַר מְשֻׁלָּשׁ for בְּפָרִם שְׁלשָׁה ), feminine מְשֻׁלֶּשֶׁת Gen 15:9 (twice in verse); הַחוּט הַמְשֻׁלָּשׁ Eccl 4:12 the threefold cord; female plural מְשֻׁלָּשׁוֺת Ezek 42:6 three-storied chambers.
