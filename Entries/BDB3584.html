[יָנָה] verb suppress (?), oppress, maltreat (Late Hebrew Hiph`il vex with words; so Aramaic Aph`el אוֺנִי, but also ᵑ7 for Hebrew הוֺנָה maltreat; Arabic is laxus, debilis fuit, IV. debilitavit, defatigavit , but connection dubious) —

Qal Imperfect 1 plural suffix נִינָם Ps 74:8 Participle יוֺנָה Jer 25:38 + 3 t. + Ps 123:4 (see below); — suppress (?) followed by accusative Ps 74:8 (text dubious); elsewhere oppress; Participle absolute as adjective הָעִיר הַיּוֺנָה Zeph 3:1 substantive f. collective הַיּוֺנָה oppressors Jer 25:38 (but read חֶרֶב for חֲרוֺן, with ᵐ5 Ew Hi Gf Ke etc., as Jer 46:16; Jer 50:16); Ps 123:4 Qr לִגְאֵי יוֺנִים proudest oppressors (stative construct in superlative, compare Ges«GKC:133»§ 133, 3, R. 1; yet most follow Kt לּגּאייונים, and derived from גַּאֲיוֺן q. v. above)
