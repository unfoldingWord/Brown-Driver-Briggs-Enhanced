BDB1883 [H1747]

Biblical Hebrew

דּוּמִיָּה, דֻּמִיָּה (Ps 65:2) noun feminine silence, still waiting, repose — Ps 39:3 silence; Ps 62:2 still waiting, resignation (Dr§ 189. 2; but read probably, as Ps 62:6, דֹּמִּי, Bi Gr Che); Ps 65:2 id. (but Ew Che Bä and others דֹּמִיָּה is meet ? from I. דָּמָה, 𝔊 πρέπει, see Che); Ps 22:3 repose (from grief and tears: Jer 14:17).
