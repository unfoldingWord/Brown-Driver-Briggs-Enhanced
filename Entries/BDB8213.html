שֵׂיבָה noun feminine hoary head, old age; — absolute ׳שׂ Hos 7:9 +; construct שֵׂיבַת Gen 44:31 suffix שֵׂיבָתִי Gen 42:38; Gen 44:29, etc.; —

1. grey hair, hoary Hos 7:9(figurative), Gen 42:38; Gen 44:29; Gen 44:31(all J), 1Kgs 2:6; 1Kgs 2:9; Prov 16:31; Prov 20:29 ׳אִישׁ שׂ Deut 32:25 (opposed to יוֺנֵק); || זָקִן Lev 19:32 (H), || זִקְנָה Isa 46:4, + id. Ps 71:18 (in these transition to following); figurative of sea Job 41:24.
2. old age, טוֺבָה ׳שׂ Gen 15:15 (JE), Gen 25:8 (P), Judg 8:32; 1Chr 29:28 ׳שׂ also Ps 92:15 (trees, in figure of righteous), Ruth 4:15, + 2Sam 19:34 (ᵐ5 Ew Th Bu Kit, compare We Dr HPS, אֶתשֵֿׂיבָֽתְךָ, for אֹתְךָ).
