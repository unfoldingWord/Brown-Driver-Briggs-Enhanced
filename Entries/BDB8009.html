Biblical Hebrew

רְעוּאֵל proper name, masculine Ραγουηλ:

1. Moses' father-in-law Exod 2:18 ( J ; 𝔊 L Ιοθορ), Num 10:29 ( J E ); elsewhere יִתְרוֺ q. v.
2. Edomite Gen 36:4; Gen 36:10; Gen 36:13; Gen 36:17 (twice in verse) ( P ), || 1Chr 1:35; 1Chr 1:37.
3. Gadite Num 2:14, see דְּעוּאל p. 396.
4. Benjamite 1Chr 9:8.
