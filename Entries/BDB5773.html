עֲבָרִים proper name [of a location] 'Abarîm (properly regions beyond river or sea); — usually ׳ הַר הָע Num 27:12; Deut 32:49 ( = הַלנְֿבוֺ; both P ), and ׳ הָרֵי הָע Deut 33:47 ( לִפְנֵי נְבוֺ), v Deut 33:48 ( P ); alone only צַעֲקִי מֵעֲבָרִים Jer 22:20 the mountainous district in northwest Moab, just northeast of Dead Sea, GASmGeogr. 548 and EBi BuhlGeogr. 122; ᵐ5 Αβαρειμ(‐ειν), but τὸ ἐν τῷ πέραν Num 33:47, εἰς τὸ πέραν τῆς θαλάσσης Jer 22:20. — See also below עִיִּים.
