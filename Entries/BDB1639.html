BDB1639 [H1549]

Biblical Hebrew

גִּלָּיוֺן noun masculine table, tablet (Talmud גִּלְיוֺן, the empty margin of page or roll, see LagGN 1881, 403, compare BN 199) — קַחלְֿךָ גִּלָּיוֺן גָּדוֺל וּכְתֹב עָלָיו take thee a great tablet and write upon it Isa 8:1 plural הַגִּלְֹינִים tablets of polished metal, mirrors Isa 3:23 ᵑ7 ᵑ9 Ges Che Di De; but transparent garments, gauzes, 𝔊 Ew (compare Arabic fine garment).
