Biblical Hebrew

עַדְרִיאֵל proper name, masculine son-in-law of Saul (apparently Aramaic name, my help is God = Hebrew עַזְרִיאֵל q. v.; so NeAmos. Jour. Semitic Lang, xiii (1897), 173 HPS compare GrayProp. N. 309 ; compare Old Aramaic יעדראל ClGann:J As,1883,Fev.-Mar.,139,No.13); — 1Sam 18:19 ( 𝔊 A Ιηλ, 𝔊 L Εδριηλ), 2Sam 21:8 ( 𝔊 Σερει, A Εσδρι, 𝔊 L Εζρι).
