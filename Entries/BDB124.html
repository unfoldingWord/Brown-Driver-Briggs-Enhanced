[אֶ֫דֶן]57 noun masculine Exod 26:19 base, pedestal — אָ֑דֶן Exod 38:7 plural אֲדָנִים Exod 26:19 +; construct אַדְנֵי Song 5:15 +, etc.

1. pedestals of fine gold, on which pillars of marble were set Song 5:15.
2. pedestals of the earth on which its pillars were placed Job 38:6 (|| corner-stone).
3. (metal) pedestals, bases, or sockets in which tenons of planks & pillars of tabernacle were set up; two for each plank & one for each pillar Exod 26:19 (3 t. in verse) + 52 t. in Ex 26, 27, 35-40 Num 3, 4 (all P ); compare ᵐ5 Sm Co for אָרְכּוֺ Ezek 41:22, of altar.
