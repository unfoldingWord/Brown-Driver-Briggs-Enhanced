Biblical Hebrew

רֶ֫שַׁע noun masculine 1Sam 24:14 wickedness; — absolute ׳ ר Mic 6:10 +, רָ֑שַׁע Eccl 3:16 construct (perhaps) רֶ֫שַׁע Eccl 7:25 רִשְׁעוֺ Deut 9:27 +, etc.; —

1. wickedness, as violence and crime against civil law Eccl 3:16 (twice in verse); ׳ אֶגְרֹף ר Isa 58:4 fist of wickedness; ׳ חַרְצֻבּוֺת ר Isa 58:6 ׳ לֶחֶם ר Prov 4:17 ׳ מַטֵּה ר Ezek 7:11 ׳ מאֹזְנֵי ר Mic 6:11 ׳ אֹצְרוֺת ר Mic 6:10, compare Prov 10:2 בְּרֶשַׁע Ps 141:4; Prov 12:3 ׳ עשׂות ר Prov 16:12 ׳ מֵרְשָׁעִים יֵצֵא ר 1Sam 24:14, (proverb).
2. wickedness of enemies: of Egypt Ezek 31:11 (dubious Co; gloss Toy); ׳ שֵׁבֶט הר Ps 125:3 ( 𝔊 SS רָשָׁע).
3. wickedness, in ethical relations: || חטאת Deut 9:27 ( D2 ); || עון Jer 14:20 || עולה Hos 10:13 opposed to צֶדֶק Ps 45:8, אֱמֶת Prov 8:7 see also Ps 5:5; Ps 10:15; Job 34:10; Job 35:8; Eccl 7:25; Eccl 8:8 ׳ אַנְשֵׁי ר Job 34:8 ׳ אָהֳלֵי ר Ps 84:11 שׁוּב מֵרִשְׁעוֺ Ezek 3:19; Ezek 33:12 (see רִשְׁעָה 3.).
