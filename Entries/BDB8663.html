Biblical Hebrew

[שָׁכֹל, שָׁכַל LagBN 26] verb be bereaved (= Arabic ᵑ7 תְּכוֺל, תְּכֵל; compare Syriac loss of children); —

Qal Perfect 1 singular כַּאֲשֶׁר שָׁכֹלְתִּי שָׁכָ֑לְתִּי Gen 43:14 ( E ) if I am bereaved, I am bereaved (of father, expressing resignation; on כָ֑לְ see Ges«GKC:29u»§ 29u); Imperfect 1 singular אֶשְׁכַּל Gen 27:45 ( E ), with accusative of person be bereaved of you both (of mother); 3 feminine singular תִּשְׁכַּל מִנָּשִׁים אִמֶּ֑ךָ 1Sam 15:33 thy mother shall be bereaved above (more than) women.
Pi`el Perfect 3 feminine singular שִׁכְּלָה 1Sam 15:33 +, suffix וְשִׁכְּלָ֑תָּה Ezek 14:15, etc.; Imperfect 3 feminine singular תְּשַׁכֵּל Deut 32:25 +, 2 feminine singular תְּשַׁכְּלִי Ezek 36:14 Qr (see כָּשַׁל Pi`el); Infinitive construct suffix לְשַׁכְּלָם Ezek 36:12 Participle מְשַׁכֵּלָה Exod 23:26, כֶּ֫לֶת- Ezek 36:13, כָּ֫לֶת- — 2Kgs 2:19; 2Kgs 2:21 —
1. make childless, accusative of father Gen 42:36 ( E ), of mothers 1Sam 15:33 (subject חֶרֶב), compare Ezek 5:17 (subject famine and beasts), and Ezek 14:15 (id.; object land); accusative of people Hos 9:12 (מֵאָדָם), Jer 15:7 (+ אִבַּתְִּי), Ezek 36:12, compare Ezek 36:13 Ezek 36:14 (see above); object omitted (subject חֶרֶב) Deut 22:25; Lam 1:20; Ezek 21:19 (Co, for תִּכָּפֵל, see כָּפַל ; otherwise Toy Krae).
2.
a. cause barrenness, or abortion, ׳ הָאָרֶץ מְשַׁכְּ 2Kgs 2:19 , compare 2Kgs 2:21 (Thes otherwise, see ׳ מְשׁ infra).
b. shew barrenness, or abortion, (1) of any female Exod 23:26 ( E ; + עֲקָרָה), animals Gen 31:38 ( E ) Job 21:10 (2) of vine Mal 3:11.
