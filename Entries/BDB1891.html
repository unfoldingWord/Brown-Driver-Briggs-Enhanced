Biblical Hebrew

דּוּר noun [masculine] circle, ball —

1. circle: Du Isa 29:3 thinks noun here also is כַּדּוּר (or כִּדּוֺר) = כִּידוֺר onset of combatants; Marti כְּדָוִד as ᵐ5 Lo Brd and others; וְחָנִיתִי כַּדּוּר עליך Isa 29:3 ( > ᵐ5 Brd כְּדָוִד).
2. ball צָנוֺף יִצְנָפְךָ צְנֵפָה כַּדּוּר Isa 22:18 (so most; but BöNÄ ii. 134 De Or JastrPAOS 1888, xciv ff. regard noun as כַּדּוּר, ball, as Talmud, compare also MV SS under the word; see כדר ). [
3. heap, pile Ezek 24:5 Thes Ke MV compare מְדוּרָה Ezek 24:9 but see above below דּוּר verb]
