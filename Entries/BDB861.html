BDB860 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB862

אֵתָם proper name, of a location (perhaps = Egyptian Chetem, compare EbGS 521 f. but ᵐ5 Ὀθομ, Ὀθωμ, compare LagBN 54) Exod 13:20 in Egypt, place on edge of desert, so Num 33:6; Num 33:7 אִדְבַּר אֵתָם Num 33:8.

אַתֶּם see below אנת.

אֶתְמוֺל see below תמל.
