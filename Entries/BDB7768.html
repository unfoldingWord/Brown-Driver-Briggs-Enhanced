Biblical Hebrew

I. [רָדָה] verb have dominion, rule, dominate (Late Hebrew רָדָה, ᵑ7 Jon רָדָא, chastise; Arabic tread, trample; Syriac chastise, also (and so Assyrian radû ) go, flow); —
