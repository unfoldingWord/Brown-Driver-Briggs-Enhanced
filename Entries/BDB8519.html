I. שָׁוָה verb (be even, smooth, see Pi`el, hence) agree with, be like, resemble (Arabic II. make even, flat, uniform (with something else); Aramaic שְׁוָא (derived species) be like, be equal, fit, like, Pa`el lay out smoothly, Aph`el make plain, fit, worthy); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular לִי ׳ לֹא שׁ Job 33:27 (si vera lectio) it was not equalled to me, i.e. (see infra; according to most) not requited (so De Di Da, and Isa 16:6 ᵑ7 ); Bu אֵל לֹא שִׁוָּה God did not requite (or שִׁלֵּם for שׁוה); Du לֹא שִׁוָּה כַעֲוֺנִי (compare ᵐ5 ); Imperfect 1 singular of ׳ י, אֶלמִֿי ֗֗֗ אֶשְׁוֶה Isa 40:25 to whom shall I be like ? (|| תְּדַמְּיוּנִי), implying that he is incompare; 3 masculine plural לֹא יִשְׁווּבָֿהּ Prov 3:15 are not comparable with her (wisdom), so Prov 8:11 2 masculine singular פֶּןתִּֿשְׁוֶהלֿוֺ Prov 26:4 lest thou be like him (a fool); Participle שֹׁוֶה בְּ Esth 7:4 is not an equivalent for; with לִ, suitable for Esth 3:8, so Esth 1:22 Hi Or RyKau (reading כֹּל שֹׁוֶה עִמּוֺ for כִּלְשׁוֺן עַמּוֺ, but see Siegf.); adequate for Esth 5:13.
Pi`el Perfect 3 masculine singular שִׁוָּה Isa 28:25 he hath levelled its (the ground's) surface; 1 singular שִׁוִּיתִי וְדוֺמַמְתִּי נַפְשִׁי Ps 131:2 I have smoothed (composed) and stilled my soul; so, that is נַפְשִׁי Isa 38:13 ᵑ9 Ges De and others; Houb Lo Che Du and others read שִׁוַּעְתִּי I cried; accusative מִשְׁפָּטִים Ps 119:30 accounted suitable, meet (|| דֶּרֶךְ אֱמוּנָח בָּחָ֑רְתִּי ), Hi, compare Gr Ps 119:128 (see Qal Esth 3:8); We inserts לְנֶגְדִּי (II. ׳ שׁ, compare Ps 16:8); Zenner Du אִזִּיתִי.
Hiph`il make like: Imperfect 1 singular מָח אַשְׁוֶחלָֿךְ Lam 2:13 (|| אֲדַמֶּה); 2 masculine plural לְמִי תְדַמְּיוּנִי וְתַשְׁווּ Isa 46:5 (compare Qal Isa 40:25).
