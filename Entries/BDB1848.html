[דַּד] noun masculine Prov 5:19 breast, better teat, nipple (τιτθοί Aq Prov 5:19 Symm Ezek 23:3; Ezek 23:21 Late Hebrew & Aramaic דַּד; primitive caressing word; according to Fl in NHWBi. 489) — Du. construct דַּדֵּי Ezek 23:3; Ezek 23:8) both strike out ᵐ5 ᵑ6 Co); suffix דַּדַּיִךְ Ezek 23:21 (read בתוליך ᵑ6 Co); דַּדֶּיהָ Prov 5:19 — of breasts of woman Prov 5:19 (Hi Bi read דֹּדֶיהָ her love compare Prov 7:18); of Samaria & Jerusalem under figure of young women Ezek 23:3 ( דַּדֵּי בְּתוּלֵיהֶן) compare Ezek 23:8 דַּדַּיִךְ Ezek 23:21 but probably strike out ׳ ד in Ezekiel compare above & GeiUrachrift 397 . — See also שַׁד below שׁדה.
