BDB5236 [H4541]

Biblical Hebrew

II. מַסֵּכָה noun feminine woven stuff, web, especially as covering; — absolute הַנְּסוּכָה עַלֿ ׳ הַמּ Isa 25:7 the web that is woven over all the nations (i.e. mourning-veil, symbol of distress; || הַלּוֺט הַלּוֺט); = bed- covering Isa 28:20 (in figurative). Perhaps also ׳ לִנְסֹךְ מ Isa 30:1 weave a web (|| לַעֲשׂוֺת עֵצָה), reference to negotiations with Egypt (so ᵑ9 Aq Ew De Che Di; > 𝔊 Ges Hi Kn Brd Du pour a libation; ׳ מ not elsewhere = נֶסֶךְ).
