חרסות Kt , חַרְסִית Qr noun feminine collective potsherd — פֶּתַח שַׁעַר הַחַרְסִ֯ות Jer 19:2, designation of a gate in Jerusalem; opening of the gate of potsherds, i.e. where they were thrown (see Jer 19:10 Jer 19:11 and Gf); it led into the valley of Hinnom; 𝔊 Ξαρς(ε)ιθ favours Qr

see BaldenspergerPEF 1904, 136 on the crushing of potsherds in the Valley of Hinnom.
