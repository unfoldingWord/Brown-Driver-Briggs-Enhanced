מָקוֺר noun masculine Zech 13:1 spring, fountain (apparently originally well); — absolute ׳ מ Zech 13:1; Prov 25:26 construct מְקוֺר Jer 2:13 +, מְקֹר Lev 12:7; Lev 20:18 suffix מְקוֺרוֺ Hos 13:15, etc.; —

1. spring of water :
a; figurative, of ׳ י, מַיִם ׳ מ חַיִּים Jer 2:13; Jer 17:13, compare חַיִּים ׳ מ Ps 36:10 חַיִּים ׳ מ (more generally), Prov 10:11; Prov 13:14; Prov 14:27; Prov 16:22 + Prov 18:4 (so read for חָכְמָה ׳ מ ᵐ5 Hebrew Manuscripts Toy).
b. figurative of purification Ezek 13:1.
c. מָקוֺר מָשְׁחָת Prov 25:26 (figurative; + מַעְיָן).
d. figurative of source of life and vigour Hos 13:15; Jer 51:36 of a nation's original source, stock Ps 68:27 (Kay Che, of temple); source of joy Prov 5:18 (figurative of wife; || אֵשֶׁת ).
2. figurative of eye, מְקוֺר דַּמְעָה, Jer 8:23.
3. source of menstruous blood, דָּמֶיהָ ׳ מְ Lev 20:18, so מְקֹרָהּ vLev 20:18 ( H ).
4. = flow of blood after child-birth דָּמֶיהָ ׳ מְ Lev 12:7 ( P ).
