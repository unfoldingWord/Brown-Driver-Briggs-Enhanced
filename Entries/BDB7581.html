קִרְיַת יְעָרִים proper name [of a location] πόλ(ε)ις Ιαρειμ[ν], Καριαθιαρειμ, etc. (city of forests ); — city of the Gibeonites Josh 9:17, assigned to Judah Josh 15:60 compare Judg 18:12 (twice in verse), on border of Benjamin Josh 15:9 ( יְעָרִים הַר Josh 15:10, 𝔊 πόλιν Ιαρειν, see יְעָרִים ), Josh 18:14 assigned to Benjamin Josh 18:28 (יְעָרִים omitted by textual error before עָרִים, see 𝔊 ); near Beth Shemesh 1Sam 6:21, long the abode of ark 1Sam 7:1; 1Sam 7:2; 1Chr 13:5; 1Chr 13:6; 2Chr 1:4 named in genealogical list 1Chr 2:50; 1Chr 2:52; 1Chr 2:53 named also Neh 7:29 = עָרִים ׳ ק Ezra 2:25 (read יְעָרִים); with article הַיְּעָרִים ׳ ק Jer 26:20 abbreviated (in poetry) (שְׂדֵיֿ) יָ֑עַר Ps 132:6 called also קִרְיַתבַּֿעַל (Καριαθβααλ) Josh 15:60; Josh 18:14 + Josh 18:15 (where read for יְעָרִים ׳ ק, so 𝔊 Di Benn Steuern); this abbreviated into בַּעֲלָה, Josh 15:9; Josh 15:10 [( Ιε)βααλ], also Josh 15:11 Josh 15:29, and מִבַּעֲלֵי יְהוּדָה 2Sam 6:2 (read ׳ בַּעַל יְה, or ׳ בַּעֲלַת יְה), בַּעֲלָ֑תָה || 1Chr 13:6 (see on these II. בַּעֲלָה). Identification uncertain; RobBR ii. 11 conjectures Kiryat el-`Enab, 8 miles + west of Jerusalem (compare Καριαθιαρειμ LagOnom. 271); `Erma (HendersonPal. 85, 112, 210 ConderSurvey Mem. iii. 43 ff.), compare GASmGeogr. 225 f.
