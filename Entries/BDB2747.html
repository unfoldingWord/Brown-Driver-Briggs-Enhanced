Biblical Hebrew

I. חֵ֫לֶב noun masculine Exod 29:13 fat (Late Hebrew id., fat, so Phoenician חלב (probably); Aramaic (also diaphragm in Lexicons, see PS1274) originally diaphragm, midriff; Arabic , including midriff-fat, RSSemitic i. 360); — ׳ ח absolute Lev 3:16 + 40 t.; construct Gen 45:18 + 21 t.; suffix חֶלְבּוֺ Lev 3:9 + 6 t.; חֶלְבָּהּ Lev 4:31; Lev 4:35, חֶלְבָּם Num 18:17, חֶלְבָּ֫מוֺ Ps 17:10, חֶלְבְּהֶ֫ן Lev 8:16; Lev 8:25 (read plural noun חלביהן Samaritan Di); plural הַחֲלָבִים Lev 8:26 + 7 t.; construct חֶלְבֵי Lev 6:5 + 4 t.; suffix חֶלְבֵהֶן Gen 4:4 (חלביהן Samaritan Di, compare Lev 8:16; Lev 8:25 above); —

1. fat of human body Judg 3:22 (covering intestines); גִּבּוֺרִים ׳ ח 2Sam 1:22 as smearing warrior's shield; of grossness of wicked כִּסָּה פָּנָיו בְּחֶלְבּוֺ Job 15:27 fat (of midriff) figurative of unreceptive heart סָֽגְרוּ ׳ ח Ps 17:10, see also Ps 73:7 (read מֵחֵלֶב עֲוֺנָמוֺ for מחלב עֵינֵמוֺ 𝔊 ᵑ6 Hi Ew Ol De Che Bae and others); ׳ טָפַשׁ כַּח לִבָּם Ps 119:70 i.e. their heart is as unresponsive as the midriff-fat near it.
2. fat of beasts:
a. as rich food (poetry) Deut 32:14 and in simile Ps 63:6 (|| דֶּשֶׁן); so ᵑ0 Ezek 34:3 (but read הֶחָלָב, see חָלָב ), Ezek 39:19 ( ᵑ6 Co בשׂר).
b. especially as offered in sacrifice, singular, to ׳ י Exod 23:18 ( J E ), 1Sam 2:15 ; 1Sam 2:16 ; Exod 29:13 (twice in verse); Exod 29:22 (3 t. in verse); Lev 3:3 (twice in verse); Lev 3:4; Lev 3:9 (3 t. in verse); Lev 3:10; Lev 3:14 (twice in verse); Lev 3:15 + 23 t. Leviticus; Num 18:17 (all P ), Lev 17:6 ( H ), 1Sam 15:22; Isa 1:11; Isa 43:24; Ezek 44:7; Ezek 44:15 see Isa 34:6 (twice in verse); Isa 34:7 of fat of Edomites slaughtered by ׳ י's sword (under figure of lambs, rams, and bulls); as eaten by (strange) gods Deut 32:38 (poetry), not to be eaten by men Lev 3:17; Lev 7:23; Lev 7:24 (twice in verse); Lev 7:25 (all P ); also plural, fat parts or pieces Gen 4:4 ( J ) Lev 6:5 + 8 t. Leviticus; 1Kgs 8:64 (twice in verse); 2Chr 7:7 (twice in verse); 2Chr 29:35; 2Chr 35:14.
3. choicest, best part of products of land Gen 45:18 ( E ), specifically of oil Num 18:12 and wine Num 18:12 of corn and oil Num 18:29 Num 18:30 Num 18:32 also (poetry) כִּלְיוֺת חִטָּה ׳ ח Deut 32:14 kidney-fat of wheat (i.e. the very choicest, compare Isa 34:6), חִטָּה ׳ ח Ps 81:17 , חִטִּים ׳ ח Ps 147:14 .
