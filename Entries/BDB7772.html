Biblical Hebrew

תַּרְדֵּמָה noun feminine deep sleep; — absolute ׳ ת Gen 2:21 +; construct תַּרְדֵּמַת 1Sam 26:12 — deep sleep, usually with נָפַל + עַל person, and usually by supernatural agency: עַל ׳ ת ׳ א ׳ וַיַפֵּל י Gen 2:21 ( J ; 𝔊 ἔκστασις), נָָֽפְלָח עַל ׳ ת Gen 15:12 ( J ; 𝔊 id.), Job 4:13 = Job 33:15 ( 𝔊 [δεινὸς], φόβος), so ׳ תַּרְדֵּמַת י 1Sam 26:12 ( 𝔊 θάμβος, result of slothfulness (על and person omitted) Prov 19:15 figurative for insensibility of spirit, ׳ רוּחַ ת ׳ נָסַךְ עֲלֵיכֶם י Isa 29:10.
