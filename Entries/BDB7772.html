BDB7772 [H8639]

BDB7771 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7773

תַּרְדֵּמָה noun feminine deep sleep; — absolute ׳ת Gen 2:21 +; construct תַּרְדֵּמַת 1Sam 26:12 — deep sleep, usually with נָפַל + עַל person, and usually by supernatural agency: עַל ׳ת ׳א ׳וַיַפֵּל י Gen 2:21 (J; ᵐ5 ἔκστασις), נָָֽפְלָח עַל ׳ת Gen 15:12 (J; ᵐ5 id.), Job 4:13 = Job 33:15 (ᵐ5 [δεινὸς], φόβος), so ׳תַּרְדֵּמַת י 1Sam 26:12 (ᵐ5 θάμβος, result of slothfulness (על and person omitted) Prov 19:15 figurative for insensibility of spirit, ׳רוּחַ ת ׳נָסַךְ עֲלֵיכֶם י Isa 29:10.
