1. hard, difficult, of a legal question (דָּבָר) Exod 18:26 (E).
2. a. severe, of battle 2Sam 2:17, wind Isa 27:8, servitude (עֲבֹדָה) 1Kgs 12:4 = 2Chr 10:4; Deut 26:6; Isa 14:3; Exod 1:14; Exod 6:9 (both P); as n.collective הִרְאִיתָ עַמְּךָ קָשָׁה Ps 60:5 thou hast made thy people see severe things; קְשֵׁה יוֺם Job 30:25 i.e. one whose time (life) is hard, so 1Sam 1:15 (reading קְשַׁת יוֺם for רוּחַ ׳ק, ᵐ5 Th We Dr and others); severe, rough, of lord (אֲדֹנִים) Isa 19:4, compare 2Sam 3:39 (with מִן compare [מִמֶּנּוּ? see HPS]); הָלוֺח וְקָשָׁה עַלֿ Judg 4:24 the hand of the sons of Israel went on being more and more severe upon Jabin; fierce, relentless, of ׳י's sword Isa 27:1 (in figurative); rough, rude 1Sam 25:3 severe things, of language Gen 42:7; Gen 42:30 (E; with דִּדֶּר), 1Sam 20:10; 1Kgs 12:13 = 2Chr 10:13 (all with עָנָה); וְאָנֹכִי שָׁלוּחַ אֵלַיִח קָשָׁה 1Kgs 14:6 I am commissioned with a severe (message) unto thee (Ges«GKC:121d»§ 121d.
