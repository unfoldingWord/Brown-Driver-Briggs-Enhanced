BDB1428 [H1363]

Biblical Hebrew

גֹּ֫בַהּ noun masculine height — Job 22:12 + 9 t.; suffix גָּבְהוֺ 1Sam 17:4 + 5 t.; plural construct גָּבְהֵי Job 11:8 —

1. height, of buildings and trees Ezek 1:18; Ezek 19:11; Ezek 31:10; Ezek 31:14; Ezek 40:42; Ezek 41:8; 2Chr 3:4; Amos 2:9 probably also Ezek 43:13 (of altar), so 𝔊 Ew Co for ᵑ0 גַּב (q. v.); of man 1Sam 17:4 heaven Job 11:8; Job 22:12 (גבה construct ST:6 of rock).
2. exaltation, grandeur Job 40:10.
3. haughtiness, Jer 48:29 אַף ׳ גּ Ps 10:4 לִֿבּוֺ ׳ גּ 2Chr 32:26 רוּחַ ׳ גּ Prov 16:18 .
