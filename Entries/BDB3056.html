BDB3056 [H2674]

Biblical Hebrew

חָצוֺר, חָצֹר proper name [of a location] (LagBN 47 f.) — 𝔊 Ασωρ, etc.;

1. a royal city of northern Canaan Josh 11:1 ׳ יָבִין מֶלֶךְחֿ, compare Josh 12:19, and Judg 4:2; Judg 4:17; 1Sam 12:9; Josh 11:10 (twice in verse); Josh 11:11; Josh 11:13; Josh 19:36; 2Kgs 15:29 חָצֹר 1Kgs 9:15.
2. places in the Negeb:
a. Josh 15:23. b. c. Josh 15:25 וחָצוֺר חֲדַתָּה וּקְרִיּוֺת חֶצְרוֺן הִיא חָצוֺר.
3. חָצוֺר Neh 11:33 a Benjamite town, probably = בַּעַל חָצוֺר q. v.
4. מַמְלְכוֺת חָצוֺר אֲשֶׂר הִכָּה נְבוּכַדְרֶאצַּו֯ר Jer 49:28, compare Jer 49:30 Jer 49:33 , an Arabian locality, see WinerBW Hazor Gf Gie. — ׳ עֵין ח Josh 19:37, see below עַיִן.
