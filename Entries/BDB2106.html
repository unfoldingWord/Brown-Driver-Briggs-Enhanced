BDB2106 [H3076 H3110]

Biblical Hebrew

יְהוֺחָנָן, יוֺחָנָן proper name, masculine ᵐ5 mostly Ἰωαναν, Ἰωναν (׳ י hath been gracious, compare אֶלְחָנָן חֲנַנְאֵל & references; also Phoenician יחנבעל בעליחן) —

1.
a. ׳ יְהוֺ a priest Neh 12:13
b. high priest Ezra 10:6 = יוֺחָנָן Neh 12:22; Neh 12:23 apparently = יוֺנָתָן Neh 12:11 (twice in verse) ( ᵐ5 Ἰωναθαν).
2. ׳ יְהוֺ Levite Neh 12:42.
3. ׳ יְהוֺ son of Tobiah Neh 6:18 ( ᵐ5 Manuscripts Ἰωναθαν).
4. ׳ יְהוֺ porter in David's time 1Chr 26:3 ( ᵐ5 Ἰωνας, ᵐ5 L Ἰωναθαν).
5. ׳ יְהוֺ a Judaite captain 2Chr 17:15 apparently also 2Chr 23:1.
6. ׳ יְהוֺ an Ephraimite 2Chr 28:12.
7. ׳ יְהוֺ an Israelite, Ezra's time Ezra 10:28.
8. ׳ יוֺ (& so all following) a Jewish captain, after fall of Jerusalem 2Kgs 25:23; Jer 40:8; Jer 40:13; Jer 40:15; Jer 40:16; Jer 41:11; Jer 41:13; Jer 41:14; Jer 41:15; Jer 41:16; Jer 42:1; Jer 42:8; Jer 43:2; Jer 43:4; Jer 43:5
9. eldest son of king Josiah 1Chr 3:15 ᵐ5 L Ἰωαχας (apparently = יְתוֺאָחָז 2Kgs 23:30 + ).
10. a post-exilic prince of the line of David 1Chr 3:24.
11. father of Azariah, priest in Solomon's time 1Chr 5:35; 1Chr 5:36.
12. two of David's mighty men:
a. a Benjamite 1Chr 12:5
b. a Gadite 1Chr 12:13.
13. a returning exile Ezra 8:12.
