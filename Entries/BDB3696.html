Qal Imperfect 3 feminine singular תֵּקַע Jer 6:8 וַתֵּ֫קַע Gen 32:26 + 2 t.; dislocated Gen 32:26 (Jacob's thigh); elsewhere figurative of נפשׁ; torn away, alienated from any one, followed by מִן Jer 6:8; Ezek 23:17 followed by מֵעַל Ezek 23:18.
