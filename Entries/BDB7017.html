Biblical Hebrew

[צוּרָה] noun feminine form, fashion; — construct צוּרַת Ezek 43:11 see foregoing; suffix צוּרַתוֺ Ezek 43:11 ( ᵐ5 מִצְוֺתָיו, so Co, or תּוֺרֹתָיו, so Berthol Krae; Toy as MT); so also Kt Ezek 43:11 Ezek 43:11 ( Qr plural suffix צוּרֹתָיו) 2nd of these strike out as dittograph all moderns; 1st read as Kt Co Berthol, as Qr Toy; singular suffix צוּרָם (Ges § 91 e Köii.1, 440) Ps 49:15 Qr ( Kt צירם) their form (of the dead).
