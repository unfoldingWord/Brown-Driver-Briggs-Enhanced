BDB5647 [H5576]

Biblical Hebrew

סַנְחֵרִיב proper name, masculine Sennacherib (= Sin-a—ê-irba , Sin multiplied brothers, SchrCOT 2Kgs 18:13 ); — son of Sargon and father of Esarhaddon, king of Assyria ( B.C. 705-681; compare TieleGeschichte. 285 ff.); 2Kgs 18:13; 2Kgs 19:16; 2Kgs 19:20; 2Kgs 19:36 = Isa 36:1; Isa 37:17; Isa 37:21; Isa 37:37; 2Chr 32:1; 2Chr 32:2; 2Chr 32:9; 2Chr 32:10; 2Chr 32:22 (all as above, except סַנְחֵרִב 2Kgs 19:20 ); ( 𝔊 Σενναχηρειμ, 𝔊 L Σενναχειρειμ; Σεναχήριβος JosAnt. «JOSEPHUS:Antiquities 10:1»x. 1 ff.; Σαναχάρβον (accusative) Herodotii. 141.
