2. ׳ ח combined with עשׂר, עשׂרה ten = fifteen:
a. חֲמֵשׁ עֶשְׂרֵה (c. noun feminine) Gen 5:10; Gen 7:20; Exod 27:14; Exod 27:15; Exod 38:14; Exod 38:15; 2Kgs 14:17 ; 2Kgs 14:23 ; 2Kgs 20:6 ; 2Chr 15:10; 2Chr 25:25; Isa 38:5.

c. חֲמֵשֶׁת עָשָׂר before noun 2Sam 19:18 +.
d. חֲמֵשֶׁת עָשָׂר, without noun = ordinal fifteenth 1Chr 24:14 + 4 t. + Ezek 45:12 (read חֲמִשִּׁים A Hi Co).
3. חֲמֵשׁ מֵאוֺת = 500 Gen 5:30; Gen 5:32 + 49 t. + Ezek 42:16 Qr .
4. חֲמֵשֶׁת אֲלָפִים = 5000 Num 31:32; Josh 8:12; Judg 20:45; 1Sam 17:5; Ezek 45:6; Ezek 48:15; 1Chr 29:7; 2Chr 35:9; Ezra 1:11; Ezra 2:69 — in 25,000, 35,000 etc. חֲמִשָּׁה (usually without ׳ א) precedes the 20,000, 30,000, etc.
5. with other numerals:
a. חָמֵשׁ before larger numeral: (1) noun repeated Gen 5:6 + 4 t.; (2) noun with 2nd number only, Gen 5:17 + 8 t. + Ezek 45:3 Kt ; (3) noun with בְּ with 2nd number Ezek 40:21 + 2 t.
b. חָמֵשׁ after larger number: (1) noun repeated Gen 25:7 (2) noun following ׳ ח (only) Judg 14:10 + (especially in expressions of age and duration); (3) noun before both numbers, stative absolute, 2Chr 3:15 stative construct 2Chr 15:19.
c. no noun expressed, ׳ ח between other number, e.g. ושׁבעים ׳ שֵׁשׁ מאות ח Num 31:37.
d. חֲמִשָּׁה before larger number, noun following larger number Num 1:25 + 22 t.
e. ׳ ח follows, and (1) is followed by noun Judg 20:35 + 7 t.; (2) noun precedes both 1Kgs 7:3; Jer 52:30.
f. ׳ ח precedes, no noun expressed 1Kgs 5:12.
g. ׳ ח follows, no noun expr Gen 18:28 +.
h. no noun expressed ׳ ח between other number Exod 38:28; Ezra 2:5.
