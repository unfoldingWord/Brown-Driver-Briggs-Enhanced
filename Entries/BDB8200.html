BDB8200 [H7814]

Biblical Hebrew

שְׂחֹק, שְׂתוֺק noun [masculine] laughter, derision, sport (properly infinitive construct); — absolute שְׂחֹק Jer 48:26 + 5 t.; construct Eccl 7:6 absolute שְׂחוֺק Jer 20:7 + 7 t.; —

1. laughter (joyous) Job 8:21 (|| תְּרוּעָה), Ps 126:2 (|| דִנָּה ), Eccl 2:2 (|| שִׂמְחָה), Eccl 10:19 (|| שִׂמֵּחַ); as hollow Prov 14:13 (opposed to כָּאַב), Eccl 7:3 (opposed to כַּעַס), compare הַבְּסִיל ׳ שׂ Eccl 7:6.
2. (object of) derision, ׳ הָיָה לִשׂ, Jer 20:7; Jer 48:26; Jer 48:27 (read ׳ לִשׂ for ᵑ0 ׳ הַשּׂ 𝔊 ᵑ6 ᵑ9 Gie Du; Co strike out vv), Jer 48:39; Lam 3:14 elsewhere Job 12:4 (twice in verse)
3. sport Prov 10:23 .
