אִישׁבּֿ֫שֶׁת proper name, masculine Ishbosheth (for אִישׁ בַּעַל man of Baal see בּשֶׁת , בַּעַל & Di ή βααλ MBAk, June 1881 )

1. son of Saul, & king of Israel, with David as rival 2Sam 2:8; 2Sam 2:10; 2Sam 2:12; 2Sam 2:15; 2Sam 3:8; 2Sam 3:14; 2Sam 3:15; 2Sam 4:5; 2Sam 4:8 (twice in verse); 2Sam 4:12 also 2Sam 4:1 2Sam 4:2 ᵐ5 Dr compare We; = אֶשְׁבָּ֑עַל 1Chr 8:33; 1Chr 9:39 compare also
2. 2Sam 23:8, where read אשׁבשׁת for ישׁב בשׁבת so ᵐ5 We Dr; one of David's heroes; see יָשָׁבְעָם 1Chr 11:11; 1Chr 27:2.
