BDB2343 [H2128]

Biblical Hebrew

זִיף proper name

1. proper name [of a location]
a. city south east from Hebron, Josh 15:55; 1Chr 2:42; 2Chr 11:8, modern Tell Zîf; RobBR i. 492, 498 GuérinJudée iii. 159 ff.: ׳ מִדְבַּרזֿ 1Sam 23:14; 1Sam 23:15; 1Sam 26:2 (twice in verse); with ה locative, זִ֫יפָה 1Sam 23:24.
b. city of southern Judah Josh 15:24, site unknown.
2. proper name, masculine a son of יְהַלֶּלְאֵל of Judah 1Chr 4:16, whether related to proper name [of a location] above does not appear. 𝔊 Ζαφα, Ζιφ(αι). (1Chr 2:42 see above)
