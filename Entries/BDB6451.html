BDB6451 [H6227]

Biblical Hebrew

I. עָשָׁן noun masculine Exod 19:16 smoke; — absolute ׳ ע Judg 20:38 +, construct עֲשַׁן Josh 8:20; Josh 8:21, עֶ֫שֶׁן Exod 19:18 suffix עֲשָׁנוֺ Exod 19:18, עֲשָׁנָהּ Isa 34:10 —

1.
a. of burning city Josh 8:20; Josh 8:21; Judg 20:40, compare Nah 2:14 heralding a foe Isa 14:31.
b. in figurative of destruction of Israel Isa 9:17, Edom Isa 34:10.
c. simile of dust-cloud Song 3:6 of transitoriness Hos 13:3; Isa 51:6; Ps 37:20 (reading ׳ כֶּע, as 𝔊 ᵑ6 ᵑ9 ), Ps 68:3 ; Ps 102:4 simile of the unpleasant, Prov 10:26.
d. breath of crocodile Job 41:12.
2.
a. attending theophany, ׳ תַּנּוּר ע Gen 15:17 of mountain Exod 19:18 , compare simile הַכִּבְשָׁן ׳ כְּע Exod 19:18; Isa 6:4, also Isa 4:5; Joel 3:3.
b. of God's anger, בְּאַפּוֺ ׳ ע 2Sam 22:9 = Ps 18:9 so probably also Isa 65:5.
