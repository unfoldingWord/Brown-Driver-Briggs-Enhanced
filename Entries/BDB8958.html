BDB8958 [H8202]

Biblical Hebrew

שָׁפָט proper name, masculine (he hath judged); — usually Σαφατ[θ]:

1. father of Elisha 1Kgs 19:16; 1Kgs 19:19; 2Kgs 3:11; 2Kgs 6:31.
2. prince of Simeon Num 13:5.
3. grandson of Zerubbabel 1Chr 3:22.
4. Gaddite chief 1Chr 5:12 (Σαβατ, Σαφαμ [ν]).
5. herdsman of David 1Chr 27:29 (Σωφαν, A Σωφατ, 𝔊 L Σαφατ).
