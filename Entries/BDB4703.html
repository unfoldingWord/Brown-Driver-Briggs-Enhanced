BDB4703 [H4454]

Biblical Hebrew

מָלַק verb nip, nip off (head of bird; without necessarily severing it from body, compare Lev 5:8) (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic מְלַק, ); —

Qal Perfect consecutive וּמָלַק אֶתרֿאֹשׁוֺ Lev 1:15, i.e. head of dove; compare וּמָלַק אֶתרֿאֹשׁוֺ מִמּוּל עָרְפּוֺ וְלֹא יַבְדִּיל Lev 5:8. compare Di-Ry. 𝔊 ἀποκνίζω.
