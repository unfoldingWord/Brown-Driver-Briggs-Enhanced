BDB2724 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2726

יְחִיאֵל proper name, masculine(may El live; Phoenician יחואלן, יחומלך see EutZMG 1876, 136) — ᵐ5 usually Ιειηλ, Ιαιηλ; —

1. one of David's chief musicians 1Chr 15:18 ( ᵐ5 Ιειηλ, ᵐ5 L Ιαιηλ), 1Chr 15:20 ( ᵐ5 Ειθηλ, ᵐ5 L Ιειηλ), 1Chr 16:5 ( ᵐ5 Ειειηλ, ᵐ5 L Ιαιηλ).
2. one of David's chiefs of the Levites 1Chr 23:8 ( ᵐ5 Ι(ει)ηλ), 1Chr 29:8 ( ᵐ5 Βεσιηλ, A ᵐ5 L Ιειηλ).
3. tutor of David's sons 1Chr 27:32 ( ᵐ5 Ιε(ρι)ηλ, ᵐ5 L Ιωηλ).
4. son of Jehoshaphat 2Chr 21:2.
5. יְחִו֯אֵל Hemanite of Hezekiah's reign 2Chr 29:14.
6. over-seer in Hezekiah's reign 2Chr 31:13.
7. ruler of the temple in Josiah's reign 2Chr 35:8.
8. contemporaries of Ezra, Ezra 8:9 ( ᵐ5 Ιεμα A Ιεειηλ, ᵐ5 L Ιειηλ), Ezra 10:2; Ezra 10:21; Ezra 10:6.
