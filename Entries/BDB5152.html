III. נַ֫חַת proper name, masculine

1. son of Reuel, grandson of Esau Gen 36:13; Gen 36:17 ( P ; ᵐ5 Ναχομ, Ναχοθ ᵐ5 L Ναχεθ), 1Chr 1:37 ( ᵐ5 Ναχες, A ᵐ5 L Ναχεθ).
2. grandson of Elkanah 1Chr 6:11, ᵐ5 Καιναθ, ᵐ5 L Νααθ; — probably corrupt for תּוֺחַ v.1Chr 6:19, or תֹּחוּ 1Sam 1:1 (see these words and Wede gent. 37 f. [who proposes תַּחַת for נַחַת], Dr 1Sam 1:1 ).
3. an overseer, Hezekian's time 2Chr 31:13, ᵐ5 Μαεθ, A Ναεθ, ᵐ5 L Νααθ.
