Biblical Hebrew

פְּדָיָהוּ, פְּדָיָה proper name, masculine (׳ י hath ransomed; in late Assyrian Padâma, HilprUniv. Pa, Exp. ix. 27, 68); — פְּדָיָה:

1. father-in-law of Josiah 2Kgs 23:36, but 𝔊 Εδειλ ( 𝔊 L Ιερεμιας, from 2Kgs 23:31).
2. father of Zerubbabel 1Chr 3:18; 1Chr 3:19.
3.
a. Neh 3:25.
b. Neh 8:4.
4. Benjamite Neh 11:7.
5. Levite Neh 13:13.
6. פְּדָיָהוּ, Manassite 1Chr 27:20 — 𝔊 (except 1) Φα(λ)δαιας, etc.
