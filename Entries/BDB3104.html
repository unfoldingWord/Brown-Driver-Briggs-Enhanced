BDB3104 [H2733 H2033]

BDB3103 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3105

חֲרֹדִי adjective [of location] only with article ׳ הַח, of Harod 2Sam 23:25 a (see Dr and Judg 7:1); = הַהֲרוֺרִי 1Chr 11:27 (read הַהֲרוֺדִי); also 1Sam 23:25b but probably not genuine; not in ᵐ5 or 1Chr 11:27 (see Dr and אֱלִיקָא above).
