( Qr אֵלַמָּיו). — Co all singular: — plural אֵלַמּוֺת Ezek 40:16 ( Co singular) Ezek 40:30 (strike out Co compare MSS . of HCT, B etc., also Ew Hi Sm). — porch, of Ezekiel's temple Ezek 40:16; Ezek 40:21 + 13 t.
