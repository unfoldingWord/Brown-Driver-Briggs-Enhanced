BDB4202 [H3761 H3762]

Biblical Hebrew

כַּרְמְלִי adjective [of a people], with article, the Carmelite, of Nabal ׳ הַכּ 1Sam 30:5; 2Sam 2:2; 2Sam 3:3, so also 1Sam 27:3 ( 𝔊 We Klo Dr Bu Kit; ᵑ0 הַכַּרְמְלִית, of Abi-gail); of חצרו 2Sam 23:35 = 1Chr 11:37. feminine הַכַּרְמְלִית the Carmelitess, 1Chr 3:1, of Abigail.
