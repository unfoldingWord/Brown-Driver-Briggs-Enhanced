סָדִין noun [masculine] linen wrapper (perhaps foreign word; compare Assyrian sudinnu DlHWB 490, a garment; Late Hebrew סָדִין, ᵑ7 סְדִינָא, Syriac , (rare) Mark 15:46 > compare Arabic , veil, saddle-cover (see Lane1335), or , Greek [and ᵐ5 ] σινδών, compare Frä48 LewyFremdw. 85); — absolute ׳ ס Prov 31:24 plural סְדִינִים Judg 14:12; Judg 14:13; — wrapper or rectangular piece of fine linen , worn as outer, or (at night) as sole garment (compare GFMJudg 14, 12 ), + חֲלִפֹּת בְּגָדִים Judg 14:12; Judg 14:13, in list of women's finery Isa 3:23, made and sold by the capable woman Prov 31:24.
