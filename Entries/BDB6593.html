Biblical Hebrew

פִּטְדָה noun feminine (masculine according to AlbrZAW xvi (1896), 108) topaz or chrysolite (according to 𝔊 τοπάζιον, ᵑ9 topazius, etc.; probably foreign word, compare Sanskrit pîta (properly yellow)); — named with other precious stones Ezek 28:13; Exod 28:17 = Exod 39:10 construct פִּטְדַתכּֿוּשׁ Job 28:19. — See PlinNH xxxvii. 8 (32), Houghton in SmDB, Topaz RiHWB, Edelstein.
