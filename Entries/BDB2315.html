Biblical Hebrew

זוּזִים proper name [of a people] an ancient trans-Jordanic people, dwelling in הָם Gen 14:5 otherwise unknown; abode apparently in (later) land of Ammon, between Bashan and Moab; commonly supposed = זַמְזֻמִּים q. v., in that case perhaps text error in one case or the other, compare NöUntersuchungen z.
