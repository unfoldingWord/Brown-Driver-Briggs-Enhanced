Biblical Hebrew

יְשַׁעְיָ֫הוּ proper name, masculine (salvation of Yah; compare אֱלִישָׁע above; ישעאל on scarab ClGannJAs 1883, Fev.-Mars, 135, No. 8) —

1. Isaiah, son of Amos, the prophet: Isa 1:1 + 15 t. Isaiah; 2Kgs 19:2 + 12 t. Kings; 2Chr 26:22; 2Chr 32:20; 2Chr 32:32 𝔊 Ηξαιας, ᵑ9 Isaias.
2. one of the children of Jeduthun 1Chr 25:3; 1Chr 25:15, 𝔊 Ισαια, etc.
3. a Levite ancestor of one of David's treasurers 1Chr 26:25, 𝔊 Ωσαιας, 𝔊 L Ιωσηε.
