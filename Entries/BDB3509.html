BDB3508 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3510

[יָחַד] verb be united (Arabic be alone, II. make one; Late Hebrew Pi`el יִחֵד make or declare one; Aramaic יַחֵד unite, set alone) —

Qal Imperfect 3 feminine singular Gen 49:6 בִּקְהָלָם אַלתֵּֿחַד כְּבוֺדִי in their assembly let my glory not be united (|| בְּסֹדָם אַלֿ תָּבאֹ נַפְשִׁי); 2 feminine singular Isa 14:20 לֹא תֵחַד אִתָּם בִּקְבוּרָה.
Pi`el Imperative Ps 86:11 יַחֵד לְבָבִי לְיִרְאָה שְׁמֶ֑ךָ unite my heart (i.e. concentrate its affections, compare Jer 32:39) to fear thy name (but ᵐ5 ᵑ6 ᵑ9 Gr Bi Ch יִחַדְּ (from חָדָה) let my heart rejoice , etc.)
