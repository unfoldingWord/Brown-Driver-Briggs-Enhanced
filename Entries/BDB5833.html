Biblical Hebrew

III. עֵ֫דֶן proper name [of a territory] (probably associated by Hebrew with I. עֵדֶן); — district in which lay garden of ׳ י home of Adam and Eve: וַיִּטַּע גַּןבְּֿעֵדֶן Gen 2:8, river ׳ יֹצֵא מֵע Gen 2:10, ׳ קִדְמַתעֿ Gen 4:16 ׳ גַּןעֿ Gen 2:15; Gen 3:23; Gen 3:24 (all 𝔊 Εδεμ); compare ׳ כְּגַןעֿ Ezek 36:35; Joel 2:3 and ׳ כְּע Isa 51:3 (|| ׳ גַּןיֿ), all simile of fertility; גַּןאֱֿלֹהִים ׳ ע Ezek 28:13, אֲשֶׁר בְּגַן הָאֱלֹהַים ׳ עֲצֵי ע Ezek 31:9, ׳ עֲצֵי ע Ezek 31:16 Ezek 31:18, Ezek 31:18 ( 𝔊 Ezekiel Joel ἡ τρυφή; Isaiah παράδεισος).
