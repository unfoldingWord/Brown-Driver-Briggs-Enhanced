מִיָּמִן, מִיָּמִין, מִנְיָמִן, מִנְיָמִין proper name, masculine

1. priestly name:
a. מִיָּמִן assigned to David's time, 1Chr 24:9.
b. Nehemiah's time Neh 10:8 = מִנְיָמִין Neh 12:17; Neh 12:41.
c. מִנְיָמִן assigned to Hezekiah's time 2Chr 31:15.
d. מִיָּמִין went up with Zerubbabel Neh 12:5.
2. one of those who took strange wives Ezra 10:25. — ᵐ5 Βενιαμειν and (especially A ᵐ5 L ) Μιαμειν.
