חֹזֶה noun masculine seer; — absolute ׳ח Amos 7:12 + 7 t.; construct חֹזֵה 1Chr 21:9 + 3 t.; חוֺזֵה 2Chr 35:15 plural חֹזִים Isa 29:10 + 4 t.; חוֺזִים 2Chr 33:19 (so read with ᵐ5; חוֺזָי֑ ᵑ0 scribal error) —

1. seer:
\ \ a. of unnamed persons, synonym נבא(יםׅ 2Kgs 17:13; Isa 29:10 רֹאִים Isa 30:10 קֹסְמִים Mic 3:7 historical writers דִּבְרֵי הַחֹוִים 2Chr 33:18, דברי חוזים (ᵐ5) 2Chr 33:19 (ᵑ0 apparently proper name)
\ \ b. named prophets; Gad 2Sam 24:11 = 1Chr 21:9; 1Chr 29:29; 2Chr 29:25 Amos Amos 7:12 Iddo 2Chr 9:29; 2Chr 12:15 Jehu 2Chr 19:2.
\ \ c. the singers: Heman 1Chr 25:5 Asaph 2Chr 29:30 Jeduthun 2Chr 35:15.

Note — It seems best to add
2. vision (compare רֹאֶה Isa 28:7) עִם שְׁאוֺל עָשִׂינוּ חֹזֶה Isa 28:15 with She'Ùl we made a vision (had a vision, in connection with She'Ùl — i.e. by necromancy, see Du — which makes us secure; || כרת ברית אֶתמָֿוֶת; Ew§ 156 e transitive oracle here and 2Kgs 17:13 HoffmZAW iii. 95 puts this under 1: 'we have appointed a prophet with She'ol (SS 'Ein bei der Scheol be-stellter Seher'), who is answerable to us for it;' ᵐ5 ᵑ9 ᵑ7 AV RV Che Di and others, are at (or have made an) agreement (prophetic advice of seers being taken at time of making treaties; see חָזוּת 1), — See also כָּלחֹֿזֶה proper name, masculine below כּל.
