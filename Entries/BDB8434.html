שְׁבוּעָה, שְׁבֻעָה noun feminine (Josh 2:17 read הַזּה AlbrZAW xvi(1896), 116) oath, curse; — absolute ׳ שְׁבוּ Josh 9:20 +, ׳ שְׁבֻ Gen 26:3 +; construct שְׁבֻעַת Exod 22:10 +; suffix שְׁבֻעָתִי Gen 24:8 etc.; plural שְׁבֻעוֺת absolute Ezek 21:28, construct Hab 3:9 [Jer 5:24; Ezek 45:21 see שָׁבוּעַ ]; — oath [as accusative of cognate meaning with verb with הִשָּׁבַע etc. Num 30:3 + (in rel.cl.) 5t.]:

1. of man:
a. attesting innocence Exod 22:10 ( E ), friendship 2Sam 21:7, promise 1Kgs 2:43 (after שָׁמַר keep; all ׳ שְׁבֻעַת י oath to to (׳ י); innocence also Num 5:21 a; friendship Neh 6:18 (לוֺ ׳ בַּעֲלֵישׁ), promise Lev 5:4 of vow (|| נדר) Num 30:3; Num 30:11; Num 30:14 (שְׁבֻעַת אִסָּר ), Josh 9:20 (all P ), Neh 10:30 (|| אָלָה), Eccl 9:2, of covenant with ׳ י 2Chr 15:15 , שְׁבוּעַת אֱלֹהִים Eccl 8:2 (probably of oath of allegiance to king), שְׁבֻעַת שֶׁקֶר Zech 8:17 of threat Judg 21:5 ; 1Sam 14:26 ( ᵐ5 ׳ שְׁבֻעַת י); keep, discharge the oath is ׳ הֵקִים הַשּׁ Gen 26:3 ( J ), Jer 11:5 ׳ נָקָה מִשׁ be free, exempt from an oath Gen 24:8 ( J ), compare Josh 2:17; Josh 2:20 .
b. = curse, Isa 65:15 and (|| אָלָה) Num 5:21 ( P ), Dan 9:11 .
