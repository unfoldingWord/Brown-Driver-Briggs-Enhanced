מוֺעֵד223 noun masculine Ps 102:14 appointed time, place, meeting; ׳ מ Exod 9:5 + 183 t.; מֹעֵד Deut 31:10 מוֺעֲדוֺ Num 9:2 + 2 t.; מֹעֲדוֺ Lam 2:6 + 3 t. + 5 t. suffixes; plural מוֺעֲדִים Dan 12:7 + 6 t.; מֹעֲדִים Zech 8:19; 1Chr 23:31 מוֺעֲדוֺת 2Chr 8:13 construct מֹועֲדֵי Lev 23:2 + 6 t.; מֹעֲדֵי Lev 23:44 suffixes מוֺעֲדַי Ezek 44:24 מֹעֲדֵיכֶם Num 15:3 + 6 t. suffixes; —

1. appointed time:
a. in general with prefix לְ, at an or the appointed time Gen 18:14; Exod 13:10 ( J ), Exod 23:15; Exod 34:18; Josh 8:14 (all J E ), Gen 17:21; Gen 21:2 ( P ), 1Sam 9:24 ; 1Sam 13:8 (after אשׁר insert either אָמַר ᵐ5 ᵑ7 or שָׂם Dr), v1Sam 13:11; 2Kgs 4:16; 2Kgs 4:17; Hab 2:3; Dan 8:19; Dan 11:27; Dan 11:29; Dan 11:35 דוד ׳ למ at he time appointed with David 1Sam 20:35 (Thes SS place appointed); with prefix בְּ Hos 2:11; Lev 23:4; Num 9:2; Num 9:3; Num 9:7; Num 9:13; Num 28:2 ( P ); with מִן 2Sam 20:5 מ ׳ עַד עֵת 2Sam 24:15 unto the time appointed (but dubious, see Dr); with verbs ׳ בא מ Ps 102:14 ׳ לקח מ Ps 75:3 ׳ שׂים מ Exod 9:5 ( J ); ׳ העביר המ Jer 46:17 the stork ׳ ידעה מ Jer 8:7 knows her appointed time; צאתך ׳ מ Deut 16:6 time of thy going forth (from Egypt).
b. in particular (compare Exod 13:10; Exod 23:15; Exod 34:18 ), sacred season, RV usually set feast or appointed season (wider than חַג, which was only a feast celebrated by a pilgrimage), ׳ יוֺם (ימי) מ day (s) of appointed season (i.e. festivals) Hos 9:5; Hos 12:10; Lam 2:7; Lam 2:22 singular indefinite Lam 1:4 of the feast of booths Deut 31:10 || חג חדשׁ שׁבת Hos 2:13 || שׁבת Lam 2:6 usually plural יהוה ׳ מ Lev 23:2; Lev 23:4; Lev 23:37; Lev 23:44 ( P ), 2Chr 2:3 ; Ezra 3:5 with suffixes referring to ׳ י Lev 23:2 ( P ); ׳ בּמ Num 15:3; Num 29:39 ( P ), Ezek 36:38; Ezek 46:9 קרית ׳ מ city of our solemnities (sacred seasons) Isa 33:20 טובים ׳ למ Zech 8:19 || שׁבתות Ezek 44:24 || חדשׁים Isa 1:14; 1Chr 23:31 ; 2Chr 8:13; 2Chr 31:3; Neh 10:34 || חנים Ezek 46:11 חגים חדשׁים Ezek 45:17 || ראשׁי חָדְשֵׁכֶם Num 10:10 ( P ). —
Notes. It is most probable that in Gen 1:14 ( P ), where ׳ מ || אֹתֹת, the reference is to the sacred seasons as fixed by moon's appearance; and so also ׳ עשׂה ירח למ he made the moon for sacred seasons Ps 104:19 , although many Lexicons & Commentaries refer these to the seasons of the year. — למועד מועדים וחצי Dan 12:7 for a set time, times, and a half = 3 1/2 appointed times = half the prophetic week of years, compare BrMP 453 f. — ׳ ויאכלו את המ they ate throughout the sacred season (of Maƒƒoth) 2Chr 30:22 AV RV , but Thes SS Be Ke Öttli they ate the offerings of the sacred season ; ᵐ5 וַיְכַלּוּ.
2. appointed meeting: לְכָלחַֿי ׳ בֵּית מ Job 30:23 house of meeting for every living (of She'ol); ׳ קְרִאֵי מ Num 16:2 ( P ) called to the assembly = קְרִאֵי הָעֵדָה; ׳ קרא עלי מ Lam 1:15 called a festal meeting against me; ׳ בקרב מ Ps 74:4 in the midst of thine assembly; הַר מוֺעֵד Isa 14:13 mount of meeting or assembly (of the gods; the mountain of the gods in the extreme north, the oriental Olympus, Persian Alborg , Hindu Meru, Babylon. Aralli, compare LenOrigines ii, ch. ix).
3. appointed place:
a. the temple, || שׂכּוֺ Lam 2:6, ׳ נוּגֵי ממ afflicted (and driven) from the place of assembly Zeph 3:18 (compare BrMP 225).
b. synagogues, כָּלמֿוֺעֲדֵיאֵֿל בָּאָרֶץ all the appointed places of El in the land Ps 74:8 = בֵּית וַעַד Sota Ps 9:15.
4. appointed sign, signal, Judg 20:38.
5. אֹהֶל מוֺעֵד tent of meeting (of God with his people: see יָעַד Niph`al 1); the sacred tent of the Exodus (see אֹהֶל 3) Exod 33:7 (twice in verse); Num 12:4; Deut 31:14 (twice in verse) ( E ), Num 11:16 ( J ); often in P , as Exod 27:21; Exod 29:4; Lev 1:1; Lev 1:3; Josh 18:1; Josh 19:51, etc. (131 t.)>; elsewhere only 1Sam 2:22b (omitted by ᵐ5 ), 1Kgs 8:4 = 2Chr 5:5; 1Chr 6:17; 1Chr 9:21; 1Chr 23:32; 1Chr 2:3; 1Chr 2:6; 1Chr 2:13.
