BDB7434 [H7024 H7025]

Biblical Hebrew

II. קִיר in proper name [of a location] of Moabite cities (compare קר = city MI11, 12, 24 , plural קרן Jer 1:29); — 𝔊 usually omitted, or reads τὸ τεῖχος; —

1. מוֺאָב ׳ ק Isa 15:1, possibly Rabba, south of Arnon, see BuhlGeogr.270.
2. חֶ֫רֶשׂ ׳ ק Jer 48:31; Jer 48:36, חָ֑רֶשׂ ׳ ק Isa 16:11 = חֲרֶ֫שֶׂת ׳ ק Isa 16:7, חֲדָ֑שֶׂת ׳ ק 2Kgs 3:25 ( Gi חֲרָ֑שֶׁת ׳ ק), perhaps Kerak, south of Rabba, see Buhllb., and compare (on Kerak) PalmerDes. of Ex. ii. 472 DowlingPEQ. Oct. 1896, 327, especially Mauss in de LuynesVoyage à la Mer Morte (1864), ii. 106 ff., iii (Atlas, near the end), 13 plural
