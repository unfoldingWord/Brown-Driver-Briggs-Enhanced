חַשְׁמַל noun [masculine] etymology and exact meaning dubious; evidently some shining substance; AV amber; supposed by Thes (q. v.) and most to be a brilliant amalgam of gold and silver, 𝔊 ἤλεκτρον (see Liddell & Scott under the word 2), ᵑ9 electrum; see also Dl in BaerEzech. xii; only in the combination ׳ כְּעֵין ח like the appearance of ׳ ח Ezek 1:4; Ezek 1:27 בְּעֵין הַחַשְׁמַ֑לָה Ezek 8:2 (on ending הָ֯ see Ges«GKC:90»§ 90, 2 R a; Co strike out)
