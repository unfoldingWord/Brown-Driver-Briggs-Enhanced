Biblical Hebrew

מִיכַל proper name, feminine (according to Ol§ 277 f., contracted from מִיכָאֵל): — younger daughter of Saul, wife of David ( 𝔊 Μελχολ), 1Sam 14:49; 1Sam 18:20; 1Sam 18:27; 1Sam 18:28; 1Sam 19:11; 1Sam 19:12; 1Sam 19:13; 1Sam 19:17 (twice in verse); 1Sam 25:44; 2Sam 3:13; 2Sam 3:14; 2Sam 6:16 = 1Chr 15:29; 2Sam 6:20; 2Sam 6:21; 2Sam 6:23 also 2Sam 21:8 ( 𝔊 Μιχολ), but error for מֵרַב ( 𝔊 L Μεροβ).
