Biblical Hebrew

זֵתָם proper name, masculine (sub זית in Thes, = foregoing) — a Levite (Gershonite) 1Chr 23:8, apparently son of לַעְדָּן and brother of יְחִיאֵל and יוֺאֵל; 1Chr 26:22 apparently grandson of לַעְדָּן, and son of יְחִיאֵלִי (expressly called brother of יוֺאֵל); ᵐ5 Ζεθομ, Ζοθομ, ᵐ5 L Ζηθαν.
