1.
a. complete, bring to an end, finish a thing, task, work, etc., with accusative of thing Exod 5:13; Exod 5:14 (+ infinitive; both J ), 1Kgs 6:9; 1Kgs 6:14; 1Kgs 7:1; Ezek 42:15; Gen 2:2; Gen 16:16 ; Exod 40:33 ( P ), Ruth 2:21; 2Chr 31:1 ; 2Chr 7:11; Ezra 9:1 , so לְכַלֵּא הַפֶּשַׁע Dan 9:24 to make an end of the transgression (compare VB Bev); Lev 23:22 (modified from Lev 19:9 q.see below); accusative omitted 2Chr 31:7 (opposed to הֵחֵל לְ ), 2Chr 24:10; 2Chr 24:14; Neh 3:34.
b. complete a period of time יָמִים Ezek 4:6; Ezek 4:8 complete one's days, enjoy the full measure of human life Job 21:13; Job 36:11, or bring one's years to an end Ps 90:9, with added idea of transitoriness.
c. finish doing a thing, usually followed by ל infinitive Gen 18:33; Gen 24:15; Gen 24:19 (twice in verse); Gen 24:22 (all J ), Gen 17:22; Exod 31:18; Num 4:15 (all P ), Amos 7:2; 1Kgs 1:41; 2Chr 29:29; Ruth 3:3 + 31 t. including 2Sam 11:19, where object of infin. precedes it, see Dr, so Lev 19:9 (compare Lev 23:22 above); + 1Chr 27:24 (לְ infinitive omitted; opposed to הֵחֵל לְ), 2Chr 29:17 + Dan 12:7 (infinitive without לְ ; but see Qal 2.c near the end); followed by מִן infinitive, 1Sam 10:13; 2Sam 6:18 = 1Chr 16:2 ; Exod 34:33; Lev 16:20; Josh 19:51 (all P ), Ezek 43:23.
d. make an end, end, followed by בְּ make an end with, finish dealing with 2Chr 20:23 ; Ezra 10:17 absolute, opposed to הֵחֵל, בַּגָּדוֺל הֵחֵל וּבַקָּטֹן כִּלָּה Gen 44:12 ( J ) he began with the eldest and with the youngest he ended; so infinitive absolute adverb. הָחֵל וְכַלֵּה 1Sam 3:12 a beginning and an ending, i.e. doing it thoroughly (see Dr); also עַדכַּֿלֵּה = utterly 2Kgs 13:17 ; 2Kgs 13:19, to extremity Ezra 9:14 (of ׳ י's anger), עַדלְֿכַלֵּה (late) = completely 2Chr 24:10 (until all had given), 2Chr 31:1 (until all were destroyed).
e. accomplish, fulfil, bring to pass, a thing Ruth 3:18 with accusative of ׳ י's wrath (חֲמָתוֺ) Lam 4:11 so ( חֲמָתִי, אַפִּי) + ב person Ezek 5:13; Ezek 6:12; Ezek 7:8; Ezek 13:15 ; Ezek 20:8 ; Ezek 20:21 .
f. accomplish in thought, determine (compare Qal 1.d) followed by רָעָה Prov 16:30 (|| חשׁב).
