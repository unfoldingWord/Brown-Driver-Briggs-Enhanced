Biblical Hebrew

מַרְבֶּה noun [masculine]

1. abundance; — absolute ׳ מ Isa 33:23, as adverb, in abundance.
2. increase (?), construct לְםַרְבֵּה (sic) Isa 9:6 for the increase of the dominion (take לם as dittograph of foregoing, and read רַבָּה, 𝔊 Gr Che Kit BuhlLex Marti; Du לְמַרְבֵךְ, in view of following לְשָׂלוֺם).
