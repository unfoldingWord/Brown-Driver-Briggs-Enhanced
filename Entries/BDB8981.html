Biblical Hebrew

[שִׁפְעָה], שִׁפְעַת noun feminine abundance, quantity; — apparently absolute שִׁפְעַת 2Kgs 9:17, a multitude (of men), (Bur שִׁפְעָה, or שִׁפְעַת אֲנָשִׁים as Klo Benz and others; > 𝔊 B Kit עָפָר); construct שִׁפְעַת יֵהוּא 2Kgs 9:17 Jehu's multitude; construct, of horses Ezek 26:10, camels Isa 60:6 of waters Job 22:11; Job 38:34.
