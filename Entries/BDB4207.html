BDB4207 [H3768]

Biblical Hebrew

כַּרְפַּס noun masculine cotton (or fine linen) (loan-word from Sanskrit karpâsa , cotton, Persian fine linen; hence also κάρπασος, carbasus, Arabic Talmud כָּרְפְּסָא; compare LagArm. Stud. § 1148); — וּתְכֵלֶת ׳ חוּר כ Esth 1:6 ( ᵐ5 καρπασίνοις): compare Cels.Hierob. ii. 157 ff.
