BDB14 [H35]

Biblical Hebrew

אֲבִיּוֺנָה noun feminine caper-berry (as stimulating desire) Eccl 12:5 (see GFMJBL 1891, 55 ff.; so 𝔊 ᵑ9 , Mishna אביונות, compare NHWB; see also ᵑ6 ; i.e. capparis spinosa, compare RiHWB ; so Thes, Ew De, etc.; but Wetzst in De (German ed. 1875) proposes אֶבְיוֺנָה (as feminine of אביון) the poor soul in sense = נִשְׁמָתוֺ הָאֶבְיוֺנָה compare Symm ᵑ6 , where double translation).
