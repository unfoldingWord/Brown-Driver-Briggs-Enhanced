BDB7281 [H6896 H6897]

Biblical Hebrew

קֵבָה, suffix קֳבָתָהּ (Köl.c. compare Ges«GKC:10h»§ 10h) noun feminine stomach, belly; —

1. absolute ׳ הַקּ of sacrificial victim, assigned to priest Deut 18:3, 𝔊 τὸ ἔνυστρον, i.e. fourth stomach of ruminants (compare Dr), ᵑ9 ventriculum.
2. suffix of woman, apparently more generally, belly Num 25:8 ( P ).
