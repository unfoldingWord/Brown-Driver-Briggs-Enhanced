BDB1581 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1583

גָּזִית noun feminine a cutting, hewing — גָּזִית Exod 20:25 + 10 t., always absolute; — hewing, ׳ אַבְנֵי ג = hewn stones (building-stones) 1Kgs 5:31; 1Chr 22:2; Ezek 40:42 (for tables), compare ׳ אֲבָנִים יְקָרוֺת כְּמִדּוֺת ג 1Kgs 7:9; 1Kgs 7:11 (but Klo strike out as gloss, compare ᵐ5 ); also without אבני = hewn stones Exod 20:25 (altar) Amos 5:11 (׳ בָּֽתֵּי ג) Isa 9:9 (opposed to לְבֵנִים); ׳ טוּרֵי ג 1Kgs 6:36, ׳ טוּרִים ג 1Kgs 7:12 also Lam 3:9 ׳ גָּדַר דַּרְכִּי בְּג.
