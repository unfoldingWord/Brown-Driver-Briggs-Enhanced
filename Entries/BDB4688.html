Biblical Hebrew

מִלְכֹּם proper name [of a divinity] Milcom, god of Ammonites (compare RSSemitic i. 67 ) — שִׁקֻּץ עַמֹּנִים ׳ מ 1Kgs 11:5 (|| עַשְׁתֹּרֶת); compare אֱלֹהֵי בְנֵי עַמּוֺן ׳ מ 1Kgs 11:33 (|| ׳ ע + כְּמוֺשׁ), so read also (for ᵑ0 מֹלֶךְ) 1Kgs 11:7 (|| כְּמוֺשׁ); תּוֺעֲבַת ׳ מ בְּנֵיעַֿמּוֺן 2Kgs 23:13 (|| as 1Kgs 11:33 ); read מִלְכֹּם also for מַלְכָּם Jer 49:1; Jer 49:3 ( 𝔊 Μελχολ, Μελχομ), compare Gie, and Zeph 1:5 ( ᵑ6 ᵑ9 , compare Now), probably 2Sam 12:30 = 1Chr 20:2 (see מֶלֶךְ 5 d), and perhaps Amos 1:15 (whence Jer 49:3 compare Dr Now; — not We GASm).
