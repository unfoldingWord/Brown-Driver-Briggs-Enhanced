Biblical Hebrew

II. תִּקְוָה34 noun feminine hope; — absolute ׳ ת Hos 2:17 +; construct תִּקְוַת Job 8:13 +; suffix תִּקְוָתִי Job 6:8 +, etc.; —

1. hope, Jer 31:17; Lam 3:29; Job 5:16; Job 7:6; Job 11:18; Job 11:20; Job 14:7; Job 14:19; Job 17:15a Job 19:10; Ps 62:6; Prov 19:18 ; Prov 26:12 ; Prov 29:20; Ruth 1:12 ׳ פֶּתַח ת Hos 2:17 ׳ אֲסִירֵי הַתּ Zech 9:12 (i.e. with hope of deliverance).
2. = ground of hope Job 4:6; Ps 71:5 (compare Ps 62:6).
3. things hoped for, outcome, Ezek 19:5; Ezek 37:11; Job 6:8; Job 8:13; Job 17:15b (but read טוֺבָתִי ᵐ5 Me Bi Siegf Beer Bu Du), Job 27:8; Ps 9:19; Prov 10:28; Prov 11:7; Prov 11:23; Prov 23:18; Prov 24:14 (strike out Toy as gloss); ׳ אַחֲרִית וְת Jer 29:11 (i.e. by hendyadis, the hoped-for future).
