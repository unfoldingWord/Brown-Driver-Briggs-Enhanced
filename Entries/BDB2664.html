חָזוּת noun feminine vision, conspicuousness (on formation compare LgBN 202) — absolute ׳ ח Isa 21:2; Dan 8:5; Dan 8:8 construct חָזוּת Isa 29:11 suffix חָזוּתְכֶם Isa 28:18 —

1. vision, oracle of a prophet חָזוּת קָשָׁה הֻגַּדלִֿי Isa 21:2 a hard vision is declared to me; וַתְּהִי לָכֶם חָזוּת הַּכֹּל כְּדִבְרֵי הַסֵּפֶר הֶחָתוּם Isa 29:11 the vision (prophecy) of the whole is become to you as the words of a sealed writing; חָזוּתְכֶם אֶתשְֿׁאוֺל לאֹ תָקוּם Isa 28:18 your agreement with Sheol will not stand, so Thes MV De Che Di and others after Vrss , but Ew§ 156 e renders oracle , and Siegf. Hellseherei, see חֹזֶה 2.
2. conspicuousness in appearance: קֶרֶן הָזוּת a conspicuous horn Dan 8:5 = the great horn vDan 8:8 vDan 8:21 so חָזוּת אַרְבַּע Dan 8:8, but < read אֲחֵרוֺת others, as ᵐ5 Gr Bev.
