BDB3947 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3949

יָכִין proper name, masculine (he will establish); —

1. a Simeonite Gen 46:10 = Exod 6:15; Num 26:12, ᵐ5 Ιαχειμ(ν).
2. name of right-hand pillar before temple 1Kgs 7:21 = 2Chr 3:17, ᵐ5 Ιαχουμ (opposed to בֹּעַז , q. v. f. above; see also RSSemitic i, 191 (208)).
