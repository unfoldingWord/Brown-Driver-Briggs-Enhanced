יְאֹר, יְאוֺר noun masculine Exod 7:21 stream of the Nile, stream, canal (Egyptian loan-word = Egyptian °iotr, °io°r , watercourse, Coptic eioor, ior ; also °iotr±o, °io°r±o , Coptic eiero, iaro , Nile, SteindBAs i. 612 ; in Assyrian ia°uru , stream, °Iaru°û , river Nile, Idib., Hptib. 171 Jägerib 466) — absolute הַיְאֹר Gen 41:1 + 37 t.; הַיְאוֺר Isa 19:8 הַיְאֹ֫רָה Exod 1:22 יְאוֺר Isa 19:7 (3 t. in verse) + 2 t.; כָּאֹר (err. for כַּיְאֹר) Amos 8:8 construct כִּיאֹר Amos 9:5 כִּיאוֺר Amos 8:8 suffix יְאֹרִי Ezek 29:3b ( Co יאר, compare Ezek 29:9 and ᵑ6 ᵑ7 ); plural יְאֹרִים Exod 8:1 + 4 t.; construct יְאֹרֵי Isa 7:18 + 2 t.; יְאוֺרֵי 2Kgs 19:24 suffix יְאֹרֶיךָ Ezek 29:4 (twice in verse); Ezek 29:5 + Ezek 29:4 (strike out ᵐ5 Co) + Ezek 29:10 (Co יְאֹרְךָ); יְאֹרָיו Ezek 29:3 יְאֹדֵיהֶם Exod 7:19; Ps 78:44 —

1. stream of the Nile, river Nile, usually with article Gen 41:1; Gen 41:2; Gen 41:3 (twice in verse); Gen 41:18; Exod 1:22; Exod 2:3; Exod 2:5 (twice in verse); Exod 4:9 (twice in verse); Exod 7:15; Exod 7:17; Exod 7:18 (3 t. in verse); Exod 7:20 (twice in verse); Exod 7:21 (3 t. in verse); Exod 7:24 (twice in verse); Exod 7:28; Exod 8:5; Exod 8:7; Exod 17:5 (all J E ), Amos 8:8 ; Amos 9:5; Isa 19:7 (3 t. in verse); Isa 19:8 ; Isa 23:3 (|| שִׁחֹר), Isa 23:10; Jer 46:7; Jer 46:8 (simile of Egyptian invasion), Ezek 29:3b (read יאר , see above), Ezek 29:9; Zech 10:11 more fully יְאֹר מִצְרַיִם Amos 8:8; Amos 9:5.
2. plural:
a. Nile-arms, Nile-canals, יְאֹרֵי מִצְרַים Isa 7:18 יְאֹרֵי מָצוֺר Isa 19:6 (|| נְהָרוֺת), Isa 37:25 = 2Kgs 19:24 see also Nah 3:8; Exod 7:19; Exod 8:1 (both P ; || נְהָרֹת, אֲגַמִּים, etc.), Ps 78:44 (|| נֹזְלִים ), Ezek 29:3; Ezek 29:4 (twice in verse); Ezek 29:5; Ezek 29:10 + Ezek 29:4 ᵑ0 (but strike out ᵐ5 Co), Ezek 30:12.
b. watercourses in general, יְאֹרִים רַחֲבֵי יָדָ֑יִם Isa 33:21 (|| נְהָרִים; compare Late Hebrew יְאוֺר, late Aramaic יְאוֺרָא).
3. shafts, made in mining Job 28:10 (בַּצּוּרוֺת).
4. יְאֹר, singular, of Tigris, Dan 12:5 (twice in verse); Dan 12:6; Dan 12:7 (compare Dan 10:4).
