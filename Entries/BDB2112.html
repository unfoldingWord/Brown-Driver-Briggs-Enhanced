BDB2112 [H3082 H3122]

BDB2111 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2113

יְהוֺנָדָב, יוֺנָדָב proper name, masculine (׳ י is noble, or is liberal, or hath impelled, compare נְדַבְיָה) —

1. ׳ יְהוֺ son of Rechab & chief of the Rechabites ( ᵐ5 Ἰωαναδαβ) 2Kgs 10:15 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 10:23; Jer 35:8; Jer 35:14; Jer 35:16; Jer 35:18 = ׳ יוֺ Jer 35:6; Jer 35:10; Jer 35:19.
2. ׳ יְהוֺ nephew of David ( ᵐ5 Ἰωναδαβ, Ἰωναδαμ; ᵐ5 L Ἰωαναθαν) 2Sam 13:5 = ׳ יוֺ 2Sam 13:3 (twice in verse); 2Sam 13:32; 2Sam 13:35 (apparently called Jonathan 2Sam 21:21; 1Chr 20:7, compare יְהוֺנָתָן3)
