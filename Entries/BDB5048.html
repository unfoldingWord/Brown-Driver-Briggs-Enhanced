BDB5048 [H5121]

Biblical Hebrew

נוית Kt (i.e. נָוִית, or נָָֽוְיַת, Ginsb ), נָיוֺת Qr ; probably proper name [of a location] in Ramah (possibly = habitations, but dubious, see Dr 1Sam 19:18 HPS); — adode of prophets 1Sam 19:18; 1Sam 19:19; 1Sam 19:22; 1Sam 19:23 (twice in verse) and 1Sam 20:1 (where Baer נוות Kt ); followed by בָּרָמָה (except 1Sam 19:18); 𝔊 Ἀυαθ (ν dropped out after ἐν), so 𝔊 L A Ναυιωθ.
