Biblical Hebrew

עוּת verb very dubious; only in לָדַעַת לָעוּת אֶתיָֿעֵף דָּבָר Isa 50:4 usually to help, so Ges Del Di RyKau and others, but in that case an Aramaism (Aramaic * עות = Arabic , q. v. below עוּשׁ); text probably corrupt, 𝔊 OortThT 1891, 469 לְעִתּוֺ, and strike out אֶתיָֿעֵף; Klo CheComm. לִרְעוֺת 'teach, edify,' Gr לַעֲנוֺת, CheHpt. לְהַחֲיוֺת.
