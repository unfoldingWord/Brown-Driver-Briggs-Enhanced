BDB4705 [H4471]

Biblical Hebrew

מַמְרֵא proper name, masculine and [of a location] Mamre, only in Genesis:

1. in J only אֵלֹנֵי מַמְרֵא terebinths of Mamre (proper name, masculine?), Gen 13:18 (defined by אֲשֶׁר בְּחֶבְרוֺן ), Gen 18:1 (in both the dwelling-place of Abraham).
2. proper name, masculine in Gen 14:13 (הָאֱמֹרִי ׳ אֵלֹנֵי מ), compare Gen 14:24 (where ally of Abraham)
3. proper name [of a location] in P : near Abraham's place of burial Gen 23:17; Gen 25:9; Gen 49:30; Gen 50:3 apparently identified with Hebron Gen 23:19; Gen 35:27, compare Di. — 𝔊 Μαμβρη.
