BDB9155 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB9157

תָּכֵּנִית noun feminine measurement, proportion; — absolute Ezek 43:10 (< read תָּכְּ נִיתוֺ ᵐ5 Ew Hi Co Toy and others); ׳חוֺתֵם ת Ezek 28:12 thou wert one sealing up (the) measure (RVm), i.e. exhibiting perfect proportion; Manuscripts Sm and others ׳חוֺחַם ת the seal of (perfect) proportion; but dubious, see Commentaries
