BDB5110 [H5155]

Biblical Hebrew

[נְחִילָה] noun feminine meaning unknown; only plural in phrase אֶלהַֿנְּחִילוֺת Ps 5:1 (title); most conjecture = נְחִלּוֺת √חלל = חָלִיל flute; see against this BaeEinl. xi, who thinks (compare 𝔊 ὑπὲρ τῆς Κληρονομούσης = אֶלהַֿנֹּחֶלֶת [compare ᵑ9 ], Jerome [ Aq Symm] pro hereditatibus = אֶלהַֿנְּחָלֹת) possibly designation of a melody.
