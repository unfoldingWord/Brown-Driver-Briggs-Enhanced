BDB1107 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1109

בִּכְּרִי proper name, masculine (youthful) —

1. a Benjamite 2Sam 20:1; 2Sam 20:2; 2Sam 20:6; 2Sam 20:7; 2Sam 20:10; 2Sam 20:13; 2Sam 20:21; 2Sam 20:22 only in phrase שֶׁבַע בֶּןבִּֿכְּרִי.
2. perhaps adjective [of a people] plural with article הַבִּכְּרִים 2Sam 20:14 the Bichrites (i.e. family of Sheba`), ᵑ0 הַבֵּרִים; compare ᵐ5 ἐν Ξαρρει = (ב)כרי; so Klo Dr.
