מַרְאֶה102 noun masculine Exod 3:3 sight, appearance, vision; — ׳ מ absolute Gen 12:11 +; construct מַרְאֵה Deut 28:34 +; suffix מַרְאֵהוּ Joel 2:4 +, מַרְאֶהָ Lev 13:4 +; apparently plural construct מַרְאֵי Eccl 11:9 ( Köii. 1, 112), suffix (probably in fact singular Ges93 ss) מַרְאַיִח Song 2:14 (twice in verse), מַרְאֵיהֶן Nah 2:5 +, etc.; —

1. Psalms
a. sight, phenomenon, spectacle Exod 3:3 ( J ).
b. appearance ׳ נֶחְמָד לְמ Gen 2:9 ( J ) desirable in appearance, ׳ גָּדוֺל לְמ Josh 22:10 appearance of man (or woman), Judg 13:6 (twice in verse) (angel), Isa 52:14 (servant of ׳ י), Song 5:15; Dan 8:15; Dan 10:18 = outward person (opposed to inner man) 1Sam 16:7 visible form Song 2:14 (twice in verse); Dan 1:13 (twice in verse); Dan 1:15 ׳ בְּמ Num 12:8 ( E ; so read ⅏ 𝔊 ᵑ6 ᵑ7 Di, for ᵑ0 מַרְאֶה), i.e. in personal presence ( < Ew PatHpt {abbrev}לֹא בְמ); כְּמַרְאֵה סוּסִים מַרְאֵהוּ Joel 2:4 of crocodile Job 41:2 ׳ כְּבוֺד י ׳ מ Exod 24:17 ( P ), הַנֶּגַע ׳ מ Lev 13:3, אֵֿשׁ ׳ מ Num 9:15; Num 9:16, בָּרָק ׳ מ Dan 10:6 +; especially Ezekiel appearance person or of thing (27 t., sometimes redundant), Dan 1:5; Dan 1:13; Dan 1:26; Dan 8:2; Dan 10:1 +, מָתְנָיו ׳ מִמּ Dan 1:27 (twice in verse); Dan 8:2 (|| מִמָּתְנָיו) of beauty וִיפֵה מַרְאֶה fair of appearance Gen 39:6 ( J ; +יְפֵהתֹֿאַר), compare 1Sam 17:42 (read עֶלֶם [q. v.] for עִם); feminine ׳ (אִשָּׁה) יְפַתמֿ Gen 12:11 ( J ), Gen 29:17 ( E ; +יְפַת תּאַֹר), 2Sam 14:27 of kine ׳ יְפוֺת (הַ)מ Gen 41:2; Gen 41:4 ( E ), opposed to ׳ רָעוֺת (הַ)מ Gen 41:3 Gen 41:4, מַרְאֵיהֶן רַע Gen 41:21(all E ); of women also ׳ טֹבַת מ Gen 24:16 ; Gen 26:7 (both J ), 2Sam 11:2; Esth 1:11; Esth 2:3; Esth 2:7, ׳ טוֺבוֺת מ Esth 2:2 of boys ׳ טוֺבֵי מ Dan 1:4, compare ׳ מ alone in וְנֶחְמְדֵהוּ ׳ לֹאמֿ (of suffering servant of ׳ י) Isa 53:2 ׳ אִישׁ מ 2Sam 23:21 (but read as || 1Chr 11:23 אִישׁ מִדָּה We Dr and others).
c. appearance, sight, vision Num 8:4 ( P ).
2. in general what is seen, עֵינָיו ׳ מ Isa 11:3 i.e. what his outward eyes see (compare 1Sam 16:7 1 b), עֵינֶיהָ ׳ לִמ Ezek 23:16 , עְינֶיךָ אֲשֶׁר תִּרְאֶה ׳ מ Deut 28:34; Deut 28:67, ׳ לְכָלמֿ עֵינֵי הַכֹּהֵן Lev 13:12 ( P ).
3. specifically a (supernatural) vision (in Ezekiel, Daniel; often accusative of cognate meaning with verb with ראה): Ezek 8:4; Ezek 11:24; Ezek 43:3 (3 t. in verse), read also מַרְאֵה הָרֶכֶב Ezek 43:3 (for מַרְאוֺת see I. מַרְאָה above); ׳ בַּמּ Ezek 11:24 ; Dan 8:16; Dan 8:27; Dan 9:23; Dan 10:1 ׳ מ הָעֶרֶב וְהַבֹּקֶר Dan 8:26.
4. sight, vision = power of seeing (and enjoying), late: עֵינַיִם ׳ מ Eccl 6:9, הַלֵךְ בְּדַרְּכֵי לִבְּךָ וּבְמַרְאֵי עֵינֶיךָ Eccl 11:9 .
