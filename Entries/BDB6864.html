BDB6863 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6865

פְּתוֺר proper name, of a location home of Balsam; — with ה locative פְּת֫וֺרָה Num 22:5, φαθουρα, A Βαθουρα; אֲרַם ׳פ נַהֲרַיִם Deut 23:5, but omitted ׳פ ᵐ5; — compare Assyrian Pitru (on western bank of upper Euphrase) SchrKGF 220; COT Num 22:5 Dl Pa 269 Dr Hastings DB; Egyptian Pe-d-ru WMMAs.u.Eur:291.
