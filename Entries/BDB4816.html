BDB4815 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4817

מְרָיוֺה proper name, masculine (on form compare LagBN 51); —

1. descendant of Aaron:
a. grandfather of Ahitub and great-grandfather of Zadok 1Chr 5:32; 1Chr 5:33; 1Chr 6:37; Ezra 7:3 ᵐ5 Μαρειηλ, Μαρερωθ, Μαραιωθ, etc.
b. as son of Ahitub and father of Zadok 1Chr 9:11 ; Neh 11:11 ᵐ5 Μαρμωθ Μαρ(α)ιωθ.
2. name of a priestly house Neh 12:15 (probably error for מְרֵמוֺת , q. v., Neh 12:3), ᵐ5 L Μαριμωθ.
