Biblical Hebrew

II. [חָרֵשׁ] verb

1. be silent, dumb, speechless;
2. be deaf; chiefly poet. (Late Hebrew Pi`el make deaf; Aramaic , be dumb, deaf; Arabic be dumb, speechless, compare Assyrian —arâšu , restrain, according to DlPr 100 ; see LagBN 120)
