תִּמְנָה, תִּמְנָ֫תָה proper name [of a location] (probably = portion, territory); — (חִמְנָה Josh 15:10; Josh 15:57; 2Chr 28:18 תִּמְנָ֫תֶה [as nominative see GFM Judg 14:1 ] Josh 19:43; Judg 14:1; Judg 14:2; Judg 14:5 with ה locative תִּמְנָ֫תָה Gen 38:12; Gen 38:13; Gen 38:14; Judg 14:1; Judg 14:5 ᵐ5 Θαμνα, Θαμναθα): —

1. place in hill-country of Judah Gen 38:12; Gen 38:13; Gen 38:14. probably = Josh 15:57. Site unknown (compare Di Gen 38:12 RobBR ii. 17 n.).
2. place on border of Judah Josh 15:10; Josh 19:43 (assigned to Dan), Judg 14:1 (twice in verse); Judg 14:2; Judg 14:5 (twice in verse); 2Chr 28:18. Modern Tibneh, approximately 15 miles south of west from Jerusalem compare RobBR ii.17 BuhlGeogr. 196.
