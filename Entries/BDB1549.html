BDB1549 [H1465]

Biblical Hebrew

II. גֵּוָה noun feminine back — (so also Di Hoffm SS; 𝔊 Thes De AV RV body = גְּוִיָּה, compare BaZMG 1887, 605) Job 20:25 וַיֵּצֵיא מִגֵּוָה it comes out from the back (of arrow which has struck a fugitive, and is then extracted; Hoffm reads מִגֵּוֺה). — I. גֵּוָה below גאה.
