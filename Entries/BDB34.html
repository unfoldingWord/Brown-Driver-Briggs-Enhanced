BDB34 [H74]

Biblical Hebrew

אֲבִינֵר proper name, masculine (my father is Nêr, or is a lamp compare 2Sam 21:17 accusative to LagBN 75 = אֲבֶן (= בֶּן) + נֵר = son of Ner; compare 𝔊 Ἀβεννηρ) — so only 1Sam 14:50, elsewhere אַבְנֵר — cousin of Saul, and captain of his host 1Sam 14:50 ; 1Sam 14:51; 1Sam 17:55 (3 t. in verse) + 52 t. 1 & 2 Samuel; + 1Kgs 2:5; 1Kgs 2:32; 1Chr 26:28; 1Chr 27:21.
