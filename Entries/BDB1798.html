Biblical Hebrew

דָּאָה noun feminine a bird of prey, possibly kite ( ᵑ9 Saad BoHieroz. ii. 191 Di Lev 11:14 Aramaic דַּיְתָא, ; Late Hebrew דַּיָּה of different birds of prey; name probably from flying, swooping), Lev 11:14 forbidden as food; compare also Deut 14:13 וְהָרָאָה ואתהָֿאַיָּה והַדַּיָּה, where for הראה read הַדָּאָה & strike out הַדַּיָּה; so ⅏ 𝔊 ; compare Di l.c.
