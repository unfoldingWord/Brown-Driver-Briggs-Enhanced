Biblical Hebrew

[סְוֵן, < סְרָן], סְוֵנֵה proper name [of a location] Syene, city on southern border of Egypt, toward Ethiopia, only in phrase (וְעַדגְּֿבוּל כּוּשׁ) ׳ מִמִּגְדֹּל ס Ezek 29:10 compare Ezek 30:6 , read probably סְוֵ֫נָה or < סְוָ֫נָה (Coptic Suan ), with ה locative (JDMich Sm Co Berthol) = Egyptian Sun , Coptic Suan, modern Aswân; ᵐ5 ἔως Συηνης; read probably סְוָן also Ezek 30:16 (for ᵑ0 סִין q. v.) ᵐ5 Συηνη. — See further JosBJ «JOSEPHUS:Wars of the Jews 4:10»iv. 10, 5 Straboxxxii.817-820, BrugschGeogr. Inscr. i. 155; Reiseber. aus Aegypt. 247 BdEgypt.4.323f. BudgeNile 284.
