BDB8040 [H7494]

Biblical Hebrew

רַ֫עַשׁ noun masculine Jer 10:22 quaking, shaking; — ׳ ר absolute 1Kgs 19:11 +, construct Nah 3:2; Job 41:21 —

1. specifically earthquake, Amos 1:1; Zech 14:5; 1Kgs 19:11 (twice in verse); 1Kgs 19:12 figurative of ׳ י's judgement Isa 29:6; Ezek 38:19 compare (in vision) קוֺל גָּרוֺל ׳ ר Ezek 3:12; Ezek 3:13 probably also Ezek 37:7 ( 𝔊 Thes Co Krae; > rustling, rattling, Hi-Sm Toy); hence hyperbole of shaking of earth by tramping of warriors Isa 9:4, by war-chariots Jer 47:3, compare Jer 10:22, or wheels Nah 3:2.
2. quaking, trembling, of person Ezek 12:18.
3. shaking, quivering, of horse eager to start Job 39:24 (+ רֹגֶז), of dart Job 41:21 (al. rushing sound).
