תּוֺשָׁב noun masculine Lev 25:45 sojourner, only P ( H ) and late; absolute ׳ ת Gen 23:4 + 6 t; construct תּוֺשַׁב Lev 22:10 suffix תּוֺשָֽׁבְךָ Lev 25:6 plural תּוֺשָׁבִים vLev 25:6 + 2 t.; construct תּשָׁבֵי 1Kgs 17:1, but read תִּשְׁבֶּה (q. v.) ᵐ5 Ew Th Hi; — sojourner, apparently of a more temporaty and dependent (Lev 22:10 ; Lev 25:6) kind than the גֵּר (with which it is often joined): || שָׂכִיר Exod 12:45 ( P ), Lev 22:10 (כֹּהֵן ׳ ת apriest's sojourner), Lev 25:6; Lev 25:40 (all H ); הַתּוֺשָׁבִים הַגָּרִים עִמָּכֶם vLev 25:45 (c. participle גָּר also vLev 25:6); with עִם, also Lev 25:47 (twice in verse) ( H ); with בְּתוֺךְ Num 35:15 ( P ).
