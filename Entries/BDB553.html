(אַמִּץ) אַמִּיץ adjective mighty — ׳ א absolute 2Sam 15:12 + 2 t.; construct Job 9:4 + 2 t. — mighty, of Absa-lom's conspiracy 2Sam 15:12 elsewhere of persons; as substantive, instrum. of ׳ י (i.e. Assyrians) Isa 28:2 (|| חזָק; לִבּוֺ ׳ א = valiant, conqueror Amos 2:16 of ׳ י, כֹּחַ ׳ חֲכַם לֵבָב וְא Job 9:4 compare ׳ כֹּחַ א Job 9:19 (see Di & RV ), כֹּחַ ׳ א Isa 40:26 read probably אֹמֶץ (ᵐ5) (|| רֹב אוֺנִים).
