BDB4145 [H4375]

Biblical Hebrew

מַכְפֵּלָה proper name [of a location] near Hebron, where the patriarchs and their wives were buried, only in P , always with article; ׳ שׂדה אשׁר בַּמּ Gen 23:17, ׳ מְעָדַת הַמּ the cave of Machpelah Gen 23:9, Gen 25:9, מְעָרַת ׳ שְׂדֵה הַמּ Gen 23:19; Gen 50:13, ׳ מְעָרָה אֲשֶׁר בִּשְׂדֵה הַמּ Gen 49:30. — compare BuhlGeogr. 160, 161. — (Evidently originally appellative, but meaning dubious; 𝔊 τό διπλοῦν; according to Thes = part, lot , portion, as Ethiopic ).
