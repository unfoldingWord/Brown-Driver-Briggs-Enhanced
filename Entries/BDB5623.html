Biblical Hebrew

II. סֶ֫לַע proper name [of a location] in Edom, ׳ מֵהַהַסּ Judg 1:36 (strike out מֵ, compare GFM); ׳ הַסּ captured in war 2Kgs 14:7 (and called יָקְתְאֵל); ׳ מִסּ Isa 16:1 site dubious; old identification with Petra denied, plausibly, by BuhlEdom GFMJu, 36 (who thinks of an actual cliff ), but held Benz 2Kgs 14:7 BdPal (1898), 206; ᵐ5 (ἡ)πέτρα.
