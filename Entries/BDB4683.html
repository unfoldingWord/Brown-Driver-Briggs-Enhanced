BDB4683 [H4441]

Biblical Hebrew

מַלְכִּיָּהוּ (only Jer 38:6), מַלְכִּיָּה proper name, masculine (my king is Yah; compare GrayProp. N. 119 f., 146 and elsewhere); — 𝔊 Μελχ(ε)ια(ς). —

1. father of Pashhur Jer 21:1; Jer 38:1, compare 1Chr 9:12 (priest), Neh 11:12.
2. a prince Jer 38:6.
3. priests:
a. 1Chr 24:9.
b. Neh 12:42.
4. one of the sealed Neh 10:4 perhaps also Neh 8:4.
5. a Levite 1Chr 6:25.
6. various men:
a. Ezra 10:25 a.
b. Ezra 10:25 b.
c. Ezra 10:31 = Neh 3:11.
d. Neh 3:14.
e. Neh 3:31.
