נֵד noun masculine Josh 3:13 heap of waters; of Red Sea Exod 15:8 (song), Ps 78:13 of Jordan Josh 3:13; Josh 3:16; Ps 33:7 (simile). נֵד קָצִיר Isa 17:11 harvest-heap is perhaps corrupt: Ges Ew Di Du derive from נוד, reading (probably) נָד (Di) fled is the harvest; CheHpt proposes לָכֵן therefore; BuhlLex 13 עַד, after ᵐ5 ᵑ7 .
