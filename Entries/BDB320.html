BDB319 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB321

אֲחַזְיָ֫הוּ, אֲחַזְיָה, אַחְזַי proper name, masculine (Yah(u) hath grasped; ᵐ5 Ὀχοζ(ε)ιας 1Kgs 22:52 etc., compare LagBN 53)

compare Arabic VIII, + = adopt, hence perhaps ׳ י hath adopted.
1. king of Israel, son of Ahab 1Kgs 22:40; 1Kgs 22:50; 1Kgs 22:52; 2Kgs 1:18; 2Chr 20:37 (אֲחַזְיָהוּ); + 2Kgs 1:2; 2Chr 20:35 ( אֲחַזְיָה).
2. king of Judah, son of Joram 2Kgs 8:24; 2Kgs 8:25; 2Kgs 9:21; 2Kgs 10:13; 2Kgs 11:1; 2Kgs 12:19; 2Kgs 13:1; 2Kgs 14:13; 2Kgs 1:11; 2Kgs 22:1 + 15 t. 2 Kings, 1 Chronicles (all אֲחַזְיָהוּ); 2Kgs 9:16; 2Kgs 9:23; 2Kgs 9:27; 2Kgs 9:29; 2Kgs 11:2 ( אֲחַזְיָה).
3. אַחְזַי a priest Neh 11:13 (for which יַחְזֵרָה 1Chr 9:12 see SmListen, Tafel).
