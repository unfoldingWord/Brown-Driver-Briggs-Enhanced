BDB7571 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7573

מִקְרֶה noun masculine 1Sam 6:9 accident, chance, fortune; — absolute ׳ מ 1Sam 6:9 +; construct מִקְרֵה Eccl 2:15 (so read also Eccl 3:19 (twice in verse) with ᵐ5 RuëtKau Wild, for ᵑ0 מִקְרֶה); —

1. accident, chance, 1Sam 6:9; 1Sam 20:26 with קָרָה Ruth 2:2 (see ׳ ק 1).
2. in Ecclesiastes, fortune, fate, ׳ מ אֶחָד יִקְרֶה אֶתכֻּֿלָּם Eccl 2:14 compare Eccl 2:15, Eccl 3:19 (3 t. in verse); Eccl 9:2; Eccl 9:3.
