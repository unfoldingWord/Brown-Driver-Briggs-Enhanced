נְתַנְיָ֫הוּ, נְתַנְיָה proper name, masculine (compare GrayProp. N. 294), ᵐ5 Ναθανιας; —

1. נְתַנְיָ֫הנּ father of Jehudi Jer 36:14.
2. ׳ נ father of Ishmael Gedaliah's murderer Jer 40:8; Jer 41:9 = נְתַנְיָה 2Kgs 25:23; 2Kgs 25:25; Jer 40:14; Jer 40:15; Jer 41:1 + 9 t. Jer 41.
3. נְתַנְיָ֫הוּ Levite name:
a. 2Chr 17:8.
b. 1Chr 25:12 = נְתַנְיָה v1Chr 25:2.
