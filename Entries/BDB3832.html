Biblical Hebrew

שָׁרוֺן proper name [of a location] Sharon (plausibly connected with √ ישׁר by Thes Ges12 and others, compare GASmGeogr. 52; = *יְשָׁרוֺן; Phoenician שרן) — strictly noun masculine Isa 65:10 plain, level; —

1. ׳ הַשּׁ (with article), name of maritime plain on Mediterranean, from Joppa northward, noted for fertility, Isa 33:9 (|| בָּשָׁן, כַּרְמֶל; 𝔊 ὁ Σαρων), compare הֲדַר כַּרְמֶל וְהַשָּׁרוֺן Isa 35:2; Isa 65:10 (|| עֵמֶק עָכוֺר; 𝔊 τῷ δρυμῷ); ׳ חֲבַצֶּלֶת הַשּׁ Song 2:1 ( 𝔊 ἄνθος τοῦ πεδίου); ׳ הַבָּקָר הָרֹעִים בַּשּׁ 1Chr 27:29 so also Josh 12:18 where read ׳ מֶלֶךְ אֲפֵק לַשּׁ, so Di WeSam 55 Ges12 after 𝔊 , compare RSOTJC 2, 273, 435. — On this plain see GASmGeogr. 147 ff. BuhlGeogr. § 65.
2. מִגְרְשֵׁי שָׁרוֺן, region east of Jordan 1Chr 5:16 (|| גִּלְעָד, בָּשָׁן; 𝔊 τὰ περίχωρα Γεριαμ, 𝔊 A Σαρων); identified by many with הַמִּישׁוֺר (Deut 3:10 etc., see above)
