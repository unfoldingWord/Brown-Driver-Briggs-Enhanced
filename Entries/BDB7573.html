קוֺרָה noun feminine rafter, beam (properly a thing meeting, fitting into, another); — suffix קֹרָתִי Gen 19:8 ( J ) = my roof-tree; plural קֹרוֺת rafters, absolute 2Chr 3:7, construct || 1Kgs 6:15b (reading קוֺרוֺת הַסִּפֻּן 𝔊 Th Kit Benz, for ᵑ0 קִירוֺת), + 1Kgs 7:7b (reading הַקּוֺרוֺת for הַקַּרְקָ֑ע, see ׳ ק); Song 1:17 (|| רחיטנו Kt , רָהִיטֵנוּ Qr ); more gen beam, singular absolute קוֺרָה 2Kgs 6:2; 2Kgs 6:5.
