כִּלְמַד proper name, of a location (si vera lectio) Ezek 27:23, named after אַשּׁוּר, ᵐ5 Ξαρμαν; = modern Kalwâdha near Bagdad, according to G. SmithTSBA i, 61 DlPa 206, compare SchrCOT; but text dubious see Co; ᵑ7 ומדי, whence MezStadt Harran 34 כָּלמָֿדַי all Media; JKi Hi Co כְּלִמּוּד (רְכֻלָּתֵךְ) Asshur was as thine apprentice (see below למד) in trading; but sense not very probably
