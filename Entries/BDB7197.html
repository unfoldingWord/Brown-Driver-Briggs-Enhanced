צְפַנְיָה(וּׅ proper name, masculine Σοφονιας (׳ י hath treasured; Old Hebrew צפניהו, Phoenician צפנבעל); —

1. priest, יָה- Jer 21:1; Jer 22:25; Jer 22:29; Jer 52:24 = יָהוּ- 2Kgs 25:18 ( ᵐ5 L Σαφανιας), Jer 37:3. Elsewhere יָה-:
2. the prophet Zeph 1:1.
3. a Judaean Zech 6:10; Zech 6:14.
4. ancestor of Heman 1Chr 6:21 (Σαφανιας; = אוּרִיאֵל v.1Chr 6:9).
