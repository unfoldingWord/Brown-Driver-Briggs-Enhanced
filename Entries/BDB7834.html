Biblical Hebrew

רָזָה verb be or grow lean (Arabic grow thin and weak); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular רָזָה Zeph 2:11 object false gods, apparently make lean ( ᵑ9 attenuavit; ᵐ5 is ἐξολεθρύσει), but sense strange, < רִזָּה (see Arabic above), yet even this very doubtful.
Niph`al be made lean, Imperfect 3 masculine singular מִשְׁמַן בְּשָׂרוֺ יֵרָזֶה Isa 17:4.
