BDB6063 [H5919]

Biblical Hebrew

עַכְשׁוּב noun masculine usually asp, viper (so 𝔊 ᵑ9 ; perhaps conject. from sense and || נָחָשׁ), but perhaps = (? corrupt for) עַכָּבִישׁ spider (q. v.; compare ᵑ7 on the passage ); — ׳ חֲמַת ע Ps 140:4 a viper's (? spider's) venom is under their lips (see especially Che and references).

עַל see עלה עֹל see III. עלל .
