II. עֲרֹעֵר10, עֲרוֺעֵר5, עַרְעֹר 1 proper name [of a location] Αροηρ, MI26 ערער; —

1. city on north bank of Arnon, southern limit of east Jordan Israel, עֲרֹעֵר Num 32:34 ( J E ), Deut 2:36 ; Deut 3:12; Deut 4:48; Josh 12:2 ( D ) 2Kgs 10:33; 1Chr 5:8 עֲרוֺעֵר Josh 13:9 ( D ), Josh 13:16 ( P ), 2Sam 24:5 Moabitish Jer 48:19 (compare MI26 ); once עַרְעֹר Judg 11:26 modern `Ar`âir Buhl Geogr. 269, TristrMoab 129 ff.
2. city near Rabbah in Ammon, עֲרֹעֵר Judg 11:33, ׳ עֲרוֺ Josh 13:25 ( P ), site unknown.
3. city in southern Judah, עֲרֹעֵר 1Sam 30:28 ( 𝔊 Αροηρ + Αμμαδει, latter originally according to We, indicating form ערערה; 𝔊 L Παγουηλ); + Josh 15:22 reading ׳ ע (or עַרְעָרָה) for ᵑ0 עַדְעָרָה, 𝔊 B Αρουηλ, We 1Sam 30:28 (compare Dr HPS), Dion the passage Benn Steuern; possibly `Ar`âra approximately 9 miles south of east from Beersheba, but compare BuhlGeogr. 183. — Isa 17:2 read עָרֶיהָ עֲדֵי עַד 𝔊 Lo LagSemitic i. 30 Che BuhlLex and others (for ᵑ0 עָרֵי עֲרֹעֵר). — See also CheEncy.
