BDB2361 [H2139]

Biblical Hebrew

זַכּוּר proper name, masculine 𝔊 Ζακχυρ, Ζακχουρ, etc.: —

1. a Reubenite Num 13:4 ( P ).
2. a Simeonite 1Chr 4:26.
3. Levites:
a. 1Chr 24:27 .
b. an Asaphite 1Chr 25:2 (Σακχους), 1Chr 25:10 (Ζαχχουθ), Neh 12:35 ( Ζακχουρ), compare זִכְרִי above 3 c. c. Neh 10:13 compare Neh 13:13
4. companion of Ezra, Ezra 8:14 Qr ( Kt זבוד, compare above).
5. contemporary of Nehemiah, Neh 3:2.
