BDB949 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB951

תְּבוּאָה noun feminine proventus, product, revenue — ׳תּ absolute Ps 107:37 + 3 t.; construct תְּבוּאַת Lev 23:39 + 11 t.; suffix תְּבוּאָתִי Job 31:12; Prov 8:19 תְּבוּאָתְּךָ Deut 14:28 + 2 t.; תְּבוּאָתֶ֑ךָ Prov 3:9 תְּכוּאָתוֺ Lev 19:25 + Ezek 48:18 Qr (Kt תבואתה), תְּבוּאָתֹה Jer 2:3 תְּבוּאָתָהּ Exod 23:10 + 7 t. + Ezek 48:18 Kt (so Co); תְּבוּאָתֵנוּ Lev 25:20 plural תְּבוּאֹת Lev 25:15 + 4 t.; תְּכוּאוֺת Prov 14:4; Prov 16:8 תְּבוּאֹתֵיכֶם Jer 12:13 —

1. product, yield, usually of earth (= crops, etc.) הָאָרֶץ ׳תּ Exod 23:10; Lev 19:25; Lev 23:29; Lev 25:3; Lev 25:7 (used as food for man & beast, compare vLev 25:22) Josh 5:12, compare Neh 9:37; Lev 25:15; Lev 25:16, also Ezek 48:18 in Gen 47:24 ׳בַּתּ must = of the crops (בּ partitive, compare בְּ
I. 2. b; ᵐ5 strike out בְּ); שָׂדֶה׳ת 2Kgs 8:6; 2Chr 31:5 כֶּרֶם ׳תּ Deut 22:9 compare Ps 107:37 ׳לָחֶם תּ חָאֲדָמָה Isa 30:23 גֹּרֶן ׳תּ & יָ֑קֶב ׳תּ Num 18:30 זֶרַע ׳תּ Deut 14:22 דָּגָן ׳תּ 2Chr 32:38 as property of husbandmen, or people Lev 25:20; Lev 16:25; Deut 26:12; Prov 3:9 crops as determined by season, שָׁ֑מֶשׁ ׳תּ Deut 33:14 (|| גֶרֶשׁ יְרָחִים); yield of a year שָׁנָה Lev 25:12; Lev 25:22 compare vLev 25:21 ׳עָשָׂה תּ, subject שָׁנָה.
2. a. income, revenue, in general Job 31:12 (almost = possessions) Prov 10:16; Prov 14:4; Prov 15:6; Prov 16:8; Eccl 5:9 compare Isa 23:3 (revenue of Tyre from trade with Egypt in bread stuffs).
\ \ b. figurative gain of wisdom חָכְמָה ׳תּ Prov 3:14; Prov 8:19 product of lips (שְׂפָתָיו ׳תּ) Prov 18:20, i.e. results of his speech (|| פְּרִי פִיאִֿישׁ); of Israel as Yahweh's product Jer 2:3 of Israel's wickedness Jer 12:13.
