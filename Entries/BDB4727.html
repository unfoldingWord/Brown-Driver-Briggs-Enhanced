BDB4727 [H4511]

BDB4726 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4728

מִנִּית proper name [of a location] in Ammonite territory, site unknown (4 Roman miles from Heshbon Euseb.Onom. 253, 33; compare BuhlGeogr. 266); — Judg 11:33 ( ᵐ5 ἄχρις Ἀρνών, A εἰς Σεμωειθ, ᵐ5 L Σεμενειθ); ׳ חִטֵּי מ Ezek 27:17 wheat of Minnîth (compare wheat from Ammon 2Chr 27:5) so Berthol , but dubious; Co conjectures חִטִּים וּנְכאֹת wheat and storax.

[מְנֻסָה] see מְנוּסָה below נוס.
