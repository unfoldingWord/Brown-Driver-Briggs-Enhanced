Biblical Hebrew

II. מוֺרֶה noun masculine Isa 30:20 teacher; — absolute in אֵלוֺן מוֺרֶה Gen 12:6 ( J ) = אֵלוֺנֵי מֹרֶה Deut 11:30 ( ⅏ 𝔊 אֵלוֺן), the teacher's terebinth (see אֵלוֺן ) near Shechem; compare גִּבְעַת הַמּוֺרֶה Judg 7:1 teacher's hill near the plain of Jezreel, probably Little Hermon, Nebî Daµî BdPal 244; the terebinth being a holy tree from which divine teaching was given, and the hill of the teacher the seat of a holy place whence divine teaching was given; see also (of God) Job 36:22 here belong probably likewise מוֺרָ֑י Prov 5:13, and מוֺרֶיךָ Isa 30:20 (twice in verse).
