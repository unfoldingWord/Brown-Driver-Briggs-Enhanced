BDB5563 [H5512]

Biblical Hebrew

I. סִין proper name [of a location] Sin, i.e. Pelusium, east frontier city of Egypt (Egyptian 'Imt = clay, of which סין is translated (compare Aramaic סְיָן clay), according to SteindBAS i. 599, who compare IIηλούσιον(πηλός = dirt, mud), compare BrugschDict. Géogr. 1081 ff.); — Ezek 30:15 ( 𝔊 Σαιν accusative), + Ezek 30:16, but here Co Toy read plausibly סון, i.e. סְוָן = Syene (see [סְוָן], סְוֵנֵה), 𝔊 συηνη. — On Pelusium see BdEgypt.4 (1898), 169.
