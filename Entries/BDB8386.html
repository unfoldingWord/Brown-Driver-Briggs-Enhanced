Biblical Hebrew

שְׁאֵר noun masculine Ps 73:26 flesh (perhaps originally the inner flesh, full of blood, next the bones, compare HoffmZAW iii (1883), 107, and בָּשָׂר = (originally) flesh next the. skin); — ׳ שׁ absolute Ps 78:20; Ps 78:27, construct Mic 3:3 +, suffix שְׁאֵרִי Jer 51:35; Ps 73:26, etc; —

1. flesh :
a. as food, Exod 21:10 (HPS also 1Sam 9:24, for הַנִּשְׁאָר), Ps 78:20 (|| לֶחֶם), Ps 78:27 (|| עוֺף כָּנָף); figurative Mic 3:2 (|| עוֺר), Mic 3:3 (|| id.), Jer 51:35 (|| דָּם),
b. figurative for physical power Ps 73:26 (+ לֵבָב), Prov 5:11 (+ בָּשָׂר ).
2. in HP = flesh- (= blood-) relation (i.e. one near of kin):שְׁאֵר אָבִיךָ Lev 18:12 compare Lev 18:13 ; Lev 20:19 , also Lev 18:17 (read שְׁאֵרְךָ for שַׁאֲרָה 𝔊 Ew Dr-Wh; compare of. Di Baen); + אֵלָיו הַקָּרֹב Lev 21:2; Num 27:11 בְשָׂרוֺ ׳ שׁ Lev 18:6; Lev 25:49.
3. = self, Prov 11:17 (|| נֶפֶשׁ),
