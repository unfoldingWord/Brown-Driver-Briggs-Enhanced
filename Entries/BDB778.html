Biblical Hebrew

אַרְפַּד proper name [of a location] city in northern Syria (Assyrian Arpadda DlPa 275) — ׳ א Isa 10:9 elsewhere אַרְפָּ֑ד; approximately 15 miles north of Aleppo, modern Tel Erfâd; in OT only as conquered by Assyria, always named with Hamath, etc. 2Kgs 18:34; 2Kgs 19:13 = Isa 36:19; Isa 37:13 (on 𝔊 Ῥαφέθ = Ἀρφάθ compare LagBN 78), also Isa 10:9; Jer 49:23 (compare NöZMG 1871, 258 Kiepib. 655.)
