BDB1784 [H1651]

Biblical Hebrew

גְּשׁוּרִי adjective [of a people] = substantive ׳ הַגּ;

1. people dwelling in Geshûr (above) Deut 3:14; Josh 12:5; Josh 13:11; Josh 13:13.
2. a tribe of, or near, the Philistines Josh 13:2 read probably הֵגִּוְרִי We and Dr (on 1Sam 27:8), MeyZAW i. 126 n GFM Judg 1:29 n. , also 1Sam 27:8 , but deleted 𝔊 (not 𝔊 L ) compare We Dr; read possibly אשׁורי HomAufsätze i. (1892), 9; 2Sam 2:9 read ׳ הגּ for האשׁורי.
