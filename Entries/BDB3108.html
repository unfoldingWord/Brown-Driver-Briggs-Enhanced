BDB3108 [H2740]

Biblical Hebrew

חָרוֺן noun masculine (burning of) anger (compare LagBN 204 Anm. * *) — ׳ ח Ezek 7:12 + 2 t.; construct חֲרוֺן Num 25:4 + 33 t.; suffix חֲרוֺנִי Ezek 7:14 ( Co חרון), חֲרוֺגוֺ Ps 2:5 חֲרֹנְךָ Exod 15:7 plural suffix חֲרוֺנֶי֑ךָ Ps 88:17 — always of God's anger (see note, below), חֲרוֺן אף Exod 32:12; Num 25:4; Num 32:14; Josh 7:26 ( J ), Deut 13:18; 1Sam 28:18; 2Kgs 23:26; 2Chr 28:11; 2Chr 28:13; 2Chr 29:10; 2Chr 30:8; Ezra 10:14; Hos 11:9; Nah 1:6; Zeph 2:2; Zeph 3:8; Isa 13:9; Isa 13:13; Jer 4:8; Jer 4:26; Jer 12:13; Jer 25:37; Jer 25:38; Jer 30:24; Jer 49:37; Jer 51:45; Jonah 3:9; Ps 69:25; Ps 78:49; Ps 85:4; Job 20:23; Lam 1:12; Lam 4:11 אף omitted, Exod 15:7 (song), Neh 13:18; Ps 2:5; Ezek 7:12 (strike out Co), Ezek 7:14 plural bursts of burning anger Ps 88:17. —

Note: כְּמוֺחָֿרוֺן Ps 58:10 burning anger 𝔊 ᵑ9 Jerome AV Bae Che; but Thes MV SS DeW Ew Ol Pe, something burning, e.g. thorns, antith. green thorns; Hengst De cooked flesh, antith. raw; Jer 25:38 הַיּוֺנָה ׳ מפני ח because of the fierceness of the oppressor (? AV ) would then be the only use of the word of other than God's anger; here 𝔊 ᵑ7 Ew Hi Gf Ke Che Gie RV rightly read חרב oppressing sword (as Jer 46:16; Jer 50:16). חרון is not used in H P D2 or E (except song, Exod 15:7) of the Hexateuch; nor in Ezekiel except Ezek 7:12 (strike out Co ), Ezek 7:14.
