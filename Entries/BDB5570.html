BDB5570 [H5518]

Biblical Hebrew

I. סִיר noun masculine Jer 1:13 and feminine 2Kgs 4:38 pot (probably foreign word (Egyptian ?), Arabic a large water-jar Lane1276 is perhaps loan-word ; on Greek derivatives σιρός, σίρινος see LewyFremdw. 107); — ׳ ס absolute 2Kgs 4:38 +, construct Exod 16:3 +; plural סִירֹת Exod 38:3, סִירוֺת 1Kgs 7:45 +; suffix סִירֹתָיו Exod 27:3, סִירֹתֵיכֶם Ps 58:10 — pot:

1.
a. household utensil for boiling 2Kgs 4:38; 2Kgs 4:39; 2Kgs 4:40; 2Kgs 4:41 (twice in verse) הַבָּשָׂר ׳ ס Exod 16:3 ( P ), Zech 14:21 נָפוּחַ ׳ ס Jer 1:13 (compare נפח in vision), symbolic of Jerusalem Ezek 11:3; Ezek 11:7; Ezek 11:11; Ezek 24:3; Ezek 24:6 figurative also Ps 58:10 (compare Che); in simile Mic 3:3; Job 41:23; Eccl 7:6.
b. רַחְצִי ׳ ס Ps 60:10 my washing-pot, Ps 108:10.
2. pots used in sanctuary, Ezek 27:3; Ezek 38:3 (both P ), 1Kgs 7:40 (read סִירוֺת for ᵑ0 כִּיִּרוֺת, so Manuscripts of HCT 𝔊 ᵑ9 Th Ke Klo Kmp Benz), 1Kgs 7:45 = 2Chr 4:11; 2Chr 4:16; 2Kgs 25:14 = Jer 52:18 , also Jer 52:19; 2Chr 35:13 ; Zech 14:20. — Here belongs probably סִרָה in בּוֺר הַסִּרָה.
