BDB5146 [H5180]

Biblical Hebrew

נְחֻשְׁתָּן proper name, feminine given to bronze serpent 2Kgs 18:4 probably = bronze-god (so Thes, see also BauSemitic Rel. i. 288; compare נְחַשׁ נְחשֶׁת v 2Kgs 18:4; Num 21:9 (twice in verse) ); — chief arg. ag. this are vocalization of 𝔊 Νεσθαλει, A 𝔊 L Νε(ε)σθαν, and comparative unimportance of material of which image was made; others think of נָחָשׁ serpent (NöZMG xlii (1888), 482 תן + נחשׁ, Kloon the passage יתן + נחשׁ); LagBN 188, 205 thinks loan-word
