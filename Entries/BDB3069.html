חָקָה verb only Pu`al cut in, carve (Late Hebrew represent, imitate; || חקק) —

Pu`al Participle מְחֻקֶּה 1Kgs 6:35; Ezek 8:10 + Ezek 23:14 ( Co מְחֻקִּים, after 𝔊 ᵑ6 ᵑ7 ᵑ9 ), carved figure on wall Ezek 8:10; Ezek 23:14 (where read אנשׁים מחקים, see above) = substantive carved work 1Kgs 6:35 (on doors of temple).
Hithpa`el Imperfect 2 masculine singular עלשָֿׁרְשֵׁי רַגְלַי תִּתְחַקֶּה Job 13:27 thou gravest thee a graving (= markest a line) for (i.e. about) the soles of my feet, fixest limits for them (see Di).
