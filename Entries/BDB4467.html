BDB4467 [H3955]

Biblical Hebrew

[לָשָׁד] noun masculine juice, juicy or dainty bit, dainty; — construct כְּטַעַן לְשַׁד הַשָּׁ֑מֶן Num 11:8 ( J ) simile of taste of manna ( 𝔊 ἐγκρίς, ᵑ9 panis oleatus), i.e. a dainty prepared with oil (compare Di ); לְשַׁדִּי בְּחַרְבֹנֵי קַיִץ נֶהְפַּךְ Ps 32:4 my juice (sap, life-moisture) is transformed, etc.
