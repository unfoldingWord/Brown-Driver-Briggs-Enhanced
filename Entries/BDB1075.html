BDB1075 [H1039]

Biblical Hebrew

בֵּית נִמְרָה proper name [of a location] east of Jordan, in Gad (place of leopard) Num 32:36 = Josh 13:27 𝔊 Ναμραμ, Βαιθαναβρα, etc., see also LagOnom. Sacr. 232, 2nd ed. 246; — modern Tel Nimrîn SurveyEP i. 337 BdPal 179, compare also מֵי נִמְרִים Isa 15:6, & RobBR i. 551.
