BDB1101 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1103

בֹּכִים proper name, of a location near Bethel, אלהַֿבֹּכִים Judg 2:1, בֹּכִים vJudg 2:5 (compare vJudg 2:4); ᵐ5 Judg 2:1 τὸν Κλαυθμῶνα καὶ ἐπὶ Βαιθηλ; vJudg 2:5 Κλαυθμῶνες i.e. בְּכָּ יִם (ᵐ5L Κλαυθμών); — on possible connection with אַלּוֺן בָּכוּת Gen 35:8 compare Stu & WeBleek's Einl. ed. 4, 183, compare Hexateuch 1889, 215; but perhaps read ביתאל instead of בכים in vGen 35:1, compare We BuRS20.
