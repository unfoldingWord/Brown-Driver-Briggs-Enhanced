BDB1102 [H1066]

Biblical Hebrew

בֹּכִים proper name [of a location] near Bethel, אלהַֿבֹּכִים Judg 2:1, בֹּכִים Judg 2:5 (compare Judg 2:4); 𝔊 Judg 2:1 τὸν Κλαυθμῶνα καὶ ἐπὶ Βαιθηλ; Judg 2:5 Κλαυθμῶνες i.e. בְּכָּ יִם ( 𝔊 L Κλαυθμών); — on possible connection with אַלּוֺן בָּכוּת Gen 35:8 compare Stu & WeBleek's Einl. ed. 4, 183, compare Hexateuch 1889, 215; but perhaps read ביתאל instead of בכים in Gen 35:1 , compare We BuRS20.
