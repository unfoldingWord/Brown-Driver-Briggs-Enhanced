[אֻרְיָה] noun feminine manger, crib (Aramaic אוּדְיָא, , Arabic stall, etc., Vulgar Arabic = manager Mohit19. 15 (GFM, privately), compare Lane51c; Assyrian urû DlBAS i. 211) — Plural אֻרָוֺת 2Chr 32:28 (Aramaic form for אֳרָוֺת; compare LagBN 172); construct אֻרְוֺת 1Kgs 5:6, ֻארְיוֺת 2Chr 9:25 crib of horses 1Kgs 5:6 (app. in enumeration horses themselves, English head , or span, compare also 2Chr 9:25 with 1Kgs 10:26 So Th MV, but text here dubious. compare Klo ), hence 2Chr 9:25 of any animals (כלבֿהמה) 2Chr 32:28 (on ׳ לַאֲ see Add.).

Also plural אֲוֵרוֺת 2Chr 32:28b (read probably לָעֲדָרִים ᵐ5 ᵑ9 Kit Benz and others).
