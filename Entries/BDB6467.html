Biblical Hebrew

I. [עָשַׁת] verb smooth or shiny (?), si vera lectio (compare עֶשֶׁת); — only

Qal Perfect 3 plural שָֽׁמְנוּ עָֽשְׁתוּ Jer 5:28 (figurative) they have grown fat, they are smooth (or shine, from fat); doubtful word, Thes conjecture עָֽשְׁרוּ have grown rich (compare 𝔊 ᵑ6 ᵑ7 ), so Gie (but וַיַּעֲשִׁירוּ precedes, Jer 5:27).
