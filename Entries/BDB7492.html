Biblical Hebrew

קֵן noun masculine Deut 22:6 nest; — absolute ׳ ק Isa 10:14 +; construct קַןֿ Deut 22:6 suffix קִנּוֺ Deut 32:11+, etc.; plural קִנִּים Gen 6:14 —

1. nest, of bird Deut 22:6; Job 39:27 in sim Isa 10:14; Prov 27:8, of nestlings Deut 32:11; Isa 16:2 ( קֵן מְשֻׁלָּח֑), perhaps also עִםקִֿנִּי Job 29:18, but difficult, CheJQ July, 1897, 578 proposes זָקֵן or בִּוִקֻנַי (compare 𝔊 ); nest on high, of rock-dwellings Num 24:21 ( J E ) Jer 49:16; Obad 1:4 figurative of Chaldeans' secure abode Hab 2:9 temple as secure home for Israel Ps 84:4 (figurative of swallow).
2. cells, like nests, in Noah's ark Gen 6:14 ( P ; read probably קִנִּים קִנִּים, so Lag Ol BuUrg.255 Di Holz Gunk).
