BDB6224 [H6048]

Biblical Hebrew

עֲנַמֶּ֫לֶךְ proper name [of a divinity] of סְפַרְוַיִם (q. v.) 2Kgs 17:31, Ανημελεχ (omitted by 𝔊 L ); = Assyrian Anu-malik according to SchrCOT on the passage, but dubious, see Kiton the passage and references; CheExpos. Times, June, 1898, 429 reads ענומלך, and insert also 2Kgs 19:37 (before אלהיו); compare also Hal cited below עֲנָת.
