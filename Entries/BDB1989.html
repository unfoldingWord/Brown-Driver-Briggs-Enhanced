Biblical Hebrew

[דָּפַק] verb beat, knock (Arabic pour out, also drive (beasts)) —

Qal Perf. 3 masculine plural suffix וּדְפָקוּם consecutive Gen 33:13, but read 1 singular suffix וּדְפַקְתִּים 𝔊 ᵑ6 Sam Di beat (in driving, drive severely or cruelly); Participle דּוֺפֵק Song 5:2 absolute knocking (at door).
Hithpa`el Participle מִתְדַּפְּקִים עַלהַֿדָּ֑לֶת Judg 19:22 beat violently (beat themselves tired) against the door.
