BDB5996 [H5850]

Biblical Hebrew

I. עֲטָרָה noun feminine crown, wreath; — absolute ׳ ע Ezek 21:31; Song 3:11 construct עֲטֶרֶת 2Sam 12:30 + 17 t.; plural עֲטָרוֺת Zech 6:11; Job 31:36, עֲטָוֺת Zech 6:14 — crown:

1. of idol-image (Milcom, 𝔊 We Klo Dr Kit Löhr HPS) 2Sam 12:30 = 1Chr 20:2 golden, פָּז ׳ ע Ps 21:4 ׳ ע תִּפְאַרְתְּכֶם of king and queen, Jer 13:18 the crown of your splendour; compare Ezek 21:31 wedding-crown Song 3:11.
2. זָהָב ׳ ע Esth 8:15 (of Mordecai); of silver and gold Zech 6:11; Zech 6:14 תִּפְאֶרֶת ׳ ע crown of splendour, on personified Jerusalem Ezek 16:12 (in allegory), Jerusalem and Samaria Ezek 23:42 ׳ ע as appositive ( = simile) Job 31:36.
3. figurative of honour, etc., Job 19:9 (|| כְּבוֺדִי Lam 5:16 בַּעְלָהּ ׳ אַשֶׁת חַיִל ע Prov 12:4, compare Prov 14:24 ; Prov 17:6 תִּפְאֶרֶת ׳ ע Prov 16:31 (of שֵׂיבָה); gift of wisdom Prov 4:9 figurative of Jerusalem Isa 62:3 (|| צְנִיף מְלוּכָה, compare גֵאוּת ׳ ע Isa 28:1; Isa 28:3 figurative of Samaria; figurative of ׳ י, צְבִי ׳ ע Isa 28:5 (|| צְפִירַת תִּפְאָרָה).
