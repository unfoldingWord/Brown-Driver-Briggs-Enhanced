Biblical Hebrew

זֶ֫רַע noun masculine Deut 28:38 sowing, seed, offspring (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic זְרַע, ; Phoenician זרע; Zinjirli id.; Arabic seed-produce, seed; Ethiopic Assyrian zêru , cultivated land, BelserBAS ii. 130, compare Arabic ) — absolute ׳ ז Gen 47:23 =; זָ֑רַע Gen 1:29 +; construct זֶרַע Isa 5:10 +; זְרַעֿ Num 11:7 suffix זַרְעִי 1Sam 20:42; 1Sam 24:22 זַרְעֲךָ Deut 11:10 +; זַרְעֶ֑ךָ Eccl 11:6 +; etc.; plural suffix זַרְעֵיכֶם 1Sam 8:15 —

1. literal:
a. a sowing הַשָּׂדֶה ׳ לְז Gen 47:24 ( J ; + אָכְלְכֶם; otherwise Lev 27:16 b, see below 2.c.); ׳ לֹא מְקוֺם ז Num 20:5 no place for sowing ( J E ; + וּתְאֵנָה וְגֶפֶן וְרִמּוֺן); compare ׳ שְׂדֵה ז Ezek 17:5 a field suitable for sowing; hence
b. sowing as regularly recurring at its season Gen 8:22 ( J ; || קָצִיר, + קֹר וָחֹם וְקַיִץ וָחֹרֶף וְיוֺם וָלַיְלָה), Lev 26:5 ( H ; || דַּיִשׁ, בָּצִיר; — compare Amos 9:13 below 2 .a.).
2. seed:
a. literally, sown, to raise crops for food, usually corn (wheat, barely, etc.) Gen 47:19 ; Gen 47:23 ( J ; compare Gen 47:14f.), Num 24:7 ( J E ); נָתַן לַֹזּרֵעַ ׳ ז Isa 55:10 (|| לֶחֶם), Deut 28:38 (רַב ׳ ז), Lev 26:16 ( H ; with זָרַע), Lev 27:16 a, חֹמֶר שְׂעֹרִים ׳ ז Lev 27:16b (both P ), compare Isa 5:10.
b. figurative, of idolatry of Judah Isa 17:11 (|| קציר ); of fortunes of Zion ׳ מֶשֶׁךְ הַזּ Ps 126:6 the dropping of seed, i.e. the proper quantity for sowing (opposed to אֲלֻמֹּת ); Jer 35:7 (with זָרַע, || כֶּרֶם לֹא תִטָּ֔עוּ), compare Jer 35:9 (|| כרם , שׂדה); ׳ מְטַר ז Isa 30:23 (with זָרַע, || לֶחֶם תְּבוּאַתּ הָאֲדָמָה); also Deut 11:10 (with זָרַע), Eccl 11:6 (c. id. ); זֵרוּעַ ׳ ז Lev 11:37 , compare Lev 11:38 (both P ); as yielding the crop תְּבוּאַת זַרְעֶ֑ךָ Deut 14:22 the product of thy seed, compare d. below; ׳ וְנִגַּשׁ תוֺרֵשׁ בַּקּוֺצֵר וְדֹרֵךְ עֲנָבִים בְּמשֵׁךְ הַזּ Amos 9:13 , in figure of rapid and plentiful growth in the coming time; הָאָרֶץ ׳ ז producing a vine Ezek 17:5, of growth and prosperity of Israel; ׳ הַזּ Mal 2:3, read perhaps הַזְּרֹעַ (compare VB ).
c. seed as product; the manna is compare with גַּד ׳ ז, coriander seed Num 11:7 ( J E ), Exod 16:31 ( P ); seed of herbs and trees Gen 1:11 (twice in verse); Gen 1:12 (twice in verse); Gen 1:29 (twice in verse) (all P ). Especially
d. seed as corn-product, corn-crop, so (although not quite clearly) Deut 22:9 (with זָרַע; || תְּבוּאַת הַכָּ֑רֶם); plural 1Sam 8:15 (perhaps better arable lands, compare above As zêru ; || כרמים), הָאָרֶץ ׳ ז Lev 27:30 (|| פְּרִיעֵֿץ; otherwise Gen 47:24, compare above 1.a.); שִׁחֹר ׳ ז Isa 23:3 (|| קְצִיר יְאוֺר); בַּמְּגוּרָה ׳ הַעוֺד הַזּ Hag 2:19 (|| גֶּפֶן, תְּאֵנָה, רִמּוֺן , עֵץ הַזַּיִת); יָשִׁו֯ב זַרְעֶ֑ךָ וְגָרְנְךָ יֶאֱסֹף Job 39:12 (of harvest); so perhaps ׳ כְּבֵית סָאתַיִם ז 1Kgs 18:32 .
e. זֶרַע הַשָּׁלוֺם הַגֶּפֶן Zech 8:12, read perhaps שׁלוֺם ׳ הַזּ the crop is security (= secure), so ᵑ6 Or and others; > Ew Hi and others who translate ׳ הַשּׁ ׳ ז the growth of peace, making הַגֶּפֶּן appositive — Verbs used with ׳ ז in these senses are: — usually זַרַע Gen 1:11; Lev 26:16; Jer 35:7 +; הִפְרִיחַ Isa 17:11 נָתַן בְּ = plant, sow Ezek 17:5 נָתַן yield Isa 55:10 מָשַׁךְ Amos 9:13.
3. seed = semen virile, וְנִזְרְעָה זָ֑רַע Num 5:28, and she shall be made pregnant with seed; usually ׳ שִׁכְּבַתזֿ, flow of semen Lev 22:4 ( H ), Lev 15:16; Lev 15:32 ( P ; all with תֵּצֵא), Lev 15:17 ( P ; with הָיָה); as accusative modi שׁכב ׳ ז ׳ אתאִֿשָּׁה שׁ Lev 19:20 ( H ), Lev 15:18; Num 5:13 (both P ); so also נתן שְׁכָבְתּוֺ לְזרע Lev 18:20 ( לְ genitives); compare בְּהֵמָה ׳ אָדָם וז ׳ ז Jer 31:27 (as 2nd accusative after זָרַע q. v. 3.b.).
4. seed = offspring:
a. rarely of animals, collective Gen 3:15; Gen 7:3 (both J ). Usu.
b. of mankind, coll. = descendants, posterity; seed of the woman Gen 3:15 ( J ); seed of the patriarchs (especially Abraham) Gen 12:17; Gen 13:15; Gen 13:16 (twice in verse); Gen 16:10 ; Gen 22:17 (twice in verse); Gen 22:18; Gen 24:7; Gen 26:3; Gen 26:4 (3 t. in verse); Gen 26:24; Gen 28:13; Gen 28:14 (twice in verse); Gen 32:13 (all J ), Gen 15:13; Gen 15:18; Exod 32:13 (twice in verse); Exod 33:1 (all J E ), Gen 21:12; Josh 24:3 (both E ; in both promised seed of Abraham restricted to line of Isaac), Deut 11:9; Deut 34:4; Neh 9:8 = a son as involving posterity Gen 15:3; Gen 15:5 ( J E ) compare below; phrase זַרְעָם אַחֲרֵיהֶם Deut 1:8, compare Deut 4:37; Deut 10:15 , so P , Gen 17:7 (twice in verse); Gen 17:8; Gen 17:9; Gen 17:10; Gen 17:19; Gen 35:12; Gen 48:4 זִרְעֲךָ אִתָּךְ Gen 28:4 ( P ; compare Num 18:19); see also below c.
c. seed (= posterity) of other individuals Gen 21:13 ( E ; of Ishmael) Gen 24:60; Gen 48:19 (both J ), Num 14:24 ( J E ), Lev 21:21; Lev 22:3; Lev 22:4 (all H ), Num 17:5 ( P ), 1Sam 20:42 (twice in verse); 1Sam 24:22; 2Sam 4:8; 1Kgs 2:33 (twice in verse); 1Kgs 11:39; 2Kgs 5:27; Jer 29:32 ; Ezek 43:19 ; Job 5:25 (|| צֶאֱצָאֶיךָ), Job 21:8 (|| id.); especially of seed of David as anointed to reign Ps 18:51 = 2Sam 22:51 (|| לִמְשִׁיחוֺ), as sitting on throne Jer 33:21 , compare Jer 33:22 Jer 33:26, Ps 89:5; Ps 89:30; Ps 89:37 of Jehoiachin Jer 22:28 ; Jer 22:30, Jehoiakim Jer 36:31 (compare Jer 36:30); of child (son) as involving posterity Gen 38:8; Gen 38:9 (twice in verse) (of Er's seed by Onam); Gen 48:11 (all J ), compare Gen 19:32; Gen 19:31 ( J ), 2Sam 7:12; Ruth 4:12; Lev 21:15 ( H ); phrase זַרְעָם אַחֲרֵיהֶם of seed of Noah and his sons Gen 9:9 of Aaron Exod 28:43 of Phinehas Num 25:13 (all P ); of David 1Chr 17:11 לְזַרְעוֺ לְדֹרֹתָם Exod 30:21 ( P ), compare Lev 21:17 ( H ; both of Aaron), see also b. above ׳ ז, of seed of righteous, especially Psalms: — Ps 25:13; Ps 37:25 ; Ps 37:26 ; Ps 69:37 ; Ps 102:29 (|| בָּנִים), Ps 112:2 (|| דּוֺר ישׁרים), compare Ps 22:31 (on text see Che); of wicked Ps 21:11; Ps 37:28.
d. specifically of a particular child (son) אַחֵר ׳ ז Gen 4:25 ( J ); אֲנָשִׁים ׳ ז 1Sam 1:11 of children Lev 18:21 ; Lev 20:2 ; Lev 20:34 ; Lev 22:13 (all H ), 1Sam 2:20 (compare בָּנִים, בָּנוֺת 1Sam 2:21); children and grandchildren Gen 46:6; Gen 46:7 ( P ).
e. family Gen 17:12 ( P ; compare Assyrian zêr bit abišu AsrbAnnals iii. 10); note especially of royal family הַמֶּלֶךְ ׳ ז 1Kgs 11:14 הַמַּמְלָכָה ׳ ז 2Kgs 11:1 = 2Chr 22:10 הַמְּלוּכָה ׳ ז 2Kgs 25:25 = Jer 41:1; Ezek 17:13; Dan 1:3 = pedigree בית אבותם וְזַרְעָם Ezra 2:59 = Neh 7:61 = one's nation, as of same blood Esth 10:3 (|| עַם). — Some verbs used with ׳ ז in these senses are: — נָתַן, God subject Gen 15:3; Ruth 4:12 (followed by מִן of woman); שִׂים 1Sam 2:20 with man as subject נתן Gen 38:9 הֵקִים Gen 38:8 ׳ חִיָּה ז, keep alive, or give life to, of animals Gen 7:3 of woman Gen 19:32; Gen 19:34 (followed by מִן of man).
f. especially as name for people of Israel, seed of Abraham Isa 41:8 ; 2Chr 20:7 (|| עַמְּךָ יִשְׂרָאֵל), Jer 33:26; Ps 105:6 (|| בְּנֵי יעקִב ); of Israel or Jacob 2Kgs 17:20; Neh 9:2 (|| בני ישׂראל Neh 9:1), Isa 43:5; Isa 45:19 ; Isa 45:25 ; Jer 31:36; Jer 31:37; Jer 33:26; Ps 22:24 (twice in verse); 1Chr 16:13 (|| בני יעקב); so בֵּית ישׂראל ׳ ז Jer 23:8 ; Ezek 44:22 ( ᵑ6 𝔊 Manuscripts Co strike out בית); בית ׳ ז יעקב Ezek 20:5 in Esther. הַיְהוּדִים ׳ ז Esth 6:13 so of Edom (seed of Esau) Jer 49:10, compare מִוֶּרע מָדַי Dan 9:1.
g. seed of Israel (or Jacob, or the people), is used also, by (later) prophets, of future generations, in addressing the people: — Deut 28:46; Deut 28:59 ; Deut 30:6; Deut 30:19; Deut 31:21, compare Num 18:19 ( P ; לְזַרְעֲךָ אִתָּ֑ךְ, || וּלְבָנֶיךָ וְלִבְנוֺתֶיךָ), Jer 30:10 = Jer 46:27 (Jacob and Israel here = people), Ps 106:27 || צֶאֱצָא(יך ׅ Isa 44:3 (Jacob and Jeshurun), Isa 48:19, compare Isa 61:9 also Jer 7:15; Isa 54:3 ; Isa 66:22 (|| שִׁמְכֶם); מִפִּי זַרְעֲךָ וּמִפִּי זֶרַע זַרְעֲךָ Isa 59:21 of Jews (יהודים) Esth 9:27; Esth 9:28; Esth 9:31.
5. seed as marked by moral quality = persons (or community) of such a quality; transition to this through such cases as those of ׳ בְּרוּכֵי י ׳ ז Isa 65:23 (|| צֶאֱצָאִים), compare Isa 61:9; Isa 65:9 הַקֹּדֶשׁ ׳ ז Ezra 9:2 (of Israel), compare Isa 53:10 note, in good sense צַדִּיקִים ׳ ז Prov 11:21 (opposed to רַע); אֱמֶת ׳ ז Jer 2:21 (|| שׂוֺרֵק); אלהים ׳ ז Mal 2:15 — קֹדֶשׁ ׳ ז Isa 6:13 is a gloss; — in bad sense מְרֵעִים ׳ ז Isa 1:4 community of evil-doers (|| גּוֺיחֹֿטֵא, עַם כֶּבֶד עָוֺן , בָּנִים מַשְׁחִיתִים), Isa 14:20 מְנָאֵף ׳ ז Isa 57:3 (|| בני עֹנֲנָה); שָֿׁ֑קֶר ׳ ז Isa 57:4 (|| יִלְדֵיפֶֿשַׁע).
