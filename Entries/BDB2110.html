Biblical Hebrew

יְהוֺיָרִיב, יוֺיָרִיב proper name, masculine 𝔊 Ἰωιαρειβ, Ἰωαρειβ, Ἰωαρειμ, etc. (׳ י pleadeth, or contendeth) —

1. name of a priestly family ׳ יְהוֺ 1Chr 9:10; 1Chr 24:7 = ׳ יוֺ Neh 11:10; Neh 12:6; Neh 12:19.
2. teacher in Ezra's time Ezra 8:16.
3. a Judaite Neh 11:5.
