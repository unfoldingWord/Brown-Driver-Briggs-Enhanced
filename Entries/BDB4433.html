1. son of מְתוּשָׁאֵל (Cainite) Gen 4:18; Gen 4:19; Gen 4:23 (twice in verse); Gen 4:24 (all J ).
2. son of מְתוּשֶׁלַח (Sethite) Gen 5:25; Gen 5:26; Gen 5:28; Gen 5:30; Gen 5:31 (all P ), 1Chr 1:3. — 𝔊 Ααμεχ.
