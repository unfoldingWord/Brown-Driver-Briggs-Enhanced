Biblical Hebrew

I. גָּדִי adjective [of a people] of גָּד 1, Gadite 2Sam 23:36 (so perhaps also || 1Chr 11:38 where ᵑ0 הַגְרִי q. v.; compare Th Be Dr); elsewhere as proper name collective: so 1Chr 5:18 (only here without article, read perhaps גָּד so 𝔊 ), Deut 3:12 ; Deut 3:16; Deut 4:43; Deut 29:7; Josh 1:12; Josh 12:6; Josh 13:8; Josh 22:1; 2Kgs 10:33; 1Chr 5:26; 1Chr 12:8; 1Chr 12:37; 1Chr 26:32.
