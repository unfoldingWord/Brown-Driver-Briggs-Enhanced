Biblical Hebrew

מִיכָה proper name, masculine

1. Micha, the Ephraimite ( 𝔊 Μειχαίας), Judg 17:5; Judg 17:8; Judg 17:9 ; Judg 17:10 ; Judg 17:12 (twice in verse); Judg 17:13; Judg 18:2 ff. (12 t.), called מִיכָֽיְהוּ Judg 17:1; Judg 17:4.
2. the prophet Micah ( 𝔊 Μειχαίας), Mic 1:1 (Jer 26:18 Kt מִיכָיָה).
3. 1Chr 5:5.
4. son of Meribbaal (Mephibosheth) 1Chr 8:34f. ( 𝔊 Μιχια), 1Chr 9:40f. ( 𝔊 Μειχα), called מִיכָא 2Sam 9:12.
5. 2Sam 23:20 ( 𝔊 Μειχας), 2Sam 24:24; 2Sam 24:25 ( 𝔊 Μειχα).
6. 2Chr 18:14 ( = מִיכָֽיְהוּ 2).
7. 2Chr 34:20 (|| 2Kgs 22:12 מִיכָיָה).
