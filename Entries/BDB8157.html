BDB8157 [H7663]

Biblical Hebrew

I. [שָׂבַר] verb inspect, examine (van d. H. שֹׁבֵר [so 𝔊 συντρίβων], but Masoretic ׳ שׂ see Norzi; hence connection with Arabic probe a wound, try, examine, improbable, and this (according to Frä261) denominative from Aramaic loan-word; improbable also is connection with Aramaic סְבַר think (compare following), KauAramaic in AT, 85 ); —

Qal Participle שׂבֵר, with ב object, Neh 2:13; Neh 2:15 I examined into the wall, inspected it closely.
