BDB3226 [H2831]

Biblical Hebrew

[חַשְׁמַן] noun masculine only plural חַשְׁמַגִּים Ps 68:32 meaning unknown, 𝔊 ᵑ6 ᵑ9 ambassadors, Rabb nobles, conjectures from context; doubtless text error; NesJBL 1891, 152 proposes בשׁמנים they shall come with oils, ointments, Hilg Che (after Aq Jerome) חָשִׁם hasting.
