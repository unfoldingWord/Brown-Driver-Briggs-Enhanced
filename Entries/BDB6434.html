מַעֲשֶׂה233 noun masculine Gen 20:9 deed, work; — absolute ׳ מ Gen 44:15 +, construct מַעֲשֵׂה Isa 5:12 +; suffix מַעֲשֵׂהוּ Judg 13:12 +, etc.; plural מַעֲשִׂים Gen 20:9 +; construct מַעֲשֵׂי Jer 1:16 +; suffix מַעֲשָׂיו 1Sam 19:4 (or singular, so Dr) +, etc.; — often accusative of cognate meaning with verb with עשׂה; —

1.
a. b. (1) work, labour, husbandry Exod 23:16 ( E ), Gen 5:29 ( J ), Judg 19:16, יְדֵיכֶם ׳ מ Hag 2:17 enforced labour of Israel in Egypt Exod 5:5; Exod 5:13 ( J ); in general Exod 23:12 ( E ); also of product of labour Exod 23:16, compare Isa 65:22; Eccl 5:5 ׳ שֵׁשֶׁת יְמֵי הַמּ Ezek 46:1 the six working-days; of religious work, ׳ עֲבֹדַת בֵּית הָא ׳ מ 1Chr 23:28 , compare ׳ מ 2Chr 31:21 (2) business, pursuit, Gen 46:33; Gen 47:3 ( J ), Judg 13:12; 1Sam 25:2; Isa 54:16 in general, occupation Isa 29:15; Eccl 2:4; Eccl 2:11; Eccl 3:17; Eccl 3:22; Eccl 8:9; Eccl 9:7; Eccl 9:10 (3) undertaking, enterprise Isa 19:14; Isa 19:15; Deut 15:10 יָדֶ֑ךָ ׳ מ Deut 2:7 + 5 t. c. deed(s), work(s) of ׳ י, especially in deliverance and in judgment, Judg 2:7; Judg 2:10; Josh 24:31 ( D ), Ps 33:4 + 21 t., + יָדָיו ׳ מ etc. Isa 5:12; Ps 28:5; Ps 92:5 + 4 t.; הָאֱלֹהִים ׳ מ in general Eccl 7:13; Eccl 8:17; Eccl 11:5 compare Eccl 3:11.
