Biblical Hebrew

שֻׁלְחָן71 noun masculine Exod 25:23 table (on form of word compare GFMl.c.; properly (see above) skin or leather mat spread on ground, see also, on early Semitic 'table,' Thes1417 WeSkizzen iv. 157 RSSemitic i. 184; 2nd ed. 201 KennedyEncy. Bib iii. 2991); — absolute ׳ שׁ 1Sam 20:34 +; construct שֻׁלְחַן 1Kgs 5:7 +; suffix שֻׁלְחָנִי Judg 1:7 +, etc.; plural שֻׁלְחָנוֺת Isa 28:8 +, construct שֻׁלְחֲנוֺת 1Chr 28:16 (twice in verse); —

1. table for king's repast Judg 1:7 (with תַּחַת), 1Sam 20:29; 1Sam 20:34; 2Sam 9:11 (read דָּוִד ׳ שׁ 𝔊 Th We Dr and modern), 2Sam 9:13; 2Sam 19:29; 1Kgs 2:7; 1Kgs 5:1; 1Kgs 18:19; 1Kgs 10:5 = 2Chr 9:4, compare Isa 21:5 (with עָרַךְ (q. v. 1.c.) arrange); with עַל 2Sam 9:7; 2Sam 9:10 ; 2Sam 9:11; Dan 11:27 for governor Neh 5:17 (with עַל).
2. for private use 1Kgs 13:20 (with אֶל), Job 36:16; Ps 23:5 (figurative, with עָרַךְ), compare Ps 78:19 (id.), Ps 128:3, also (in bedroom) 2Kgs 4:10 of revellers Isa 28:8, compare Ps 69:23 (figurative), of wisdom (figurative) Prov 9:2 .
3. especially Ezekiel, P for sacred uses: in tabernacle Exod 25:23 + 17 t. 25-40, Num 3:31, שֻׁלְחַן הַפָּנִים Num 4:7 (compare 2Chr 29:18 below); הַטָּהֹר ׳ הַשּׁ Lev 24:26 (compare 2Chr 13:11 below); in Solomon's temple 1Kgs 7:48; 2Chr 29:18, also (10 in number) 2Chr 4:8; 2Chr 4:19; 2Chr 13:11, compare 1Chr 28:16 (4 t. in verse), also Ezek 23:41 (with עָרַךְ); in Ezekiel's temple Ezek 40:39 (twice in verse) + 6 t. Ezek 40 (8 in number), Ezek 40:42 (of stone, 4 in number); like altar Ezek 41:22, = altar of burnt-offering Ezek 44:16 in second temple, ׳ שֻׁלְחַן י Mal 1:7, אֲדֹנָֻ ׳ שׁ Mal 1:12 for idolatrous meal Isa 65:11 figurative of ׳ י's sacrificial feast Ezek 39:20 (eschatological).
