BDB2124 [H3098]

Biblical Hebrew

יוֺאָח proper name, masculine (׳ י is brother; compare אֲחִיָּהוּ & references) —

1. son of Asaph the chronicler in Hezekiah's time 2Kgs 18:18; 2Kgs 18:26; 2Kgs 18:37 ( 𝔊 Ἰωας, 𝔊 L Ἰωαχ), = Isa 36:3; Isa 36:11; Isa 36:22 ( 𝔊 Ἰωαχ).
2. Levites
a. son of Zimmah 1Chr 6:6
b. id. 2Chr 29:12 (twice in verse) ( 𝔊 Ἰωα, Ἰωαχ(α), Ἰωδααθ).
3. son of Joahaz the chronicles in Josiah's time 2Chr 34:8 ( 𝔊 Ἰωαχ, Ἰωας ).
4. a Levite, son of Obed-edom 1Chr 26:4 ( 𝔊 Ἰωαθ, Ἰωαα, Ἰωαδ).

יוֺאָחָז see יְהוֺאָחָז
