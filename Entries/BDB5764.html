BDB5764 [H3258]

Biblical Hebrew

יַעְבֵּץ proper name

1. masculine [person] Jabez, a man of the Calebites, 1Chr 4:9 (twice in verse); 1Chr 4:10 (where interpreted as akin to עצב; on position of this family compare MeyJudenthum 118); 𝔊 Ιγαβης A Ιαγβης, Γαβης, GL Ιαβιης, Ιαβηλ, Ιαβεις.
2. location in Judah, apparently near Bethlehem 1Chr 2:55 (compare 1Chr 2:54 ); 𝔊 Γαμες A Γαβης 𝔊 L Ιαβις.
