BDB3258 [H2860]

Biblical Hebrew

חָתָן noun masculine daughter's husband, bride-groom (as one who undergoes circumcision, see above; Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic חַתְנָא, and (also sister's husband, etc.); vulgar Arabic daughter's husband, then more generally wife's and husband's relations; Sabean (Lihyân) חֿתן daughter's husband DHMEpigr. Denkm. 87; Assyrian —atanu, daughter's husband COTGloss) — absolute ׳ ח 1Sam 18:18 + 10 t. + Gen 19:12 (where read חֲתָנֶיךָ ᵐ5 Ol Di); construct חֲתַן Exod 4:23 + 4 t.; suffix חֲתָנוֺ Judg 19:5 חֲתָנָיו Gen 19:14 (twice in verse); —

1. in relation to a father, daughter's husband, or bridegroom Gen 19:12; Gen 19:14 ( לֹקְחֵי בְּנֹתָיו ׳ ח), Gen 19:14 (all J ), Judg 15:6 ; Judg 19:5; 1Sam 18:18; 1Sam 22:14; Neh 6:18; Neh 13:28 see also חֲתַן בֵּית אַחְאָב 2Kgs 8:27 son-in-law of the house of Ahad, said of Ahaziah, whose mother Athaliah was the daughter of Ahab.
2. in relation to the bride, bridegroom דָּמִים אַתָּה לִי ׳ ח Exod 4:25 a bloody bridegroom art thou to me, compare Exod 4:26 ( J ; on this see WeProl. 1886, 355); || כַּלָּה Jer 7:34; Jer 16:9; Jer 25:10; Jer 33:11; Joel 2:16 in simile of ׳ י's work for and delight in his people Isa 61:10; Isa 62:5 of sun Ps 19:6.
