BDB5367 [H5356]

Biblical Hebrew

נִקָּיוֺן noun [masculine] innocency; — ׳ נ Ps 26:6; Ps 73:13 נִקָּיֹן Hos 8:5 construct נִקְּיוֺן Amos 4:6 נִקְיֹן Gen 20:5 —

1. freedom from guilt, innocency: כַּפַּי ׳ נ Gen 20:5 ( R JE ); דַּפָּ֑י ׳ אֶרְחַץ בנ Ps 26:6 I will wash my hands in innocency = Ps 73:13.
2. freedom from punishment Hos 8:5.
3. in physical sense, נִקְיוֺם שִׁנַּיִם cleanness of teeth (without food) Amos 4:6 (but read perhaps after 𝔊 ᵑ6 ᵑ7 קַהֲיוֺן bluntness, so LagBN 201).
