BDB6399 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6401

[עֲרִיסָה] noun feminine meaning dubious, usually coarse meal; ᵐ5 AV RV dough, so Toy (with ?) (Late Hebrew עַרְסָן barley-meal; Syriac (in Lexicons) hulled barley, or (compare LagGGN 1889, 301 f.) wheat); > SmEz CoEz kneading-trough (so Late Hebrew עֲרִיסָה Jastr; apparently secondary, עֲרִיסָה also [ bed], cradle, see ערשׂ ); — only רֵאשִׁית עֲרִיסֹתֵיכֶם Ezek 44:30, עֲרִיסֹתֵינוּ ׳ ר Neh 10:38 עֲרִסֹתֵיכֶם ׳ ר Num 15:20; Num 15:21 ( P ).

עַרְעָר, עַרְעֹר, עֲרֹעֵר, עֲרוֺעֵר see ערר .
