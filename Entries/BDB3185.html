[מַחֲרֵשָׁה, מַחֲרֶ֫שֶׁת] noun feminine ploughshare — singular suffix לִלְטוֺשׁ אִישׁ מַחֲרַשְׁתּוֺ 1Sam 8:20 to sharpen each man his ploughshare 1Sam 13:20 (+ אֵתוֺ, קַרְדֻּמּוֺ) + מַחֲרֵשָׁתוֺ 1Sam 13:20, read probably דָּרְבָּנוֺ his goad (so 𝔊 ᵑ6 We Dr, see 1Sam 13:21); plural absolute מַחֲרֵשֹׁת 1Sam 13:21 (+ similar list; on text of both verses see Dr).
