[מַחֲרֵשָׁה, מַחֲרֶ֫שֶׁת] noun feminine ploughshare — singular suffix לִלְטוֺשׁ אִישׁ מַחֲרַשְׁתּוֺ v1Sam 8:20 to sharpen each man his ploughshare 1Sam 13:20 (+ אֵתוֺ, קַרְדֻּמּוֺ) + מַחֲרֵשָׁתוֺ v1Sam 13:20, read probably דָּרְבָּנוֺ his goad (so ᵐ5 ᵑ6 We Dr, see v1Sam 13:21); plural absolute מַחֲרֵשֹׁת v1Sam 13:21 (+ similar list; on text of both verses see Dr).
