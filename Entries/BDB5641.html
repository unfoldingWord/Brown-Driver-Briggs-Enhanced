BDB5640 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5642

סְנָאָה apparently proper name, masculine ׳בְּנֵי ס Ezra 2:35 = Neh 7:38, ׳בְּנֵי הַסּ Neh 3:3 ᵐ5 Σαανα, Σανανα(τ), etc., ᵐ5L Σενναα; MeyJudenth. 150, 154 reads סְנֻאָה (׳ס for ׳שׂ, compare סְנוּאָה) = sons of the hated (rejected) woman, i.e. the poorer classes of Jerusalem.
