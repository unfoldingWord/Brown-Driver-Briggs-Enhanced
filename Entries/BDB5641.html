BDB5641 [H5570]

Biblical Hebrew

סְנָאָה apparently proper name, masculine ׳ בְּנֵי ס Ezra 2:35 = Neh 7:38, ׳ בְּנֵי הַסּ Neh 3:3 𝔊 Σαανα, Σανανα(τ), etc., 𝔊 L Σενναα; MeyJudenth. 150, 154 reads סְנֻאָה (׳ ס for ׳ שׂ, compare סְנוּאָה) = sons of the hated (rejected) woman, i.e. the poorer classes of Jerusalem.
