b. of result or display of strength ׳ עשׂה ח achieve might = do valiantly Num 24:18 ( J E ), 1Sam 14:48; Ps 60:14 = Ps 108:14; Ps 118:15; Ps 118:16.
c. often in phrases ׳ גִּבּוֺר(הַ)ח, ׳ גִּבּוֺרֵי(הַ)ח hero(es) of strength, mighty man (men) of valour Josh 1:14 ( D ), Josh 6:2 ; Josh 8:3; Josh 10:7 (all J E ), Judg 6:12 ; Judg 11:1 ; 1Sam 9:1; 1Sam 16:18; 1Kgs 11:28 ; 2Kgs 5:1; 2Kgs 15:20 ; 2Kgs 24:14; 2Kgs 24:16; 1Chr 5:24; 1Chr 7:2; 1Chr 7:9; 1Chr 8:40 + (very frequently in Chronicles), note especially ׳ הַגִּבּוֺרִים וכל גבורי ח 1Chr 28:1 also ׳ ח ׳ ג 2Chr 13:3 (|| מלחמה ׳ ג); (הַ)חֲיָלִים ׳ ג 1Chr 7:5; 1Chr 7:7; 1Chr 7:11; 1Chr 7:40; 1Chr 11:26 (see also גִּבּוֺר, p. 150); ׳ בֶּןֿ(הַ)ח, ׳ בְּנֵי(הַ)ח Deut 3:18; Judg 18:2 + (see בֵּן 8, p. 121); ׳ אִישׁ ח, ׳ אַנְשֵׁי ח Judg 3:29; Judg 20:44; Judg 20:46; Judg 1:12; Judg 11:16; 2Sam 23:20 ( Qr ; Kt חי, on phrase בןאֿישׁ חיל compare Dr) = 1Chr 11:22 ( Kt חיל), 2Sam 24:9; Nah 2:4; Jer 48:15; 1Chr 10:12; Neh 11:6; Ps 76:6 ironical לִמְסֹךְ שֵׁכָר ׳ ח ׳ א Isa 5:22 = (|| גבורים לשׁתות יין ); Eccl 12:3 metaphor of legs.
