BDB2681 [H2389]

Biblical Hebrew

חָזָק adjective strong, stout, mighty; — ׳ ח Exod 10:19 + 18 t.; feminine חֲזָקָה Exod 3:19 + 31 t. + Ezek 3:14 ( Co for ᵑ0 verb 3 feminine singular); plural חֲזָקִים Judg 18:25 + 2 t.; construct חִזְקֵי Ezek 2:4 (strike out Co. see below) Ezek 3:7 —

1. strong:
a. of men, rarely predicate Num 13:18 (opposed to רָפֶה), Num 13:31 (comparative with מִן), Josh 14:11; Josh 17:18 (all J E ), Judg 18:26 (comparative with מִן); also of גֹּאֲלָם Jer 50:34; Prov 23:11 of hand of ׳ י Josh 4:24 ( D ), so Ezek 3:14 𝔊 Co (see above and חָזַק Qal 3); compare of Tyre הָֽיְתָה חֲזָקָה בַּיָּם Ezek 26:17 (strike out B Co).
b. usually attributive; of ram of Pharaoh Ezek 30:22 (opposed to נִשְׁבָּ֑רֶת); especially of hand of ׳ י in delivering Israel from Egypt, Exod 3:19 (gloss ? see Di ), Exod 6:1 (twice in verse); Exod 13:9 (all J ), Deut 6:21; Deut 7:8; Deut 9:26; Dan 9:15 in wonders done by agency of Moses Deut 34:12 also בְּכֹחַ גָּדוֺל וּבְיָד חֲזָקָה Exod 31:11 ( J E ), compare Neh 1:10, but oftener ביד חזקה וּבִזְרוֺעַ נְטוּיָה Deut 4:34; Deut 5:15; Deut 7:19; Deut 11:2; Deut 26:6; Jer 32:21 ; Ps 136:12 of control of enemies and deliverance from exile Ezek 20:33; Ezek 20:34 more generally Deut 3:24 (|| גָּדְלְךָ), 1Kgs 8:42 = 2Chr 6:32 of ׳ י's opposition to Zedekiah Jer 21:5 once of Edom, opposed to Israel בעם כבד וביד חזקה Num 20:20 ( J E ).
c. of sword of ׳ י Isa 27:1 (|| הַקָּשָׁה וְהַגְּדוֺלָה), of wind Exod 10:19 ( J ), 1Kgs 19:11 sound of trumpet (= loud) Exod 19:16 ( E ).
d severe, sharp, hot, of war 1Sam 14:52 (predicate), battle 2Sam 11:15, sickness 1Kgs 17:17 (predicate), famine 1Kgs 18:2.
e. firm, hard, of face, פנים Ezek 3:8, forehead, מֵצַח Ezek 3:8 adamant, simile of forehead Ezek 3:9 (comparative, with מִן); of sky Job 37:18 (predicate) in plural construct לֵב ׳ ח Ezek 2:4 hard of heart (|| קְשֵׁי פָנִים) but omitted by B Co, מֵֿצַח ׳ ח Ezek 3:7 hard (= impudent) of forehead (|| קְשֵׁילֵֿב ).
2. as substantive, a strong one, of ׳ י (בְּחָזָק, as, in the character of, a strong one) Isa 40:10 of ׳ י's agent Isa 28:2 (|| אַמִּץ); see also וְחָזָק לאֹ יְאַמֵּץ כֹחוֺ Amos 2:14 with מִן compare= one too strong for Jer 31:11 ; Ps 35:10. In Ezek 34:16 ᵑ0 uses ׳ הח in bad sense (|| הִשְּׁמֵנָה), 𝔊 Co, with different text, in good sense (see VB ).
