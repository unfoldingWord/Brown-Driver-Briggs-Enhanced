מִשְׁעִי noun feminine (? = מִשְׁעִית compare Thes Sm) cleansing, only ׳ לֹא רֻחַצְתְּ לְמ Ezek 16:4 thou wast not washed for cleansing (compare ᵑ7 Thes and Add98 Sm RV and others; ᵑ9 ad salutem, as if from ישׁע; ᵐ5 omitted, and so Co Be SS, compare Buhl; form strange, and word at best dubious)

מִשְׁעוֺל see I. שׁעל .
