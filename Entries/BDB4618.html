BDB4618 [H4321 H4319]

Biblical Hebrew

מִיכָֽיְהוּ proper name, masculine (so on an Israel scarab, GanneauJAS 1883, 156, No. 42) —

1. an Ephraimite, Judg 17:1; Judg 17:4 (in Judg 17:5ff., Judg 18:2ff called מִיכָה).
2. a prophet, the son of Imlah ( 𝔊 Μειχαίας), 1Kgs 22:8-26 (9 t.), || 2Chr 18:7-25 (7 t.) + 2Chr 18:8 Qr ( Kt מיכהו).
3. a contemporary of Jeremiah, Jer 36:11 (מִכָֽיְהוּ), Jer 36:13.
