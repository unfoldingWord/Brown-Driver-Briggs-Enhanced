יוֺתָם proper name, masculine (׳ י is perfect) —

1. king of Judah, son of Azariah ( ᵐ5 Ἰωαθαμ, Ἰωαθαν, Ἰωναθαν) 2Kgs 15:5; 2Kgs 15:7; 2Kgs 15:30; 2Kgs 15:32; 2Kgs 15:36; 2Kgs 15:38; 2Kgs 16:1; 1Chr 3:12; 1Chr 5:17; 2Chr 26:21; 2Chr 26:23; 2Chr 27:1; 2Chr 27:6; 2Chr 27:7; 2Chr 27:9; Isa 1:1; Isa 7:1; Hos 1:1; Mic 1:1.
2. youngest son of Jerubbaal (Gideon) Judg 9:5; Judg 9:7; Judg 9:21; Judg 9:57 ( ᵐ5 ut above; Manuscripts Ἰωθαμ, Ἰωαθαμ).
3. a descendent of Caleb 1Chr 2:47 ( ᵐ5 Ἰωαθαμ).

יֵשׁוּעַ see יְהוֺשׁוּעַ .
