BDB4050 [H3641]

Biblical Hebrew

כַּלְנֵ֑ה (van d. H, so Norzi; Baer כַּלְנֶ֑ה) proper name [of a location] in Babylonia, Gen 10:10 ( J ), 𝔊 Ξαλαννη; DlPa 225 proposes identify. with Babylonian Kul-unu = Zirlab (conquered by Sargon in 710: COT Gen 10:10 : Amos 6:2 ), but dubious, and site of Zirlab unknown.
