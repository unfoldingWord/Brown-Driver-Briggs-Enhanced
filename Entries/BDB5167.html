BDB5167 [H4302]

Biblical Hebrew

מַטָּע noun masculine place, or act of planting; plantation; — absolute ׳ מ Ezek 34:29 construct מַטַּע Isa 61:3 suffix מַטָּעוֺ Isa 60:21 ( Kt ; Qr מַטָּעַי); מַטָּעָהּ Ezek 17:7, also Ezek 31:4, but read מַטָּעֹה 𝔊 Hi Co Berthol Toy; plural construct מַטָּעֵי Mic 1:6 —

1. planting-place, מַטָּעֵי כָ֑רֶם Mic 1:6 planting-place for a vineyard, so Ezek 31:4 (read מַטָּעֹה, see above); probably also לְשֵׁם ׳ מ < read שָׁלֹם ׳ מ 𝔊 Co Berthol Toy i.e. peaceful (fruitful) planting-place; compare ׳ עֲרֻגוֺת מ Ezek 17:7 = beds where it was planted.
2. act of planting ׳ נֵצֶר מ Isa 60:21 shoot of his (my) planting (figurative of people).
3. ׳ י ׳ מ Isa 61:3 plantation of ׳ י (id.).
