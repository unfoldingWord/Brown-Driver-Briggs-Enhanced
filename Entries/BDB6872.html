יִפְתַּחאֵֿל proper name [of a location] (God openeth; compare Sabean proper name, masculine יפתחאל Hal148, l); — in ׳ גֵּייִפ (Γαφαηλ, Γαι και Φθαιηλ A 𝔊 L Γαι (Ι)εφθαηλ), valley between Zebulun and Asher Josh 19:14; Josh 19:27 ( P ); probably near Jêfât (Jotapata), and perhaps upper part of Wady `Abellîn, see BuhlGeogr.109, 223.
