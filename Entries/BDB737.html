Biblical Hebrew

אַרְאֵלִי proper name, masculine (apparently noun, of a people from foregoing, but 𝔊 Num 26:17 Ἀριηλ (Gen 46:16 Ἀροηλεις, Ἀροηδις, Ἀπηδεις) = foregoing) a son of Gad Gen 46:16; Num 26:17 also adjective [of a people] with article as proper name collective Num 26:17 מִשְׁפַּחַת הָאַרְאֵלִי 𝔊 Ἀριηλει.
