Biblical Hebrew

חֲמוּטַל Qr , חמיטל Kt , proper name, feminine (meaning dubious; perhaps, if Kt right, my husband's father is the dew, compare אֲבִיטַל) — ᵐ5 Αμειται, Μιτατ; A Αμιταλ, Αμιταθ, ᵐ5 L Αμιταλ; — mother of the kings Jehoahaz and Zedekiah, sons of Josiah 2Kgs 23:31; 2Kgs 24:18 Qr ( Kt חמיטל) = Jer 52:1 Qr ( Kt id.)
