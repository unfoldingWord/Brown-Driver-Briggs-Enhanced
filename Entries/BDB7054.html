BDB7054 [H6731]

Biblical Hebrew

II. צִיץ noun [masculine] meaning dubious; only in תְּנוּצִֿיץ לְמוֺאָב Jer 48:9 usually (after AW Ra Ki and others) wings (collective) (compare Aramaic צִיץ wing, fin, Thes Gf and others);

Aramaic צִיץ wing dubious Levy322 b; Aramaic נוֺצִיץ very rare; but note Late Hebrew צִיץ filament, small hair. compare PerlesAnal.46 , who reads נוֺצִיץ (wing-) feathers (as in Aramaic); 𝔊 σημεῖα, whence Gie sign-post, reading צִיוּן, — or נֵס, — (indicating flight).
