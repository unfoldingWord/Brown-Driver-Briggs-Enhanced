BDB6008 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6010

עִיִּים proper name [of a location] Γαι, etc. (as if עַי):

1. station of Israel on east border of Moab, before דִּיבֹן גָּד Num 33:45, = עִיֵּי הָעֲבָרִים Num 33:44; Num 21:11 (all P ; ᵐ5 ignores הָעֲבָרִים).
2. city in Judah Josh 15:29 ( P ).

עֵילוֺם see עוֺלָם below עלם.
