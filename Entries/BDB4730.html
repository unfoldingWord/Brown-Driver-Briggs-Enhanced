Biblical Hebrew

תִּמְנָע proper name, masculine and feminine Edomite name. 𝔊 θαμνα: —

1. תִּמְנָע proper name, masculine
a. a chief of Edom Gen 36:40 = 1Chr 1:51.
b. son of Eliphaz 1Chr 1:36 (for Korah Gen 36:15; Gen 36:16).
2. תִּמְנָע֑ feminine Gen 36:22 = 1Chr 1:39 sister of Lotan (of Seir); Gen 3:12 concubine of Eliphaz son of Esau.
