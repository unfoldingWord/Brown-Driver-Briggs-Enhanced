BDB3461 [H3043]

Biblical Hebrew

יְדִיעֲאֵל proper name, masculine (Palmyrene ידיבעל DHM in MV10, 983 compare Sabean proper name with ידע DHMZMG 1875, 612) —

1. a Benjamite 1Chr 7:6; 1Chr 7:10; 1Chr 7:11, 𝔊 Αδειηλ, Ιεδιηλ, etc.
2. one of David's heroes according to 1Chr 11:45 apparently = the Manassite captain of 1Chr 12:21 (Baer; van d. H v.1Chr 12:20), 𝔊 Ελθειηλ, Ιεδιηλ.
3. a Korahite porter 1Chr 26:2 𝔊 Ιδερηλ, Ιεδιηλ.
