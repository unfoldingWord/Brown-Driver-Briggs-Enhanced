BDB5445 [H5417]

Biblical Hebrew

נְתַנְאֵל proper name, masculine (see GrayProp. N. 181, 192, 205 ; on proper names of this kind compare RSSem i. 100 n., 2nd ed. 108, GrayProp. N. 264); — 𝔊 Ναθαναηλ; —

1. a chief of Issachar Num 1:8; Num 2:5; Num 7:18; Num 7:23; Num 10:15.
2. 4th son of Jesse 1Chr 2:14.
3. name of priests:
a. 1Chr 15:24 .
b. Neh 12:21.
4. priest's son (who had strange wife) Ezra 10:22, perhaps = Neh 12:36.
5. Levite name:
a. 1Chr 24:6.
b. 1Chr 26:4.
c. 2Chr 35:9.
6. a prince of Jehoshaphat 2Chr 17:7.
