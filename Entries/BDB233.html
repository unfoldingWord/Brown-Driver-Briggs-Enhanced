BDB233 [H210]

Biblical Hebrew

旨驻指 (proper name [of a location] unknown & dubious) whence came gold, 壮 指指 值 Jer 10:9 壮 侄旨转侄 Dan 10:5 so Thes 1Kgs 10:18 指指 旨驻指 (旨驻指 = 壮 值); but 未魏喂渭慰, & 2Chr 9:17 指趾专, whence MV Klo make 旨驻指 Hoph`al Participle from 驻 q. v. Klo reads 趾驻执专 (q. v.) for 旨驻指 Jer 10:9; Dan 10:5, in view of 侄旨转侄 趾驻执专 Isa 13:12.

Corrupt, see SiegfThLz, Nov. 7, 1883, 530; he explained 壮 值 Jer 10:9 as error for 旨驻指 1Kgs 10:18, and 壮 Dan 10:5 as borrowed from Jer 10:9 in Jer 10:9 Gie reads 值趾驻执专, Du either this or 旨驻指 (see 驻); read 值趾驻执专 perhaps also Dan 10:5 (Dr).
