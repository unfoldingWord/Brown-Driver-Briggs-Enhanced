גְּדוֺר, גְּדֹר proper name (Arabic wall); —

1. proper name [of a location] city of Judah גְּדוֺר Josh 15:58 also as proper name, masculine, under figure of genealogy גְּדֹר son of Penuel 1Chr 4:4, גְּדוֺר son of Yered 1Chr 4:18 also הַגְּדוֺר 1Chr 12:7 see d. H.; הַגְּדוּד Baer, compare his note.
2. proper name [of a location] גְּדֹר 1Chr 4:39, but read Gerar, according to ᵐ5 Ew Hi Be and others
3. proper name, masculine גְּדוֺר 1Chr 8:31; 1Chr 9:37 a Benjamite, of Gibeon.
