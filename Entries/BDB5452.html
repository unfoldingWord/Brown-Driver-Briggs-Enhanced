BDB5452 [H4983]

Biblical Hebrew

מַתַּנְיָ֫הוּ, מַתַּנְיָה proper name, masculine (compare GrayProp. N. 294, No. 82 ); — ᵐ5 Μαθθας, Μανθανιας, Ματθανιας, etc.; —

1. מַתַּנְיָה, last king of Judah, name changed to Zedekiah 2Kgs 24:17.
2. מַתַּנְיָ֫הוּ, Levite name:
a. son of Heman 1Chr 25:4.
b. Asaphite 1Chr 25:16 .
c. id. , 2Chr 29:13 (Hezekiah's time).
3. מַתַּנְיָה, Levite name:
a. Asaphite 1Chr 9:15; Neh 11:17; Neh 11:22; Neh 12:8; Neh 12:35, perhaps = 2 .b., c. (but much confusion and uncertainty; e.g. ׳ מ is of Hezekiah's time 2Chr 29:13, and 4th generation before Nehemiah, Neh 11:12 ; Neh 12:35 while contemporary of Zerubbabel Neh 12:8 and of Nehemiah Neh 11:17).
b. other Levites: (1) 2Chr 20:14 , (2) Neh 12:25, (3) Neh 13:13.
4. מַתַּנְיָה, name of several who took strange wives:
a. Ezra 10:26 .
b. Ezra 10:27 .
c. Ezra 10:30 .
d. Ezra 10:37 .
