BDB6154 [H5990]

Biblical Hebrew

עַמִּיזָבָד proper name, masculine (my kinsman hath bestowed; compare זָבָד, אֶלְזָבָד, יְהוֺזָבָד, זַבְדִּיאֵל, זְבַדְיָה, and, especially on Palmyrene equivalent, see GrayProp. N. 223 f. also Lzb265 Cook46 f.); — son of Benaiah 1Chr 27:6, ᵐ5 Λαιβαζαθ, A Αμιραζαθ, ᵐ5 L Αμειναζαβαδ.
