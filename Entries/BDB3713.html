Biblical Hebrew

מוֺרָה noun [masculine] שִׁיתָה מוֺרָה לָהֶם Ps 9:21, probably appoint (Hos 6:11) terror (i.e. some awe-inspiring exhibition of power) for them Thes SS RV Ew De Hu Pe Che after Masora, ᵑ7 Aq Jerome (מוֺרָה a variation of or probably error for מוֺרָא); possibly set them a teacher, master Bae after 𝔊 ᵑ9 ᵑ6 מוֺרֶה, νομοθέτην; neither altogether satisfactory.
