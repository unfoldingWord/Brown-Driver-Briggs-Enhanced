BDB2123 [H3097]

Biblical Hebrew

יוֺאָב proper name, masculine (׳ י is father; compare אֲבִיָּהוּ, אֱלִיאָב, אֲבִיאֵל & references) —

1. David's sister's son & captain of his host ( 𝔊 Ἰωαβ) 1Sam 26:6; 2Sam 2:13 + 100 t. 2 Samuel (also 2Sam 20:6 for ᵑ0 אֲבִישַׁי compare ᵑ6 We Dr); 1Kgs 1:7 + 14 t. 1 Kings; 1Chr 2:16 + 22 t. 1 Chronicles; Ps 60:2.
2. a descendant of Judah 1Chr 4:14 (but 𝔊 B 𝔊 L Ἰωβαβ).
3. a family-name after the exile Ezra 2:6 = Neh 7:11 ( 𝔊 Ἰωβαβ, Ἰωαβ) Ezra 8:9 ( 𝔊 Ἰωαβ).
