Biblical Hebrew

II. חֹ֫רֶב noun [masculine] desolation; — ׳ עָרֵי ח Isa 61:4 cities of desolation (|| שֹׁמֲמוֺת, and, in va, חָרְבוֺת); compare Zeph 2:14 (but read עֹרֵב, 𝔊 We and others); Jer 49:13, and ׳ מִצְרַיִם חָרְבוֺת ח Ezek 29:10 Egypt shall be wastes of desolation (but 𝔊 ᵑ9 Co wastes of the sword, חֶרֶב for חֹרֶב); + Ezek 38:8 Co, who reads מֵחֹרֶב (or מֵחָרְבָּה).
