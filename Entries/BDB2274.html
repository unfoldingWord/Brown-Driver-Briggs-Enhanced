BDB2273 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2275

זַבְדִּי proper name, masculine (compare Arabic gift;? gift of ׳ י, see DrStud. Bib. i. 5 & references; perhaps my gift, gift to me: compare זַבְדִּיאֵל; in Late Hebrew proper name זבדי (זַבְדִּי or זַבְדַּי?); NT Ζεβεδαῖος, e.g. Mark 1:19, ᵑ6 , Zebedee) —

1. grandfather of Achan, of Judah ( ᵐ5 Ζαμβρ(ε)ι etc., ᵐ5 L Ζαβδ(ε)ι) Josh 7:1 (app. = זִמְרִי 1Chr 2:6, ᵐ5 Ζαμβρ(ε)ι) v1Chr 2:17 v1Chr 2:18.
2. a Benjamite 1Chr 8:19.
3. an officer of David 1Chr 27:27.
4. a Levite Neh 11:17 (omitted by B ; A Ζεχρι, ᵐ5 L Ζεχρει) probably read זִכְרִי, as || 1Chr 9:15.
