II. שַׁמָּה proper name, masculine

1. in Edom Gen 36:13; Gen 36:17; 1Chr 1:37 usually Σομε.
2. third son of Jesse 1Sam 16:9; 1Sam 17:13, Σαμ(μ)α, ᵐ5 L Σαμαα; see שִׁמְעָא , שִׁמְעָה, שִׁמְעִי.
3. Σαμαια(ς), etc., heroes of David:
a. 2Sam 23:11 (omitted accidentally || 1Chr 11:13 Dr), 1Chr 11:33.
b. 1Chr 11:25 = שַׁמּוֺת 1Chr 11:27 , Σαμαωθ, Σαμ(μ)ωθ; probably also שַׁמְהוּת 1Chr 27:8. p 1030,1035
