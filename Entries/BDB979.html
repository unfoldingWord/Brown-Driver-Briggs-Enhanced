Biblical Hebrew

בּוּר verb

Qal Infinitive construct לָבוּר Eccl 9:1 (with accusative); probably (si vera lectio) make clear, clear up, explain ( VB ; so sometimes, in Late Hebrew, ברר q. v.) but read perhaps וְלִבִּי רָאָה 𝔊 ᵑ6 Bi; or לָתוּר Gr, compare Eccl 1:13; Eccl 2:13; Eccl 7:25.
