BDB2444 [H2216]

Biblical Hebrew

זְרֻבָּבֶ֫ל proper name, masculine (according to MV perhaps = זְרוּעַ בָּבֶל begotten in Babylon, see also Thes)

probably = Babylonian Zêr-Bâbili = off-spring of Babylon, often in Inscriptions (MeyE. Jud. v). — grandson of king Jehoiachin, and son of Pedaiah 1Chr 3:19 (twice in verse) (but son of Shealtiel Ezra 3:2; Ezra 3:8 etc., compare שְׁאַלְתִּיאֵל brother (?) of פְּדָיָה 1Chr 3:17); leader of first returning exiles Ezra 2:2 = Neh 7:7; Ezra 3:2; Ezra 3:8; Ezra 4:2; Ezra 4:3; Neh 12:1; Neh 12:47; Hag 1:12; Hag 2:4; Zech 4:6; Zech 4:7; Zech 4:9; Zech 4:10 called פַּחַת יְהוּדָה Hag 1:1; Hag 1:14; Hag 2:2; Hag 2:21, called עַבְדִּי (by ׳ י) Hag 2:23. — 𝔊 Ζοροβαβελ.
