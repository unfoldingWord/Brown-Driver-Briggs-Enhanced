BDB3310 [H2900]

Biblical Hebrew

טוֺבִיָּ֫הוּ, טוֺבִיָּה proper name, masculine (Yah(u) is my good) —

1. טוֺבִיָּהוּ a Levite, assigned to reign of Jehoshaphat 2Chr 17:8, not 𝔊 ; 𝔊 L Τωβιας, elsewhere טוֺבִיָּה 𝔊 Τωβια(ς).
2. Ammonite adversary of Nehemiah Neh 2:10; Neh 2:19; Neh 3:35; Neh 4:1; Neh 6:1; Neh 6:12; Neh 6:14; Neh 6:17 (twice in verse); Neh 6:19; Neh 13:4; Neh 13:7; Neh 13:8.
3. head of a family of returning exiles of doubtful lineage Ezra 2:60 = Neh 7:62.
4. a chief of returning exiles Zech 6:10; Zech 6:14, 𝔊 χρησίμων(‐μοις)αὐτῆς, i.e. טוֺבֶיהָ.
