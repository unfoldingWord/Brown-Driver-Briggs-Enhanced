Biblical Hebrew

II. לוּז proper name [of a location]

1. former name of Bethel Gen 28:19 ( J ), Gen 35:6 ( E ), Gen 48:3 ( P ), Josh 18:13 (twice in verse) ( P ), Judg 1:23 apparently distinct from Biblical מִבֵּיתאֵֿל לוּ֫זָה Josh 16:2 ( J E ; but ׳ ל here perhaps explanatory gloss, see Di); 𝔊 Λουζα, Gen 28:19 [Ουλαμ]μαυς
2. city in hands of Hittites Judg 1:26 (on conjectures as to site see GFM ); 𝔊 Λουζα.
