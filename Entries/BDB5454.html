מַתִּתְיָ֫הוּ, מַתִּתְיָה proper name, masculine (compare GrayProp. N. 294, No. 81 ); — ᵐ5 Ματταθιας, etc; —

1. a Levitical musician, מַתִּתְיָ֫הוּ 1Chr 15:18; 1Chr 15:21; 1Chr 25:3; 1Chr 25:21 = מַתִּתְיָה 1Chr 16:5.
2. מַתִּתְיָה, Levitical name 1Chr 9:31.
3. ׳ מ, attendant of Ezra (also Levite ?) Neh 8:4.
4. one of those who took strange wives Ezra 10:43.
