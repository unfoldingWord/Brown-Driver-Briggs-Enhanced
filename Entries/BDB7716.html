Biblical Hebrew

רִבְלָה proper name [of a location]

1. ׳ ו in land of Hamath 2Kgs 23:33; 2Kgs 25:21 = Jer 52:27; Jer 39:6 with ה locative, רִבְ֫לָתָךְ 2Kgs 25:6; 2Kgs 25:20 = Jer 52:26, also ('in land of Hamath') Jer 39:5; Jer 52:9 בְּרִבְלָ֑תָה Jer 52:10 read רִבְדָמל֫תָה also Ezek 6:14 (for ᵑ0 ׳ דִּב, see [ דִּבְלָה ]); 𝔊 usually Δεβλαθα (also Ezek 6:14); modern Ribla on Orontes, RobBR ii. 507; iii. 543 ff. BdPal 3. 405.
2. (מִשְּׁפָם) הָרִבְלָה Num 34:11, on northeast border of land of Israel, certainly not so far north as 1, but site unknown; 𝔊 ἀπὀ σεπφαμαρ Βηλα, whence Di and others conjecture הַרְבֵּ֫לָךְ, but not Harmel, 8 miles southwest from ׳ ו 1 (WetzstZAW iii (1883), 274 f.), see DrHast. DB.
