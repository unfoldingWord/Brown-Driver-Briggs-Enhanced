BDB4744 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4746

[מָסַר] verb si vera lectio deliver up, offer (Late Hebrew מָסַר; Aramaic deliver up, denounce, betray, מְסַר; HomZMG xivi. 1892, 530 compare Sabean מסר take away); —

Qal Infinitive construct ׳ לִמְסָרמַֿ֫עַל בי Num 31:16 offer (commit) a trespass against ׳ י, but read לִמְעֹל, compare Thes Di (see Num 5:6; 2Chr 36:14).
Niph`al Imperfect וַיִּמָּֽסְרוּ Num 31:5 delivered over to, assigned to (of warriors), read perhaps וַיִּסָּֽפְרוּ ( ᵐ5 . ἐξηρίθμησαν) compare Thes.

מֹסֵרוֺת, מָסֹ֫רֶת see אסר .

[מֹסָר] see מוּסָר below יסר.
