BDB2107 [H3077 H3111]

Biblical Hebrew

יְהוֺיָדָע, יוֺיָדָע proper name, masculine (׳ י knoweth; compare יְדַעְיָה, אֶלְיָדָע & references, בְּעֶלְיָדָע) —

1. ׳ יְהוֺ; 𝔊 usually Ἰωδαε, A sometimes Ἰωιαδαε (2Sam 23:30 + ), 𝔊 L Ἰωαδ ( 2Sam 20:23 ὠαδδαι), father of Benaiah (David's time), in combination ׳ בְּנָיָהוּ בֶּןיְֿהוֺ 2Sam 8:18; 2Sam 20:23; 2Sam 23:20; 2Sam 23:22; 1Kgs 1:8; 1Kgs 1:26; 1Kgs 1:32; 1Kgs 1:36; 1Kgs 1:38; 1Kgs 1:44; 1Kgs 2:25; 1Kgs 2:29; 1Kgs 2:34; 1Kgs 2:35; 1Kgs 2:46; 1Kgs 4:4; 1Chr 11:22; 1Chr 11:24; 1Chr 18:17; 1Chr 27:5 יְהוֺיָדָע בֶּןבְּֿנָיָהוּ 1Chr 27:34 is probably erroneous inversion of the usual order; ׳ יְהוֺ alone only 1Chr 12:28 (where called הַנָּגִיד לְאַחֲרֹן).
2. ׳ יְהוֺ, 𝔊 usually Ἰωδαε A sometimes Ἰωιαδαε etc., (chief) priest at Jerusalem in time of Joash 2Kgs 11:4; 2Kgs 11:9 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 11:15; 2Kgs 11:17; 2Kgs 12:3; 2Kgs 12:8; 2Kgs 12:10; 2Chr 22:11; 2Chr 23:1; 2Chr 23:8 (twice in verse); 2Chr 23:9 ; 2Chr 23:11 ; 2Chr 23:14 ; 2Chr 23:16 ; 2Chr 23:18 ; 2Chr 24:2 ; 2Chr 24:3; 2Chr 24:6; 2Chr 24:12; 2Chr 24:14 (twice in verse); 2Chr 24:15; 2Chr 24:17; 2Chr 24:20; 2Chr 24:22; 2Chr 24:25; Jer 29:26.
3. יוֺיָדָע a builder at wall of Jerusalem, with Nehemiah, Neh 3:6 ( 𝔊 Ἰοειαα, Ἰοειδα, Ἰωδαε etc.)
4. ׳ יוֺ son of high priest Eliashib Neh 12:10; Neh 12:11; Neh 12:22; Neh 13:28 ( 𝔊 Ἰωδα, Ἰωαδαε, Ἰωιαδα).
