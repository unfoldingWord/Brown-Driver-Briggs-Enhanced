BDB8561 [H8669]

Biblical Hebrew

[תְּשׁוּקָה] noun feminine longing; — of woman for man, אֶלאִֿישֵׁח תְּשׁוּקָתֵח Gen 3:16 ( J ); of man for woman, אֲנִי לְדוֺדִי וְאֵלַי תְּשׁוּקָתוֺ Song 7:11 of beast to devour, figurative אֵלֶיךָ תְּשׁוּקָתוֺ Gen 4:7 ( J ). ( 𝔊 ἀποστροφή Genesis, ἐπιστροφή Canticles, whence NesMarg. 6 proposes תְּשׁוּבָתֵךְ Gen 3:16, which BallHpt reads in all; but how explain the unusual and striking word in MT?).
