מְרִיב בַּ֫עַל proper name, masculine (baal is (our, my, his) advocate (?), compare NesEg.120 Dr284, 4; but GrauProp. N. 201 thinks מרי בעל original form = hero of Baal); —

1. son of Jonathan 1Chr 8:34, בָּ֑עַל ׳ מ V:34; 1Chr 9:40 = מְרִיבַֿעַל (err. ?) V:40 = מְפִיבשֶׁת (later change, see בּשֶׁת below בושׁ) 2Sam 4:4; 2Sam 9:6 (twice in verse); 2Sam 9:10; 2Sam 9:11; 2Sam 9:12 (twice in verse); 2Sam 9:13; 2Sam 16:1; 2Sam 16:4; 2Sam 19:26; 2Sam 19:31; 2Sam 21:7, מְפִבשֶׁת 2Sam 16:25 (called בֶּןשָֿׁאוּל); ᵐ5 in 2 Μεμφι Βοσθε, in 1 Chronicles (usually) Μεριβααλ; ᵐ5 L Mεμφιβααλ in 2 Samuel (except 2Sam 4:1; 2Sam 4:2 etc., where notin HCT).
2. מְפִבשֶׁת son of saul and Rispah, according to 2Sam 21:8, ᵐ5 (including ᵐ5 L ) Μεμφιβοσθε.
