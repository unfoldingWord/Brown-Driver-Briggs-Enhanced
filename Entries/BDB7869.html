Biblical Hebrew

רְחוּם, רְחֻם proper name, masculine (compassion, or softness, gentleness ?); — post-exilic name:

1. with Zerubbabel:
a. רְחוּם Ezra 2:2, A Ιρεουμ’, 𝔊 L Ναουμ ( = נְחוּם || Neh 7:7).
b. priest, רְחֻם Neh 12:3 Ρεουμ ( = חָרִם Neh 12:15, compare Neh 7:42 = Ezra 2:39; Ezra 10:21, see חָרִם 3.a.).
2. רְחוּם, with Nehemiah:
a. Levite Neh 3:17, Βασουθ, א A 𝔊 L Ραουμ.
b. Neh 10:26, Ρα[ε]ουμ.
