יַתִּיר, יַתִּר proper name, of a location town in Judah (√ dubious); — יַתִּיר Josh 15:48, ᵐ5 Ι(ε)θερ; elsewhere יַתִּר, Levitical city Josh 21:14 (both P), ᵐ5 Αιλωμ, A ᵐ5L Ιεθερ, 1Chr 6:42, ᵐ5 [1Chr 6:43] Ιεθθαρ, A Ιεθερ; probably = יַתִּר 1Sam 30:27, ᵐ5 Γεθθορ, A Ειεθερ; — acc, to Onom. = Ιεθειρα, village 20 miles from Eleuthropolis, LagOnom. 266, 133; ed. 2; identified by RobBR. i, 494 (who, however, questions (עׅ = י) BdPal. 3, 153 with 'Attîr, halfway between Hebron and Milh; so, 'perhaps,' BuhlGeogr. § 91, p. 164; this not certain, compare Di.
