BDB498 [H491]

Biblical Hebrew

[אַלְמָנוּת] noun feminine widowhood (Mishna id., Phoenician אלמת) suffix בִּגְדֵי אַלְמְנוּתָהּ her widow's garments Gen 38:14; Gen 38:19 construct אַלְמְנוּת חַיּוּת 2Sam 20:3 (of David's imprisoned concubines; but text impossible; 𝔊 We אַלְמָנוֺת חַיּוֺת, compare Dr; Klo thinks gloss); figurative of Jerusalem Isa 54:4 אַלְמְנוּתַיִח.
