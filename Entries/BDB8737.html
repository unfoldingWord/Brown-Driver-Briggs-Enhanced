BDB8737 [H8003]

Biblical Hebrew

I. שָׁלֵם adjective complete, safe, at peace; — ׳ שׁ Gen 15:16 +; plural שְׁלֵמִים Gen 34:21; Nah 1:12 feminine שְׁלֵמָה Deut 25:15 +; plural שְׁלֵמוֺת Deut 27:6; Josh 8:31 —

1. complete:
a. full, perfect: אָבָן שְׁלֵמָה full weight Deut 25:15 , ׳ אֵיפָה שׁ Deut 25:15; Prov 11:1 of עֶוֺן Gen 15:16 ( J E ); number of captives Amos 1:6; Amos 1:9 of army Nah 1:12 (text corrupt; 𝔊 משֵׁל מַיִם, but ?; see Commentaries); of reward Ruth 2:12 of stones, whole (in natural condition, unhewn) Deut 27:6; Josh 8:31 (compare law Exod 20:25 ).
b. finished: stones for temple 1Kgs 6:7 temple 2Chr 8:16.
2. safe, unharmed, of person Gen 33:18 ( P ; Samaritan שׁלוֺם, compare בשׁלום Gen 28:21 not proper name, of a location as Vrss ).
3. in covt. of peace, friendship, with אֵת Gen 34:21 ( P ); ׳ לֵבָב שָׁלֵם עִם י a mind at peace with ׳ י, keeping covt. relation, hence complete, perfect, 1Kgs 8:61; 1Kgs 11:4; 1Kgs 15:3; 1Kgs 15:14 with אֶל 2Chr 16:9, אֶל omitted 2Kgs 20:3 = Isa 38:3 (לֵב), 1Chr 12:38; 2Chr 15:17; 2Chr 19:9; 2Chr 25:2 לֵב 1Chr 28:9; 1Chr 29:9.
