Biblical Hebrew

[יְשִׁימָה] noun feminine desolation, only intensive plural יְשִׁימוֺת Ps 55:16 Kt ( Qr יַשִּׁיא מָוֶת), so Ges Hup and others, compare proper name, of a location בית הישׁימות above; 𝔊 Ew Ol Pe De Bae and others follow Qr , let death come deceitfully upon them, see נשׁא .
