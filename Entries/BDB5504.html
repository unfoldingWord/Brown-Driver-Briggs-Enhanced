BDB5504 [H5465]

Biblical Hebrew

סַד noun [masculine] stocks, for confining feet of culprits (probably loan-word from Aramaic סַדָּא, סַדָּנָא ; Late Hebrew סַד, plural סַדִּין); = Latin nervus, Greek ποδοκάκη; — absolute רַגְלַיִם ׳ שִׂים בַּמּ Job 13:27 ( 𝔊 κώλύμα), Job 33:11 ( ᵑ6 ξύλό); (compare synonym מַהְפֶּכֶת, and Greek equivalent there cited).
