BDB5041 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5043

I. [נָוָה] verb only

Hiph`il (si vera lectio) literally beautify (then connected with נאה be comely, so Buhl SS; compare Late Hebrew נוה Niph`al shew oneself beautiful, Nithp. adorn oneself, נָוֶה adjective beautiful, LevyNHWB); — Imperfect 1 singular suffix אַנְוֵהוּ Exod 15:2 I will beautify, adorn him (with praises; object ׳ י; || רומם; ᵐ5 δυξάσω, ᵑ7 glorificabo). — adjective נָוָה Jer 6:2, see נָאוֶה below [נָאָה].
