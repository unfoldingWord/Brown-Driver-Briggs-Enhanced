Biblical Hebrew

קִיקָיוֺן noun masculine Jonah 4:6 a plant (compare Assyrian Kukkânîtum (כ) a garden-plant, DlHWB 327 ); — usually ricinus (R. communis, Linn. = castor-oil tree; compare Dioscoriv. 164 κίκι (Egypt) = κρότων [castor-oil tree]; Talmud שֶׁמֶן קִיק see especially Löwp. 353 f.); perhaps < bottle-gourd ( 𝔊 κολόκυνθα; i.e. cucurbita lagenaria, a vine growing and withering rapidly, Post in HastingsDB ii. 250); — Jonah 4:6 (twice in verse); Jonah 4:7; Jonah 4:9; Jonah 4:10.

קִיקָלוֺן see קלל .
