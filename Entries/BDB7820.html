BDB7820 [H8641]

Biblical Hebrew

תְּרוּמָה noun feminine contribution, offering, for sacred uses (not certain before Deuteronomy; chiefly Ezekiel, P and late) (properly something lifted off, separated); — absolute ׳ ת Ezek 45:13 +; construct תְּרוּמַת Exod 30:14 +; suffix תְּרוּמָתִי Exod 25:2, מַתְכֶם- Num 18:27, מָתָם- Exod 29:28 pluralתְּרוּמוֺת Neh 12:44; Prov 29:4, מֹת- 2Sam 1:21; Num 18:19 suffix תְּרוּמֹתָ֑י Num 18:8, etc.; —

1. earliest use: contribution of products of soil for ׳ י, תְּרוּמַת יֶדְכֶם Deut 12:6; Deut 12:11, compare Deut 12:17 also Esz Deut 20:40; Num 15:19; Num 15:20; Num 15:21.
2. contribution for prince, in Ezekiel's scheme: Ezek 45:13; Ezek 45:16
3. tract of land to be set apart for temple, and for use of priests and Levites, in Ezekiel's scheme: Ezek 45:1; Ezek 48:8; Ezek 48:9; Ezek 48:21b; called הַקֹּדֶשׁ ׳ ת Ezek 45:6; Ezek 45:7 (twice in verse); Ezek 48:10; Ezek 48:18; Ezek 45:18; Ezek 45:20b; Ezek 45:21a; Ezek 45:21c; הָאָרֶץ קֹדֶשׁ קָֽדָשִׁים ׳ ת Ezek 45:12.
4. contribution to ׳ י, set apart for priests, Ezek 44:30; Lev 22:12; Num 5:9; Num 18:8; Num 18:11; Num 18:19; 2Chr 31:10; 2Chr 31:12; 2Chr 31:14 specifically:
a. of cakes and cereals Lev 7:14 ; Neh 10:38; Neh 10:40 (also for Levites), Neh 12:44 (id.), Neh 13:5 compare וּשְׂדֵי תְרוּמוֺת 2Sam 1:21, i.e. (si vera lectio) fields yielding sacred imposts, but very dubious; 𝔊 L ὄρη θανάτου, compare We; JP PetJBL xii (1893), 54 proposes (הָרֵי) מָוֶת ׳ שׂ, ׳ ה as gloss; HPS שְׂדוֺת הַמָּוֶת; > Sta וּשְׂדֵי עֲרֵמוֺת; see also Dr.
b. of animal sacrifice, the thigh, ׳ שׁוֺק הַתּ Exod 29:27; Lev 7:34; Lev 10:14; Lev 10:15; Num 6:20.
c. priests' share of Levites' tithe, Num 18:26; Num 18:27; Num 18:28 (twice in verse); Num 18:29.
d. of booty Num 31:29; Num 31:41; Num 31:52.
e. tithe for Levites Num 18:24.
5. materials for tabernacle, sacred garments, etc., Exod 25:2 (twice in verse); Exod 25:3; Exod 35:5 (twice in verse); Exod 35:21; Exod 35:24 (twice in verse); Exod 36:3; Exod 36:6 for temple Ezra 8:25.
6. half-shekel for maintaining service of sanctuary Exod 30:13; Exod 30:14; Exod 30:15.
7. late, contribution, in general, אִישׁ תְּרוּמוֺת Prov 29:4 a man of contributions, i.e. demanding them, = man of exactions. — ׳ תּ Isa 40:20 of idol-image (si vera lectio), so still Di-Kit; read perhaps תְּמוּנָה 𝔊 (ὁμοίωμα Isa 40:19) Du; other conjectures in CheHpt, see also III. [סָכַן]. — Vbs. with ׳ ת are: הֵרִים Ezek 45:1 + 14 t., הֵבִיא Deut 12:6 + 9 t., נָתַן Ezek 44:30 + 9 t., לָקַח Exod 25:2 (twice in verse) + 4 t., הִקְרִיב Lev 7:14; Num 5:9. — See Dr Deut 12:6 ; Hast.
