מִכְסֶה noun [masculine] covering; — ׳ מ Exod 26:14; Exod 36:19 construct מִכְסֵה Gen 8:13 + 10 t.; suffix מִכְסֵהוּ Exod 35:11 + 2 t.; —

1. covering of the ark, deck-roof ( 𝔊 στέγη) Gen 8:13 ( J ).
2. of the skins of the tent of meeting Exod 26:14 (twice in verse); Exod 35:11; Exod 36:19 (twice in verse); Exod 39:34 (twice in verse); Exod 40:19; Num 3:25; Num 4:8; Num 4:10; Num 4:11; Num 4:12; Num 4:25 (twice in verse) (all P ).
