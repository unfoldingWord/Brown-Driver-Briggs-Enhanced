BDB9061 [H8334]

Biblical Hebrew

[שָׁרַת]97 verb Pi`el minister, serve (compare Late Hebrew שֵׁרוּת (temple-)ministry , Phoenician משרת ministry, and perhaps verb שרת minister); —

Pi`el Perfect 3 masculine singular שֵׁרֵת Num 8:26; Deut 18:7 3 masculine plural שֵׁרְתוּ Num 3:6 Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיְשָׁ֫רֶת Gen 39:4, 3 masculine plural יְשָֽׁרְתוּ Num 3:31 +, etc.; Infinitive construct לְשָׁרֵת Exod 28:35 +, לְשָׁ֫רֶת שָׁם Deut 17:12, etc.; Participle מְשָׁרֵת Num 11:28 + (2Sam 13:9 see מַשְׂרֵת , p. 602:a), feminine מְשָׁרַת 1Kgs 1:15, etc.; — serve:
1. of ministerial service (opposed to menial, עבד):
a. higher domestic service: Joseph Gen 39:4; Gen 40:4 ( J E ), Elisha's chief servant 2Kgs 4:43; 2Kgs 6:15, royal domestics 2Sam 13:17; 2Sam 13:18 ; 1Kgs 10:5; 2Chr 9:4; Esth 2:2; Esth 6:3, Abishag 1Kgs 1:4; 1Kgs 1:15.
b. of royal officers (late) 1Chr 27:1; 1Chr 28:1; 2Chr 17:19 ; 2Chr 22:8; Esth 1:10 ; Prov 29:12 .
c. Joshua as chief assistant to Moses Exod 24:13; Exod 33:11 ( E ), Num 11:28 ( J ), Josh 1:1 ( D ), Elisha to Elijah 1Kgs 19:21.
d. of angels to ׳ י Ps 103:21 , compare Ps 104:4.
e. of kings of nations in prophets Isa 60:10.
2. usually of special service in worship:
a. of Levitical priests, absolute 1Kgs 8:11 = 2Chr 5:14 with accusative ׳ י Deut 10:8; Deut 17:12 ; Deut 21:5; Jer 33:21 + (strangely) מְשָֽׁרְתֵי אֹתִי Jer 33:22 also Isa 60:7 of animals put figurative for those sacrificing them; of foreigners admitted to priesthood Isa 61:6, compare Isa 56:6 so probably Samuel conceived as priest 1Sam 2:11 ; 1Sam 3:1 , ׳ את פני י 1Sam 2:18 ( פני probably accusative, compare 𝔊 τῷ προσώπῳ 1Sam 2:11), ׳ בְּשֵׁם י ׳ שׁ Deut 18:5; Deut 18:7.
b. Levites ( P , and late); with accusative of Aaronic priests Num 3:6; Num 8:26; Num 18:2, with accusative of ׳ י 1Chr 15:2 ; 2Chr 29:11 a; probably also in reference to Levitical singer Ps 101:6 with accusative tabernacle Num 1:50 ( P ), congregation Num 16:9 ( P ), people Ezek 44:11 + Ezek 44:12 (of idolatry); absolute 1Chr 16:4; 2Chr 23:6; 2Chr 29:11 b 2Chr 31:2 ; Ezra 8:17 (participle as substantive, ministers); לִפְנֵי ׳ שׁ ׳ אֲרוֺן בְּרִית י 1Chr 16:37 , נֶגֶד הַכֹּהֲנִים ׳ שׁ 2Chr 8:14 ׳ שׁ with ב location 1Chr 26:12 especially with בְּ, of sacred vessels Num 3:31 ; Num 4:9; Num 4:12; Num 4:14; 2Kgs 25:14 = Jer 52:18 בַּשִּׁיר 1Chr 6:17 .
c. Zadokite priests (Ezekiel), absolute Ezek 42:14 + 3 t., with accusative ׳ י Ezek 40:48 + 4 t., construct מְשָׁרֲתֵי before sacred place Ezek 45:4; Ezek 45:5; Ezek 46:24 .
d. Aaronic priests, absolute Exod 28:35 + 4 t. P ; with accusative ׳ י 1Chr 23:13 ׳ לי 2Chr 13:10 ׳ מְשָֽׁרְתֵי י Joel 1:9; Joel 1:13 ; Joel 2:17 , מְשָֽׁרְתֵי מזבח Joel 1:13 , with בַּקֹּדֶשׁ Exod 28:43 + 4 t. P.
e. of idolatry, with accusative עץ ואבן Ezek 20:32 .
