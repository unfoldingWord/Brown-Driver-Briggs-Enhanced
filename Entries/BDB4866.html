מֶ֫שִׁי noun [masculine] a costly material for garments, according to Rabb silk, (so NowArchaeology i. 124); whether this is based on actual tradition is dubious (etymology unknown); — absolute ׳ מ Ezek 16:13 (|| שֵׁשׁ), מֶ֑שִׁי Ezek 16:10 (|| id.) See, further Thes Sm Da Berthol. ᵐ5 τρίχαπτα.
