Biblical Hebrew

II. נָבָל proper name, masculine (on popular etymology see 1Sam 25:25 below); — a churlish man of Carmel, whose widow David married 1Sam 25:3 ; 1Sam 25:4; 1Sam 25:5; 1Sam 25:9; 1Sam 25:10; 1Sam 25:14; 1Sam 25:19; 1Sam 25:25 (twice in verse) (כִּשְׁמוֺ כֶּןהֿ֔וּא נָבָל שְׁמוֺ וּנְּבָלָה עִמּוֺ), 1Sam 25:26 + 10 t. 1 Samuel + 2Sam 2:2; 2Sam 3:3. 𝔊 Ναβαλ.
