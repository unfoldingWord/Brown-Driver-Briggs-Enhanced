BDB4650 [H4393]

Biblical Hebrew

מְלֹא, [מְלוֺא] noun masculine Isa 8 Isa 1:4 fulness, that which fills (Arabic Lane2729); — ׳ מ construct Gen 48:19 + 19 t.; מְלוֺ Ezek 41:8 suffix מְלוֺאוֺ Isa 42:10 + 3 t.; מְלוֺאָהּ Deut 33:16 + 12 t.; —

1. fulness of hand (= handful) Eccl 4:6 (twice in verse) followed by accusative of material חָפְנֵיכֶם פִּיחַ ׳ מ Exod 9:8 compare Lev 16:12; 1Kgs 17:12 so homer-full of manna; Exod 16:33 compare Lev 16:12; Num 22:18; Num 24:13; Judg 6:38 simil. מְלֹא בְגְדוֺ 2Kgs 4:39 rarely followed by מִן Lev 2:2; Lev 5:12, compare Exod 16:32 (but read perhaps מִלְאוּ so 𝔊 ⅏ see Di).
2. mass of shepherds Isa 31:4 (in sim); multitude of nations Gen 48:19 (compare Arabic Qor 2:247 and elsewhere)
3. fullness = that which fills, entire contents, of אֶרֶץ Deut 33:16; Ps 24:1; Isa 6:3; Isa 8:8; Isa 34:1; Mic 1:2; Jer 8:16; Jer 47:2; Ezek 12:19 ; Ezek 19:7; Ezek 30:12 ; Ezek 32:15 תֵּבֵל Ps 50:12; Ps 89:12 יָם Isa 42:10; 1Chr 16:32 ; Ps 96:11 ; Ps 98:7 עִיר Amos 6:8.
4. phrases: — קוֺמָתוֺ ׳ מ = at full length 1Sam 28:20 full line, or portion הַחֶ֫בַל ׳ מ 2Sam 8:2 הַקָּנֶה ׳ מ Ezek 41:8 full rod (reed).
