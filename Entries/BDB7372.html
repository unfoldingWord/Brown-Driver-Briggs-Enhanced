מָקוֺם, מָקֹם (Exod 29:31, etc.)399 noun masculine Gen 19:13 (but see below), standing-place, place; — absolute ׳ מ Exod 21:13 +; construct מְקוֺם Gen 12:6 + (often before אֲשֶׁר Gen 39:20 +); suffix מְקוֺמוֺ 1Sam 3:2 +, מְקֹמוֺ Gen 18:33 +, etc.; plural usually מְקֹמוֺת Judg 19:13 +, מְקוֺמוֺת 1Sam 7:16; Jer 29:14, מֹ֑ת 2Sam 17:9 + 2 t.; suffix מְקוֺמֹתֵיכֶם Amos 4:6, etc. [apparently feminine (not Gen 18:24, where עִיר is in mind, but) Job 20:9 (emendation see Commentaries), and Manuscripts (either Kt of Qr ) Judg 19:13 ; 2Sam 17:9 ; 2Sam 17:12 mostly explained away by AlbrZAW xvi (1896), 53]; —

1.
a. standing-place Exod 3:5 ( E ), Josh 5:15 ; Exod 33:21; Num 23:13; Num 23:27 (all J E ), Gen 19:27 ( J ), Josh 3:3 ( D ), of מַצֵּבוֺת, etc., 2Kgs 23:14, of ark (place where it is set, stands) 1Kgs 8:7 = 2Chr 5:8, earth Isa 13:13; Job 9:6, rock Job 14:18; Job 18:4 compare Job 38:12; Job 38:19.
b. station, where soldiers are placed Josh 8:19 ( J E ), Judg 20:33 (twice in verse); post in battle 2Sam 11:16.
c. post, office 1Kgs 20:24; Eccl 10:4.
2.
a. place where a thing belongs Gen 29:3 ( J ), Josh 4:18 ( J E ), 1Sam 5:3 ; Isa 46:7 ; Job 6:17 ; Job 37:1 (of the heart), Eccl 1:5 especially of ark 1Sam 5:11 + 9 t.; of person = destination Judg 11:19 .
b. especially (1) place of human abode Gen 13:14 ; Gen 20:13 ; Judg 7:7; 1Sam 2:20 + 42 t., +מְקוֺם אָהֳלֵךְ Isa 54:2 . (2) of ׳ י's abode Hos 5:15, in heaven Mic 1:3; Isa 26:21; 1Kgs 8:30 = 2Chr 6:21 . (3) lair of lion (figurative) Jer 4:7.
3. place =
a. city Gen 18:24 ; Gen 18:26; Gen 20:11; Deut 21:19 (|| עִיר) 2Kgs 18:25 + 39 t. ( 1Sam 7:16 הַמִּקְדָּשִׁים 𝔊 , compare We HPS).
b. of land: Canaan Exod 23:20; 1Sam 12:8; Jer 16:2 ; Jer 16:3 (|| אֶרֶץ) + 17 t. (12 t. Jeremiah); Philistia 1Sam 14:46 Egypt Jer 44:29 appositive מְדִינָה Esth 4:3 , = מְדִינָה + עִיר Esth 8:17 .
c. plot, parcel, of ground Isa 7:23 מְקוֺם הַגֹּרֶן 1Chr 21:22 , compare 1Chr 21:25; 2Chr 3:1.
d. land, region Num 20:5 ; Num 32:1 Che (privately) proposes בִּמְקֹם בְּקָעוֺת Ps 84:7 (for בְּעֵמֶק הַבָּכָא; compare Isa 41:18).
