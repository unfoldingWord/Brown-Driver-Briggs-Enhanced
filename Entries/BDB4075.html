מִכְמְתָת proper name, of a location always ׳הַמּ, Josh 16:6 (ᵐ5 Ικασμων, ᵐ5L εις Αχθωθ, A Μαχθωθ), Josh 17:7 (מֵאָשֵׁר ׳הַמּ; ᵐ5 Δηλαναθ, A ἀπὸ Ασερ Μαχθωθ, and similarly ᵐ5L), place in northeast Ephraim, near border of Manasseh, between Shechem and Taanath Shilo; BuhlGeogr. 109 conjecture khirbet kefr beita.
