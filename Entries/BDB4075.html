Biblical Hebrew

מִכְמְתָת proper name [of a location] always ׳ הַמּ, Josh 16:6 ( 𝔊 Ικασμων, 𝔊 L εις Αχθωθ, A Μαχθωθ), Josh 17:7 (מֵאָשֵׁר ׳ הַמּ; 𝔊 Δηλαναθ, A ἀπὸ Ασερ Μαχθωθ, and similarly 𝔊 L ), place in northeast Ephraim, near border of Manasseh, between Shechem and Taanath Shilo; BuhlGeogr. 109 conjecture khirbet kefr beita.
