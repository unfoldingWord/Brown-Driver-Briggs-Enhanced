דָּן proper name, masculine (judge) —

1. son of Jacob and Bilhah Gen 30:6; Gen 35:25; Exod 1:4; Josh 19:47; Judg 18:29; 1Chr 2:2.
2. the tribe of Dan, מַטֵּה דָן Exod 31:6; Exod 35:34; Exod 38:23; Lev 24:11; Num 1:39; Num 13:12; Josh 21:5; Josh 21:23 (all P ); בְּנֵי דָן Gen 46:23; Num 1:38; Num 2:25; Num 7:66; Num 10:25; Num 26:42; Num 34:22; Josh 19:40; Josh 19:47 (twice in verse); Josh 19:48 (all P ) Judg 1:34 ; Judg 18:2; Judg 18:16; Judg 18:22; Judg 18:23; Judg 18:25; Judg 18:26; Judg 18:30 מַחֲנֵה דָן Num 2:25; Num 2:31 ( P ) — on Judg 13:25; Judg 18:12 see מַחֲנֶה ; מִשְׁפְּחֹת דָּן Num 26:42 ( P ); דָּן alone in poetry Gen 49:16; Gen 49:17; Deut 33:22 (twice in verse); Judg 5:17, elsewhere Num 1:12 ( P ) Deut 27:13 ; 1Chr 27:22 ; 2Chr 2:13 ; Ezek 48:1; Ezek 48:2; Ezek 48:32.
3. a city on northern frontier of Israel at one of the sources of the Jordan, originally לַיִשׁ Judg 18:29 (compare Judg 18:7 ); = לֶשֶׁם Josh 19:47 captured by a colony of Danites; named Dan, and made a sacred place with rites of worship that lasted until the exile (Judg 18:30 ); = Tell el Kâdî, RobBR iii. 351, 358 BdPa1 265.
