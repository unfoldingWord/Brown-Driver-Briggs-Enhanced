BDB1646 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1648

גַּל noun masculine Gen 31:48 heap, wave, billow (as rolled together, rolling, rolling up), also spring, ᵑ0 Song 4:12 compare 3 below — absolute גַּל Job 8:17 + 5 t. (including הַגַּל Gen 31:48 +); גָּ֑ל Gen 31:46 הַגָּ֑ל Gen 31:46 לַגָּ֑ל Isa 25:2 construct גַּלֿ Josh 7:26 + 2t. + Gen 31:47; Gen 31:48 q.see below; plural גַּלִּים 2Kgs 19:25 + 6 t.; construct גַּלֵּיֿ Isa 48:18 suffix גַּלָּיו Ps 89:10 + 6 t.; גַּלֵּיהֶם Ps 65:8 + 2 t.; —

1. heap of stones,
a. אבנים ׳ ג raised (הקים) over dead body Josh 7:26; Josh 8:29 compare 2Sam 18:17 (with הִצִּיב ).
b. גַּל alone Job 8:17 (roots wrapped about it).
c. heap or pile made (עשׂה) for use in ratifying compact of Jacob with Laban Gen 31:46 (twice in verse); Gen 31:48; Gen 31:51; Gen 38:52 ; Gen 31:52 (twice in verse) compare also גַּלְעֵד proper name, dual heap of ruins Isa 25:2 elsewhere plural Hos 12:12; 2Kgs 19:25 = Isa 37:26 ; Job 15:28; Jer 9:10; Jer 51:37.
2. waves (rollers) in poetry, only plural, waves of sea Jer 5:22; Jer 31:35; Isa 51:15 ; Job 38:11; Ps 65:8; Ps 89:10 ; Ps 107:25 ; Ps 107:29 compare also in simile Ezek 26:3; Isa 48:18 (גַּלֵּיהַֿיָּם); figurative of chastisements from ׳ י Ps 42:8 (|| מִשְׁבָּרִים) Jonah 2:3 (|| id.); of army of Babylon's conqueror Jer 51:42; Jer 51:55 compare Zech 10:11 ( VB ; but BevJPh xviii. 88 proposes גִּבְלִים compare Ezek 27:9).
3. spring, Song 4:12 גַּן נָעוּל אֲחוֺתִי כַּלָּ֑ה גַּל נָעוּל מַעְיָן חָתוּם a garden barred (is) my sister, bride, a spring barred, a fountain sealed; but גַּן for גַּל ᵐ5 ᵑ6 ᵑ9 .
