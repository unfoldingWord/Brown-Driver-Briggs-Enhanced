1. nation, people Gen 10:5 (twice in verse); Gen 10:20; Gen 10:31; Gen 10:32 (twice in verse) (all P ) +; Isa 2:2 ; Isa 2:4 (twice in verse) = Mic 4:2; Mic 4:3 (twice in verse); Job 12:23 (twice in verse); Job 34:29 ; Prov 14:34 כֹּל גּוֺיֵי הָאָרֶץ Gen 18:18; Gen 22:18; Gen 26:4 (all J ); Deut 28:1 .
a. specifically of descendants of Abraham, גּוֺי גָּדוֺל Gen 12:2 compare Gen 18:18 (both J ), גּוֺיִם Gen 17:6, הֲמוֺן גּוֺיִם Gen 17:4; Gen 17:15 (all P ); of Sarah גּוֺיִם Gen 17:16 ( P ); of Ishmael גּוֺי Gen 21:13, גָּוֺי גָּדוֺל Gen 21:18 (both E ), גּוֺי גָּדוֺל Gen 17:20 ( P ); of Jacob גּוֺי וּקְהַל גּוֺיִם Gen 35:11 ( P ), גּוֺי גָּדוֺל Gen 46:3 ( E ); of Ephraim ׳ מְלֹא הַגּ Gen 48:19 ( J ); of Moses גּוֺי גָּדוֺל Exod 32:10 ( J ) compare Num 14:12 ( J ) Deut 9:14 of Jacob and Esau as two nations Gen 25:23 ( J ).
b. definitely of Israel Exod 19:6 (קָדוֺשׁ ׳ גּ) Exod 33:13 (both J E ), Deut 4:6 (הַגּוֺי הַגָּדוֺל הַזֶּה, said by heathen compare Deut 4:7 Deut 4:8) see also Deut 4:34, Deut 26:5 compare Ps 33:12; Ps 83:5 (said by enemies) Jer 31:36; Jer 33:24; Ezek 37:22 in narrative Josh 3:17; Josh 4:1; Josh 5:8 ( J E ), Josh 5:6 ( D ), Josh 10:13 (in poetry, no article); of Israel and Judah as two nations Ezek 35:10 (said by heathen) Ezek 37:22 of Judah Isa 26:2; Isa 26:15 (twice in verse); compare Isa 58:2; Isa 60:22; Mic 4:7 once my people Zeph 2:9 (|| עַם); thy people Ps 106:5 (i.e. of ׳ י), compare also Ezek 36:13; Ezek 36:14 (read Kt ); especially of Israel and (or) Judah as sinful, rebellious Deut 32:28; Judg 2:20; Isa 1:4; Isa 10:6; Jer 5:9; Jer 5:29; Jer 7:28; Jer 9:8; Jer 12:17; (strike out ᵐ5 Co) Hag 2:14; Mal 3:9. — Note. This definite reference to Israel and (or) Judah is comparatively rare; in Hexateuch not P (yet see Gen 17:4; Gen 17:5; Gen 17:6; Gen 17:16; Gen 35:11 P ); seldom in exile & post-exile prophets; not Chronicles —
c. usually of non-Hebrew peoples Exod 9:24; Exod 34:10 ( J E ) Lev 25:44 ( H ) Num 14:15 ( J ) Deut 15:6 (twice in verse); 1Kgs 5:11; 1Chr 14:17; 1Chr 16:20; Isa 11:10; Isa 11:12 + often; opposed to Israel as ׳ עם י 2Sam 7:23; 1Chr 17:21 (twice in verse) etc., see עם, compare also Num 23:9 note especially גְּלִיל הַגּוֺיִם Isa 8:23 circle or district of the nations (see גָּלִיל ); also ׳ חֲרשֶׁת הַגּ Judg 4:2; Judg 4:13; Judg 4:16 Charosheth of the nations, & מֶלֶךְ גּוֺיִם לְגִלְגָּ֖ל Josh 12:23 king of nations (peoples, tribes) belonging to Gilgal ( ᵐ5 Di to the district, i.e 'Galilee'); especially of these peoples as heathen: idolatrous Lev 8:24; Lev 8:28 ( P ) Lev 20:23 ( H ) 1Kgs 14:24; 2Kgs 17:8; 2Kgs 17:11; 2Kgs 17:15; 2Kgs 17:26; 2Kgs 17:29 (twice in verse); 2Chr 28:3; 2Chr 32:13 +, Ezra 6:21 ; Ezek 5:6 +; hostile Gen 15:14 ( J ) Lev 26:33; Lev 26:38; Lev 26:45 ( H ) Deut 4:27 ; Deut 9:4; Deut 9:5; Deut 18:9; 1Chr 16:35 ; Jer 5:15; Ezek 4:13 + often Jeremiah Ezekiel, etc.; in simile Ezek 20:32; Ezek 25:8 sometimes || עַם Ps 33:10; Ps 33:12; Isa 11:10; Jer 6:18, see also Isa 2:2; Isa 2:4 compare with Mic 4:1; Mic 4:3.
