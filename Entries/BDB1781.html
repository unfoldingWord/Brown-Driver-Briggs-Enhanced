Biblical Hebrew

גִּשְׁפָּא proper name, masculine named after צִיחָא as an officer of Nethinim Neh 11:21 see om. B ; 𝔊 L & Manuscripts Γεσφα; name not elsewhere in OT, not even || 1Chr 9 possibly corrupted from חֲשֻׂפָא Neh 7:46 (following צִחָא; in || Ezra 2:43 חֲשׁוּפָא, צִיחָא), 𝔊 Ἁσφα ( B ; Manuscripts Ασειφα, etc.); compare BeRy.
