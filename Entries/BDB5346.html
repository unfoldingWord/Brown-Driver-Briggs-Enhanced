1. Watch, guard, keep, a vineyard Job 27:18 (compare Wetzst in DeJob (2) on the passage), Isa 27:3 (twice in verse), fig-tree Prov 27:18, fortification Nah 2:2 נֹצְרִים watchmen Jer 31:6 ׳ מִגְדַּל נ tower of watchmen 2Kgs 17:9; 2Kgs 18:8 in ethical sense of men, guarding the mouth Prov 13:3, the way Prov 16:17 לֵב Prov 4:23 the tongue מֵרַע Ps 34:14 with על, over the door of the lips Ps 141:3 of God נֹצֵר הָאָדָם Job 7:20 (thou) watcher of men (iron.).
2. Guard from dangers, preserve, with accusative, subject God or his attributes Deut 32:10; Ps 25:21; Ps 31:24; Ps 40:12; Ps 61:8; Isa 26:3; Isa 42:6; Isa 49:8; Prov 2:8; Prov 20:28 ; Prov 22:12 נֹצֵר נֶפֶשׁ Prov 24:12 with accusative and מן from which Ps 12:8; Ps 32:7; Ps 64:2; Ps 140:2; Ps 140:5. In Wisdom Literature subject is abstract: חבמה Prov 4:6 תבונה Prov 2:11 צדקה Prov 13:6 נְצוּרֵי ישׂראל Isa 49:6 preserved of Israel.
3. Guard with fidelity, keep, observe: of ׳ י, נֹצֵר חֶסֶד לאלפים Exod 34:7 ( J ); elsewhere of man observing the covenant Deut 33:9 (poem) Ps 25:10, the divine law Ps 78:7; Ps 105:45; Ps 119:2; Ps 119:22 ; Ps 119:33; Ps 119:34; Ps 119:56; Ps 119:69; Ps 119:100; Ps 119:115; Ps 119:129 ; Ps 119:145 commands of parents Ps 60:20; Ps 28:7 and discipline of Wisdom Prov 3:1; Prov 3:21; Prov 4:13; Prov 5:2.
4. Guard, keep secret, dubious: נְצֻרוֺת secret things Isa 48:6 נְצוּרִים secret places Isa 65:4 (see forms above); נְצֻרַת לֵב secret, wily minded Prov 7:10 (of harlot, so RVm close, i.e. secretive).
5. Kept close, blockaded, dubious: הַנָּצוּר the blockaded (so Ew Hi Co Toy; but Ke Bth Kau preserved; Hi Co Bth Toy strike out והנשׁאר) Ezek 6:12 עִיר נְצוּרָה blockaded city Isa 1:8 (see form above); נֹצְרִים blockaders Jer 4:16 (but 𝔊 συστροφαι = צררים i.e. foes).
