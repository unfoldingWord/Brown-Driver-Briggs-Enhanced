Biblical Hebrew

לִבְנָה proper name [of a location]

1. city in southwest Judah, exact site unknown, captured by Joshua, according to D , Josh 10:29 (twice in verse); Josh 10:31; Josh 10:32; Josh 10:39; Josh 12:15 (all D ), Josh 15:42 ( P ); Levitical city Josh 21:13 ( P ) = 1Chr 6:42 further 2Kgs 8:22 = 2Chr 21:10; 2Kgs 19:8 = Isa 37:8; 2Kgs 23:31; 2Kgs 24:18 = Jer 52:1 𝔊 Λεβνα. See LagOnom. 274, 135, 26; 2nd ed. 273 Λεβνα; BuhlGeogr. 193.
2. station of Israel in wilderness, between רִמֹּן פָּ֑רֶץ and רִסָּה Num 33:20; Num 33:21 (perhaps = לָבָן Deut 1:1); 𝔊 Λεμωνα, A 𝔊 L Λεβωνα.
