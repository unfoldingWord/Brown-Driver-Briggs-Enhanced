BDB867 [H871]

Biblical Hebrew

אֲתָרִים proper name [of a location] only Num 21:1 ׳ דֶּרֶח הָא; so ᵐ5 , perhaps (Di) name of a caravan-route, compare vestige, footprint; others (after ᵑ7 ᵑ6 ) translate way of the spies (compare Num 13:22); but הָאֲתָרִים for הַתָּרִים is highly improbable, and a locality would hardly receive its designation from the spies.
