רָפָא verb heal (Late Hebrew in derivatives; Phoenician רפא, Syriac heal; on Aramaic proper name compounded with רפא see NöZMG xi (1886), 723 Lzb369; Sabean proper name אלרפא Langer1, 1 DHMZMG xxxvii (1883), 326; Arabic (and ) darn, mend, repair, pacify; Ethiopic stitch together, mend; compare KüchenmeisterZWiss. Th. xxx (1887), 257 ff.); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular consecutive ׳ וְר Isa 6:10, suffix וּרְפָאָם Isa 19:22, 1 singular suffix consecutive וּרְפָאתִיו Isa 57:19, etc.; Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִרְפָּא 2Kgs 20:8 +, suffix יִרְפָּאֵנוּ Hos 6:1, 1 singular אֶרְפָּא Hos 14:5 +, אֶרְפָּה (Ges«GKC:75pp»§ 75pp) Jer 3:22 3 feminine plural תִּרְפֶּינָה (Idib. qq) Job 5:18, etc.; Imperative masculine singular רְפָא Num 12:13, רְפָה (Idib. pp) Ps 60:4, רְפָאָ֫ה Ps 41:5, etc.; Infinitive absolute רָפוֺא Isa 19:22 construct לִרְפֹּא Hos 5:13, etc.; Participle רֹפֵא 2Kgs 20:5 +, etc.; — heal:
1. literally,
a. of God, with accusative of person Gen 20:17 ( E ), Ps 107:20 (suffix), with ל person Num 12:13 ( J E ) 2Kgs 20:5 ; 2Kgs 20:8 .
b. of men, absolute Eccl 3:3 (opposed to הרג); participle as substantive healer, physician, Gen 50:2 (twice in verse) ( J ) 2Chr 16:12.
2. figurative, heal hurts of nation, involving ׳ י's (restored) favour (and, often, forgiveness):
a. subject ׳ י, with suffix person, Hos 6:1; Hos 11:3 (yet compare We Now), Exod 15:26 ( J ), Isa 19:22b Isa 57:18; Isa 57:19; Jer 33:6; Ps 30:3 , compare Ps 6:3 with accusative עַם 2Chr 30:20 , with accusative אֶרֶץ 2Chr 7:14 with ל of nation Hos 7:1, and (indefinite subject) Isa 6:10 (lest) one heal them (= passive, they be healed); with accusative of hurt, מִחַץ מַכָּתוֺ Isa 30:26 , שְׁבָרִים (of land) Ps 60:4, so מְשׁוּבָה Hos 14:5; Jer 3:22 (including the consequences of backsliding); with ל of hurt Ps 103:3 accusative nation (personified) + מִן of hurt Jer 30:17 I will heal thee of thy wounds (|| אֶרְפָּאֵךְ); absolute Isa 19:22 a (opposed to נגף), Deut 32:39 (opposed to מחץ); participle as substantive healer, physician Jer 8:22.
b. human subject, with ל of nation, Hos 5:13, compare Lam 2:13.
3.
a. figurative, heal individual distresses, with suffix person Jer 17:14 , with accusative נַפְשִׁי = me Jer 41:5, with ל person, לִשְׁבוּרֵי לֵב Ps 147:3 (i.e. longing exiles, compare Isa 61:3); absolute Job 5:18 (opposed to מחץ); participle as substantive Job 13:4.
