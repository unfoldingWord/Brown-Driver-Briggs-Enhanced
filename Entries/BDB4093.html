I. כְּנַ֫עַן90 proper name, masculine and [of a territory] Canaan ( 𝔊 Ξανααν, Phoenician כנען = Phoenicia; Ξνα = כנע , Hecataeus, see MüllerFr.Hist.Gr.i,17 and others; Egyptian Ka-n-±-na WMMAs.u.Europa,205 ff.; Tel Amarna Kina—na, Kina——i , etc., BezBM Tablets, 150 WklTA 39; etymology dubious GFMPAOS Oct. 1890. lxvii ff.; see also GASmGeogr. 4 f. BuhlGeogr. § 42); —

1. as proper name, masculine, son of Ham Gen 9:18; Gen 9:22; Gen 9:25; Gen 9:27; Gen 10:15 (as ancestor of Canaanites and Phoenicians; all J ); Gen 10:6 ( P ); 1Chr 1:8; 1Chr 1:13 (from Gen 10:6; Gen 10:15).
2.
a. land, west of Jordan, into which Hebrews came, and where they settled, subduing the inhabitants; מַלְכֵי כְּנַעַן Judg 5:19, ׳ מלך כ Judg 4:2; Judg 4:23; Judg 4:24 (twice in verse); hence ׳ מַמְלְכוֺת כ Ps 135:11 ׳ ישְׁבֵי כ Exod 15:15 (song in E ); compare ׳ מִלְחֲמוֺת כ Judg 3:1 ׳ עֲצַבֵּי כ Ps 106:38 idols of Canaan, i.e. of the former inhabitants; especially ׳ אֶרֶץ (ֿ) כ, ׳ אַרְצָה כ Gen 44:8; Gen 46:31; Gen 47:1; Gen 47:4; Gen 47:13; Gen 47:14; Gen 47:15; Gen 50:5; Gen 50:13 (all J ), Gen 35:6 ; Gen 42:5; Gen 42:7; Gen 42:13; Gen 42:29; Gen 42:32; Gen 45:17; Gen 45:25; Josh 24:3 (all E ), Gen 11:31 ; Gen 12:5 (twice in verse); Gen 13:12 (opposed to עָרֵי הַכִּכָּר vb), Gen 16:3; Gen 17:8 (all P ) + 40 t. P , Judg 21:12 ; 1Chr 16:18 = Ps 105:11 also ׳ הָאָרֶץ כ Num 34:2 ( P ); ׳ בְּנוֺת כ Gen 28:1 daughters of Canaan = women of the land, so Gen 28:6 Gen 28:8; Gen 36:2 (all P ); כנען is personified Hos 12:8 = apostate Israel; ׳ שְׂפַת כ Isa 19:18 = the Hebrew language (without evil implication).
b. the coast, especially Phoenicia Isa 23:11 compare אֶרֶץ פְּלִשְׁתִּים ׳ כ Zeph 2:5.
