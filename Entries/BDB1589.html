גָּזֵל noun [masculine] robbery — absolute גָּזֵל Lev 5:21 +3 t. + Ezek 18:18 compare below; construct גֵּזֶל Eccl 5:7; Ezek 18:18 (but compare below); — robbery Lev 5:21 ( P ) Ps 62:11 (|| עשֶׁק) Isa 61:8 = thing plundered, taken as plunder, as plunder, accusative of cognate meaning with verb Ezek 22:29 (גָּזַל גָּזֵל); figurative גֵּזֶל משפט = wresting of justice (compare Isa 10:2) Eccl 5:7. — Ezek 18:18 has גֵּזֶל אָח, but 𝔊 Co strike out אח; read then גָּזֵל. — compare LagBN 172.
