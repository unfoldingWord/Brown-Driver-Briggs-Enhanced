BDB8091 [H7518]

BDB8090 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8092

[רַץ] noun [masculine] usually piece, bar (?), in phrase מִתְרַפֵּס בְּרַצֵי כָ֑סֶף Ps 68:31, but very obscure and dubious; Aq ᵑ9 wheels; ᵐ5 Symm צְרֻפֵי; Che בְּבִצְרֵי כסף, or ׳בְּבֶצֵע כ, or (JBLxi (1892), 125) ׳בְּרֹצֵי כ (i.e. mercenaries); Pott We בְּרֹצֵי כָזָב; Du בְּרֹצֵי סִפְּךָ.
