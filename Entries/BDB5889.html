Biblical Hebrew

II. מָעוֺן proper name

1. location in Judah Josh 15:55 ( P ), 1Sam 25:2 ( 𝔊 Μααν, Μαων), now Ma`în ( BuhlGeogr. 163 and references), 8 miles south of Hebron; hence ׳ מִדְבַּר מ 1Sam 23:24; 1Sam 23:25 (twice in verse), so read also 1Sam 25:1 (for ᵑ0 פארן) 𝔊 Th We Dr Bu Klo Kit Löhr; compare
2. masculine in Judah 1Chr 2:45 (twice in verse), 𝔊 Μεων, Μαων.
3. of a people Judg 10:12 named with Sidonians and Amalek as ancient foes of Israel; possibly intended by writer as = מְעוּנִים q. v.; many read מִדְיָן ( 𝔊 B A 𝔊 L Μαδιαμ); see GFM. — מְעוֺן see בֵּית בַּעַל מְעוֺן; 1Chr 4:41 see מְעוּנִים .
