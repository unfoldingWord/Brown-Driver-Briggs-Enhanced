Biblical Hebrew

II. נָהַר verb shine, beam (Aramaic) (Late Hebrew id., especially in derivatives; Assyrian nûru , light, DlHWB 440; Arabic daytime, day, daytime; Aramaic shine, נְהַר id.; Palmyrene נהירא illustrious, e.g. VogPalm. No. 22); —

Qal Perfect 2 feminine singular consecutive וְנָהַרְתְּ Isa 60:5 3 plural נָהָ֑רוּ Ps 34:6 — beam, be radiant, figurative of joy: ׳ תִּרְאִי וְנ Isa 60:5, ׳ הִבִּיטוּ אֵלָיו וְנ Ps 34:6 (read ׳ הַבּ, וּנְהָרוּ, see ᵐ5 ᵑ6 ᵑ9 Che Bae Du).
