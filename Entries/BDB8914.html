BDB8914 [H8169]

Biblical Hebrew

שַׁעַלְבִים proper name [of a location] (perhaps haunt of foxes; Arabic , Assyrian šêlibu , fox); — in (original) territory of Dan, Judg 1:35 (compare GFM, on etymology and site), 1Kgs 4:9, Θαλαβεειν, Σαλαβειμ, Judges also αἱ ἀλώπεκες, as doublet, or ( A 𝔊 L ) alone; = שַׁעֲלַבִּין Josh 19:42, Σα(α)λαβειν.

שַׁעֲלַבִּין see foregoing.
