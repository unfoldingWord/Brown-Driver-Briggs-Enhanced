Biblical Hebrew

II. [חַת] adjective shattered, dismayed — masculine plural חַתִּים: —

1. shattered ׳ קֶשֶׁת גִּבֹּרִים ח 1Sam 2:4 (song; on plural חַתִּים see Dr); so figurative Ezek 32:30 according to 𝔊 Manuscripts Co (חַתִּים for ᵑ0 חִתִּיתָם) broken in their might, of Sidonians in She'ôl.
2. dismayed Jer 46:5 of Egyptians defeated by Nebuchadrezzar.
