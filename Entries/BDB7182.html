Biblical Hebrew

מִצְפָּה38 proper name [of a location] (properly outlook-point or -height ; with article (exceptHos Josh 5:1)); —

1. in Gilead, explanation as name given to Laban's cairn, north of Jabbok Gen 31:49 (on text see Ball Holz; 𝔊 ἡ ὅρασις).
2. probably south of Jabbok, Judg 10:17 (ἡ σκοπιά), Judg 11:11; Judg 11:34 (Μασσηφα; so 𝔊 L Judg 10:17), Hos 5:1 (ἡ σκοπιά ); apparently = II. מִצְפֶּה 3. BuhlGeogr. 262 (פֶּה-, פָּה-, seem to interchange); site unknown; see conjectures in Buhll.c., compare GASmGeogr. 586.
3. near Hermon, ׳ אֶרֶץ הַמּ Josh 11:3, Μασευμαν, Μασσηφα(θ), = בִּקְעַתמִֿצְפֶּה Josh 11:8, Μασσωχ, Μασσηφα; see BuhlGeogr. 240.
4. old sacred place in Benjamin, Μασσηφα, etc.; — ׳ הַמּ Judg 20:1 +, הַמִּצְפָּ֫תָה 1Sam 7:5 +; — Judg 20:1; Judg 20:3; Judg 21:1; Judg 21:5; Judg 21:8; 1Sam 7:5 + 7 t. 1Sam 7; 1Sam 10; 1Kgs 15:22 = 2Chr 16:6; 2Kgs 25:23 ; 2Kgs 25:25 ; Jer 40:10 + 12 t. 40, 41; Neh 3:7 ; Neh 3:15; Neh 3:19 = הַמִּצְפֶּה Josh 18:26 — modern Nabî Samwîl, 5 miles northwest of Jerusalem, see BuhlGeogr. 167 f..
