מִגְדָּל noun masculine tower, Gen 11:5 + 11 t.; construct מִגְדַּל Judg 8:17 + 21 t.; plural מִגְדָּלִים 2Chr 26:9 + 6 t.; מִגְדָּלֶיהָ Ps 48:12; Ezek 26:4 pluralf. מִגְדָּלוֺת 2Chr 32:5 + 2 t.; construct מִגְדְּלוֺת Song 5:13 (?); suffix מִגְדְּלֹתַיִךְ Ezek 26:9; Ezek 27:11 —

1. tower Gen 11:4; Gen 11:5; Judg 8:9; Judg 9:51 (twice in verse); Judg 9:52 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 9:17; 2Kgs 17:9; 2Kgs 18:8; 1Chr 27:25; 2Chr 14:6; 2Chr 26:9; 2Chr 26:10 ; 2Chr 26:11 ; 2Chr 27:4 ; 2Chr 32:5 ; 2Chr 30:25 ; 2Chr 33:18; 2Chr 27:11 ; Ps 48:13 , watch-tower in vineyard Isa 5:2 figurative of God as refuge Ps 61:4; Prov 18:10 beautiful neck like tower of David Song 4:4 an ivory tower Song 7:5 breasts Song 8:10 . 2. elevated stage, pulpit of wood Neh 8:4.
