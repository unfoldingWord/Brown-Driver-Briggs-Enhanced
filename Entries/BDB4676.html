מִלְכָּה proper name, feminine ᵐ5 Μελχα (= מַלְכָּה ? NöZMG xlii. 1888. 484 compare Phoenician proper name, of deities מלכת; compare GrayProp. N. 116); —

1. wife of Nahor Gen 11:29 (twice in verse); Gen 22:20; Gen 22:23; Gen 24:15; Gen 24:24; Gen 24:47 (all J ).
2. daughter of Zelophehad (of Manasseh), Num 26:33; Num 27:1; Num 36:11; Josh 17:3 (all P ). — Perhaps original town-name, Grayl.c.
