BDB6946 [H6667]

Biblical Hebrew

צִדְקִיָּ֫הוּ56, יָּ֑ה7 proper name, masculine (׳ י is righteousness; compare Sabean צדקאל DHMib., on No. 32); — σεδεκια(ς):

1. last king of Judah, מַתַּנְּיָה, changed to ׳ ׃צ יָּ֑֫הוּ 2Kgs 24:17; 2Kgs 24:18; 2Kgs 24:20; 2Kgs 25:2; 2Kgs 25:7 (twice in verse) = 2Chr 36:10; 2Chr 36:11; 1Chr 3:15; Jer 1:3 ; Jer 21:1; Jer 21:3; Jer 21:7 + 37 t. Jeremiah; יָּ֑ה Psalms; Jer 27:12; Jer 28:1; Jer 29:3; Jer 49:34.
2. false prophets:
a. under Ahab, יָּ֑֫הוּ 1Kgs 22:24 = 2Chr 18:10 ; 2Chr 18:23 יָּ֑ה 1Kgs 22:11 .
b. יָּ֑֫הוּ Jeremiah's time, Jer 29:21; Jer 29:22.
3. יָּ֑֫הוּ prince, Jeremiah's time, Jer 36:12.
4. יָּ֑ה, priest, Nehemiah's time, Neh 10:2 ( ᵐ5 L Σεχενιας).
5. יָּ֑ה, son of Jehoiachin, according to 1Chr 3:16, but probably gloss, Be SS, compare Kit.
