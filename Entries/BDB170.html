[אוֺדָה] noun feminine cause (perhaps originally circumstance, compare Sabean אוד enclosing wall) — only plural אוֺדֹת Gen 21:11 +; אֹדוֺת Num 12:1 +; (8 t. + 2Sam 13:16 see infra); אֹדוֺתַי Josh 14:6 אֹדוֺתֶיךָ ib.; — cause, always with על, & construct, except Jer 3:8, where followed by אֲשֶׁר; ׳ עַלאֿ because of Gen 21:11; Gen 21:25; Exod 18:8; Num 12:1; Num 13:24; Judg 6:7; Jer 3:8 = concerning (on occasion of) Gen 26:32; Josh 14:6 (twice in verse); in 2Sam 13:16 read, for אלאֹֿדוֺת, אַל אָחִי כִּי ᵐ5 L It.
