Biblical Hebrew

III. מַסָּה proper name [of a location] where Israel tried ׳ י in the wilderness, Exod 17:7 ( J E ), Deut 6:16 ; Deut 9:22; Ps 95:8 (all ᵐ5 (ὁ)πειρασμός), where Levi was tested Deut 33:8 ( ᵐ5 πείρᾳ).

See GallKultstätten 32 interprets as place of trial (ancient judgment-place) and identification with Kadesh, called also (מֵי) מְרִיבָה and עֵין מִשְׁפָּט.
