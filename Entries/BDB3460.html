BDB3459 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3461

יַדוּעַ proper name, masculine

1. a chief of the people, one of those sealed Neh 10:22 ᵐ5 L Ιεδδαυα.
2. a priest, probably the high priest in time of Alexander the Great Neh 12:11; Neh 12:22 ᵐ5 Ιαδου, ᵐ5 L Ιεδδου (whence LagBN 113 reads יִדּוּעַ ).
