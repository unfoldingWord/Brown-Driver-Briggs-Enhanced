[עָרַל] verb denominative count as foreskin, i.e. as uncircumcised; —

Qal Perfect 2 masculine plural, with accusative of cognate meaning with verb וַעֲרַלְתֶּם עָרְלָתוֺ Lev 19:23 ye shall regard its fruit as uncircumcised, and not eat it (for three years) (> SS remove its foreskin (its fruit), as ᵑ9 , compare ᵐ5 περικαθαριεῖτε τὴν ἀκαθαπσίαν αὐτοῦ).
Niph`al Imperative הֵעָרֵל Hab 2:16 be counted uncircumcised, i.e. be object of mockery; but dubious, read probably הֵרָעֵל reel ( ᵐ5 ᵑ6 We Now BuhlLex).
