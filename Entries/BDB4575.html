BDB4574 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4576

מַמְזֵר noun masculine bastard, specifically child of incest (Late Hebrew id. (see Levy), מַמְזֵרוּת incest; Aramaic (loan-word) מַמְזֵרָא bastard): —

1. literally bastard, Deut 23:3 (see Dr).
2. perhaps figurative collective, of mixed population Zech 9:6 ᵐ5 ἀλλογενεῖς (compare also GeiUrschrift 52f.)
