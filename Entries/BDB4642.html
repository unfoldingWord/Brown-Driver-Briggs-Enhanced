Biblical Hebrew

מָכִיר proper name, masculine 𝔊 Μαχειρ (Sabean מכר Hal4).

1. eldest son of Manasseh; — Gen 50:23; Num 26:29 (twice in verse); Num 27:1; Num 32:39; Num 32:40; Num 36:1; Deut 3:15; Josh 13:31 (twice in verse); Josh 17:1; Josh 17:3, compare 1Chr 1:21; 1Chr 1:23; 1Chr 7:14; 1Chr 7:15; 1Chr 7:16; 1Chr 7:17 see also Judg 5:14 (poetry) where = Manasseh.
2. son of `Ammiel 2Sam 9:4; 2Sam 9:5; 2Sam 17:27.
