אֵזוֺב noun masculine 1Kgs 5:13 hyssop (Mishna אזוב, Aramaic , Arabic Frey., Ethiopic ᵐ5 ὕσσωπος, herb of purging qualities, but perhaps not precise botanical term, see Di Lev 14:6 f; see also Löw93)

probably a kind of wild marjoram (origanum Muru) PostHast.
