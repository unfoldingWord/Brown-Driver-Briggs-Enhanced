BDB2102 [H3058]

Biblical Hebrew

יֵהוּא proper name, masculine (probably contracted from יהוהוא = ׳ י is He, compare יֵשׁוּעַ and references; cuneiform Ia-u-a COT 1Kgs 16:23; 2Kgs 9:2 (further SchrMBAW 1880, 277, ZA iii. 3 HptBAS i. 296, 329 Jägerib. 468); compare also אֱלִיהוּ) —

1. king of Israel who overthrew the dynasty of Omri ( 𝔊 Ειὀυ, A often Ἰηου, 𝔊 L Ἰου 1Kgs 19:16; 1Kgs 19:17 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 9:2 + 36 t. 2Kgs 9 & 10 + 2Kgs 12:2 ; 2Kgs 13:1 ; 2Kgs 14:8; 2Kgs 15:12; 2Chr 22:7; 2Chr 22:8; 2Chr 22:9; 2Chr 25:17; Hos 1:4.
2. prophet of Northern Israel in time of Baasha & Jehoshaphat ( 𝔊 Ειὀυ, A Ειἠου 𝔊 L Ἰου in Kings; 𝔊 Ἰου, Ἰησου, A 𝔊 L Ἰηου in Chronicles) 1Kgs 16:1; 1Kgs 16:7; 1Kgs 16:12; 2Chr 19:2; 2Chr 20:34.
3. one of David's heroes 1Chr 12:3 ( 𝔊 Ἰηουλ, 𝔊 L Ἰηουδ).
4. a Judaite 1Chr 2:38 (twice in verse) ( 𝔊 Ἰηους, A 𝔊 L Ἰηου).
5. a Simeonite prince 1Chr 4:35 ( 𝔊 οὗτος i.e. (י)הוּא; A 𝔊 L Ἰηου).
