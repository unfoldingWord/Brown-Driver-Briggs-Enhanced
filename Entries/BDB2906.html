II. חֹ֫מֶשׁ noun masculine belly (Aramaic ) — הַחֹמֶשׁ 2Sam 2:23 + 3 t.; ׳ וַיַּכֵּהוּ ֗֗֗ אלהֿח 2Sam 2:23 and he smote him in the belly; 2Sam 20:10 + 2Sam 4:6, read וַתָּ֫נָם וַתִּישָׁ֑ן and she slumbered and slept 𝔊 We Dr.; yet corruption difficult to explain, see Klo; ׳ וַיַּכֵּהוּ שָׁם הח 2Sam 3:27 (where probably insert אלֿ compare Dr).
