I. צָרַח verb cry, roar (Late Hebrew id., cry (of raven); Assyrian ƒarâ—u , cry aloud, Arabic Ethiopic ᵑ7 צְרַח Aph`el (of bear), Syriac especially Aph`el and derivatives ); —

Qal Participle active מַר צֹרֵחַ שָׁם גִּבּוֺר Zeph 1:14 bitterly roareth there a hero, but abrupt in context and improbable; Gr (in part after 𝔊 ) conjectures יִצְרַח כַּגִּבּוֺר ׳ קוֺל י (compare Hiph`il).
