Biblical Hebrew

יִבְלְעָם proper name [of a location] a city of Manasseh in West-Jordan land Josh 17:11 ( J E ; but ׳ יִב here dubious, see BuRS 13 f.), Judg 1:27; 2Kgs 9:27 so read also 2Kgs 15:10 for ᵑ0 קָבָלעָֿם ( 𝔊 L ἐν Ιεβλααμ; see Klo); = בִּלְעָם 1Chr 6:55, q. v.; read יִבְלְעָם also in Josh 21:25 (|| 1Chr 6:55), for ᵑ0 גַּתּ רִמּוֺן, 𝔊 Ιεβαθα ( 𝔊 L after ᵑ0 Γεθρεμμων, see Di; it lay about 13 miles east of north from Samaria, three-fifths of the way to Jezreel; modern ruin Bel`ame BdPal 228, compare SchultzZMG iii. 49; (Old Egyptian Y-b-ra-±a-mu WMM195).
