Biblical Hebrew

I. עֵ֫דֶר noun masculine Gen 29:2 flock, herd (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic עַדְרָא; on 𝔊 Γαδερ Gen 35:16 ( = HCT Gen 35:21) see LagBN 76 f., who assigns ׳ ע therefore, plausibly, to III. עדר ( ), as lagging, loitering); — ׳ ע absolute Gen 32:17 (twice in verse) +, construct Song 4:1 +; suffix עֶדְרוֺ Isa 40:11 +; plural עֲדָרִים Gen 29:2 +, construct עֶדְרֵי Mic 5:7 +, etc.; —

1. flock:
a. of sheep, צאֹן ׳ ע Gen 29:2 ( J ), Mic 5:7 , ׳ הַצ ׳ ע Joel 1:18 compare Gen 29:2 ; Gen 29:3; Gen 29:8; Gen 30:4 (all J ), 1Sam 17:34; Jer 51:23; Mal 1:14 || צאֹן Ezek 34:12 (simile), Prov 27:23 + בְּהֵמָה 2Chr 32:28 הָֽרְחֵלִים ׳ ע Song 6:6 = Song 4:2 (insert probably ׳ הָֽר); sign of desolation Isa 17:2; Isa 32:14 (מִרְעֵה ׳ ע), Zeph 2:14; Jer 6:3, of peace Jer 31:24 in simile also Mic 2:12; Jer 31:10 ; Ps 78:52 figurative of Israel Isa 40:11 ; Jer 13:20 (|| צאֹן), יהוה ׳ ע Jer 13:17, so עֶדְרוֺ Zech 10:3 .
b. of goats, הָעִזִּים ׳ ע Song 4:1; Song 6:5.
c. undefined Judg 5:16; Job 24:2; Song 1:7.
2. herds, flocks and herds;
a. including sheep, cattle, etc., Gen 32:17 (4 t. in verse); Gen 32:20 (compare v.Gen 32:14 all E ).
b. specifically בָקָר ׳ ע Joel 1:18 . — מִגְדַּלעֵֿדָר see above
