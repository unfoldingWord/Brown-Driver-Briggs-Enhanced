BDB4892 [H4968]

Biblical Hebrew

מְתוּשֶׁ֫לַח proper name, masculine descendant of Seth (? man of the dart: more probably Babylonian; see conjectures in HomPSBA 1893, 243 ff.); — ׳ מ Gen 5:22; Gen 5:25; Gen 5:26; Gen 5:27 ( P ), 1Chr 1:3, מְתוּשָׁ֑לַח Gen 5:21 ( P ). 𝔊 Μαθουσαλα.

מַתְבֵּן see תבן .
