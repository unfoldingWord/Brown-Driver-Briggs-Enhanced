Biblical Hebrew

I. שֹׁד, שׁוֺד (see below) noun masculine violence, havoc, devastation, ruin; — ׳ שׁ absolute Hos 7:13 +; construct Isa 22:4 +; —

1. violence, havoc, as social sin: חָמָס וָשֹׁד Amos 3:10; Jer 6:7; Jer 20:5; Ezek 45:2, ׳ וְח ׳ שׁ Hab 1:3, כָּזָב ׳ וָשׁ Hos 12:2 (1. וָשָׁוְא, 𝔊 We Marti a.), וָשֶׁבֶר ׳ שׁ Isa 59:7; Isa 60:18; Prov 24:2 (|| עָמָל;רְשָׁעִים ׳ שׁ Prov 21:7 with genitive object עֲנִיִּים ׳ שׁ Ps 12:6.
2. devastation, ruin, for nation Hos 7:13, כְּשֹׁד מִשַּׁדַּי יָבוֺא Isa 13:6 (simile of ׳ יוֺם י) = Joel 1:13; Isa 16:4 (Lo Gr CheHpt Marti שֹׁדֵד; || רֹמֵס), Isa 22:4, וְשֶׁבֶר ׳ שׁ Isa 51:19; Jer 48:3 more generally Amos 5:9 (twice in verse); for individual Job 5:21 (van d.
