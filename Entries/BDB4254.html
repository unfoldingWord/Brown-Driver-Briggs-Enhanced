BDB4254 [H3802]

Biblical Hebrew

כָּתֵף noun feminine Exod 27:15 shoulder, shoulder-blade, side (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic כַּתְפָּא , Arabic ); — absolute ׳ כ Zech 7:11 +; construct כֶּתֶף 1Kgs 6:8 +, app. כָּתֵף Isa 11:14 (but regard as apposition, or read כֶּתֶף); suffix כְּתֵפִי Job 31:22 כְּתֵפָם 1Chr 15:15 plural absolute כְּתֵפֹת Exod 28:7 +, כְּתֵפוֺת 1Kgs 7:30; 1Kgs 7:34 construct כִּתְפֹת Exod 28:12 +, כִּתְפוֺת Ezek 41:2 +; dual suffix כִּתֵפָיו Deut 33:12 + 3 t., כְּתֵפֶיהָ 1Kgs 7:34 —

1.
a. of man, shoulder, shoulder-blade (while שְׁכֶם , q. v. = neck and shoulders), כְּידוֺן נְחשֶׁת בֵּין כְּתֵפָיו 1Sam 17:6 compare figurative of ׳ י's dwelling between the shoulders of Benjamin Deut 33:12 (see Dr); כְּתֵפִי מִשִּׁכְמָה תִפּוֺל Job 31:22 my shoulder-blade, from the shoulder let it fall; as support for burdens Judg 16:3; Ezek 12:6; Ezek 12:7; Ezek 12:12 ; Isa 46:7 ; Isa 49:22 (in figurative), Exod 28:12; Num 7:9 (both P ), 1Chr 15:15; 2Chr 35:3 compare מְרוּטָה ׳ כָּלכֿ Ezek 29:18 every shoulder was rubbed bare (of Nebuchadnezzar's soldiers chafed by armour and toil); as aim of bird of prey Isa 11:14 (figurative); compare shoulder of Moab Ezek 25:9 (figurative, i.e. the side of M. exposed to invasion): Ezek 29:7b read probably כַּף ( 𝔊 . ᵑ6 Sm Co, compare va).
b. of beasts, Isa 30:6 (carrying), Ezek 34:21 (thrusting, in figure), סֹרָ֑רֶת ׳ כ Zech 7:11 a refractory shoulder (refusing the yoke, figurative), so Neh 9:29 of choice meat Ezek 24:4 (|| יָרֵךְ).
c. = shoulder-pieces (always plural) of ephod Exod 28:7; Exod 28:12; Exod 28:25; Exod 28:27 = Exod 39:4; Exod 39:7; Exod 39:18; Exod 39:20 ( P ).
2.
a. slope, side, of mountain, כֶּתֶף יָםכִּֿנֶּרֶת Num 34:11 , i.e. the mountain-slopes northeast of the lake, compare Josh 15:8; Josh 15:10; Josh 15:11; Josh 18:12 ; Josh 18:13; Josh 18:16 ; Josh 18:18 ; Josh 18:19 (all P ).
b. opposite side (s), that is, of door or entrance, of tabernacle-court Exod 27:14 ; Exod 27:15 = Exod 38:14; Exod 38:15 (all P ), of temple 1Kgs 6:8; 1Kgs 7:39 (3 t. in verse) = 2Chr 4:10; 2Kgs 11:11 (twice in verse) = 2Chr 23:10 (twice in verse); compare Ezek 40:18; Ezek 40:40 (twice in verse); Ezek 40:41 ; Ezek 40:44 (twice in verse); Ezek 41:2; Ezek 41:26; Ezek 46:19; Ezek 47:1 ; Ezek 47:2.
3. supports of the bases for the lavers beside the temple 1Kgs 7:30 (twice in verse); 1Kgs 7:34 (twice in verse).
