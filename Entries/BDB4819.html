Biblical Hebrew

מְרֵמוֺת proper name, masculine

1. priest, time of Zerubbabel Neh 12:3 (= מְרָיוֺת, q. v., Neh 12:15) 𝔊 L Μαρειμωθ.
2. priest, time of Ezra and Nehemiah, Ezra 8:33; Neh 3:4; Neh 3:21; Neh 10:6 (probably same). 𝔊 Μερειμωθ, Μεραμωθ, etc.
3. one of those who had taken strange wives Ezra 10:36. 𝔊 Ιεραμωθ, A Μαρεμωθ.
