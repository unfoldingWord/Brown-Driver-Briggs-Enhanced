פַּר132 noun masculine Gen 32:16 young bull, steer; — absolute ׳ פ Num 7:15 +, פָּ֑ר Ps 50:9, always הַפָּר 1Sam 1:25 +; construct פַּר (הַ)חַטָאת Exod 29:36 +; plural פָּרִים Gen 32:16 +; suffix פָּרֶיהָ Jer 50:27 — steer [see especially בַּר בֶּןבָּֿקָר Lev 4:3 + 6 t.; ׳ בֿ ׳ אֶחָד ב ׳ פ Exod 29:1 + 12 t. Num 7, compare Num 8:8 אֶחָד ׳ בֿ ׳ ב ׳ פ Num 15:24 + 3 t., compare Ezek 43:23; Ezek 45:18; Ezek 46:6 plural ׳ פָּרִים בְּנֵיבֿ Num 28:11 + 4 t.; see בֵּן בָּפָר]:

1. as gift Gen 32:16 ( E ); figurative of fierce enemies Ps 22:13.
2. elsewhere as sacrifical victims:
a. in peace-offering Exod 24:5 ( E ; Covenant Code), 1Sam 1:24 (read פַּר for פָּרִים 𝔊 ᵑ6 Th We Dr and others), 1Sam 1:25; Num 7:88 ( P ), 2Chr 30:24 (twice in verse)
b. burnt-offering Judg 6:25 (twice in verse) (on text compare GFM ), v 26, 28 1Kgs 18:23 (3 t. in verse); 1Kgs 18:25; 1Kgs 18:26; 1Kgs 18:33; Ezek 43:23 + 5 t. Ezekiel; Num 7:15 ; Num 7:21 + 46 t. Numbers ( P ); 1Chr 15:26; 1Chr 29:21; Ezra 8:35; Job 42:8; Ps 50:9 ; Ps 51:20 ; Lev 23:18 (gloss, Dr-Wh).
c. sin-offering Ezek 43:19 + 5 t. Ezekiel; Exod 29:1 ; Exod 29:3 ; Exod 29:10 (twice in verse); Exod 29:11; Exod 29:12; Exod 29:14; Exod 29:36 compare 2Chr 13:9; 2Chr 29:21; Lev 4:3; Lev 4:4 (3 t. in verse) + 25 t. Leviticus ( P ), Num 8:8 — both b. and c. Num 8:12
d. more generally Isa 1:11; Ps 69:32.
e. figurative פָּרִים שְׂפָתֵינוּ Hos 14:3 we will pay (as with) bullocks, our lips, but read פֵּרִי 𝔊 We Now, compare Che.
f. of princes and warriors slain by ׳ י, under figure of sacrifice Isa 34:7; Jer 50:27; Ezek 39:18.
