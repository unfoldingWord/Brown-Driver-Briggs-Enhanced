BDB5818 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5820

עֲדֻלָּם proper name, of a location old Canaanite city, ᵐ5 Οδολλαμ, with king Josh 12:15 (D), in the שְׁפֵלָה Josh 15:35 (P; ᵐ5L Αδαλαμ ), compare Mic 1:15; Neh 11:30 (re-)built by Rehod. according to 2Chr 11:17 (Οδολαμ); מְעָרַת ׳ע (read מְצֻדַת, מְצָדַת, see מְעָרָה below I. ערר) 1Sam 22:1; 2Sam 23:13; 1Chr 11:15 site probably I`d-el-Mîye (`Aid el-Ma), approximately 13 miles west-southwest from Bethlehem, see GASmGeogr.229 BuhlGeogr.193 and references.
