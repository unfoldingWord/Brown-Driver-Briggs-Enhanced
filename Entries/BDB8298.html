שָׂפָה176 noun feminine [masculine Exod 28:32, edge, compare AlbrZAW xvi (1896), 76], lip, speech, edge; — absolute ׳ שׂ Gen 11:1 +, construct שְׂפַת Gen 11:7 +; suffix שְׂפָתוֺ 1Kgs 7:23 +, etc.; oftener dual שְׂפָתַיִם Isa 6:5 +, construct שִׂפְתֵי Ps 12:4 +; suffix שְׂפָתַי Jer 17:16 +, suffix 3 masculine plural שְׂפָתֵימוֺ Ps 59:13; Ps 140:4; Ps 140:10, etc.; plural (in poetry and late) construct שִׂפְתוֺת Eccl 10:12, suffix שִׂפְתוֺתֶיךָ etc. Ps 45:3; Ps 59:8; Isa 59:3; Song 4:3; Song 4:11; Song 5:13 —

1. lip:
a. usually (c. 108 t.) human organ of speech (often || פֶּה, לָשׁוֺן rarely [only late poetry] חֵךְ: 10 t. Job; 25 t. Psalms; 45 t. Proverbs): (1) Isa 29:13 ; Lev 5:4 ( P ), Job 13:6 ; Mal 2:6; Ps 12:5; Prov 5:3 +; ׳ דַּל שׂ Ps 141:3 (see דָּל ); ׳ אִישׁ שׂ Job 11:2 = a talker, ׳ אֱוִיל שׂ Prov 10:8 = one talking folly, so Prov 10:10 ( ᵑ0 ; but see Toy); טְמֵא שְׂפָתַיִם Isa 6:5 (twice in verse) compare Isa 6:7 ; Dan 10:16 ׳ עֲרַל שׂ Exod 6:12; Exod 6:30 ( P ; unskilled in speech); ׳ עִקֵּשׁ שׂ Exod 19:1 ׳ פֹּתֶה שׂ Exod 20:19 see [ פָּתָה ] p. 834; ׳ מוֺצָא שׂ i.e. utterance, Deut 23:24; Jer 17:16; Num 30:13 ( P ), Ps 89:35, ׳ רוּחַ שׂ Isa 11:4 (of Messianic king), ׳ דְּבַר שׂ Ps 59:13; Prov 14:23 +, = mere, empty, word 2Kgs 18:20 = Isa 36:5 ׳ תְּבוּאַת שׂ Prov 18:20 , ׳ נוב שׂ Isa 57:19 (thanksgiving), compare Hos 14:3 ( 𝔊 ᵑ6 and most פְּדִי for פָּרִים); of flattery, ׳ חֵלֶק שׂ Prov 7:21, שְׂפַת חֲלָקוֺת Ps 12:3; Ps 12:4 compare שִׂפְתֵי מִרְמָה Ps 17:1, שֶׁקֶר ׳ שׂ Ps 31:9 + 4 t., שְׂפַת אָוֶן Prov 17:4 poison beneath (compare לָשׁוֺן) Ps 140:4 (figurative); ׳ פֶּשַׁע שׂ Prov 12:13 ׳ לְזוּת שׂ Prov 4:24 אֱמֶת ׳ שׂ Prov 12:19 , יָ֑תֶר ׳ שׂ Prov 17:7, צֶדֶק ׳ שׂ Prov 16:13 , דַּעַת ׳ שׂ Prov 14:7; Prov 20:15 ׳ חֵן שׂ Prov 22:11 שָׂפָה בְרוּרָה Zeph 3:9 רְנָנוֺת ׳ שׂ Ps 63:5 i.e. lips that shout for joy; with verbs: ׳ פָּתַח שׂ (to speak) Job 11:5; Job 30:20 (Gi Job 30:21), compare Ps 51:17 , ׳ מִפְתַּח שׂ Prov 8:6 ׳ פָּשַׁק שׂ Prov 13:3 i.e. prate; מָֽלְאוּ זַעַם ׳ שׂ Isa 30:27 ׳ שׂ subject of דִּבֶּר Isa 59:3; Prov 24:2 ׳ נָשָׂא עַלשֿׂ Ps 16:14 ׳ קָרַץ שׂ Prov 16:30 = backbite; ׳ אָטַם שׂ Prov 17:28 = keep silence, so ׳ חָשַׂךְ שׂ Prov 10:19 , כָּלָא ׳ שׂ Ps 40:10. (2) lips moving in speech, with נוּעַ 1Sam 1:13, נִיד Job 16:5. (3) transition to meaning speech, עָלָה עַלשְֿׂפַת לָשׁוֺן Ezek 36:3 = ability to speak Job 12:20.
b. organ of laughter Job 8:21.
c. of insulting grimace ׳ הִפְטִיר בְּשׂ Ps 22:8.
d. quivering in terror Hab 3:16.
e. feature of beauty Song 4:3 (scarlet).
f. place of bridle 2Kgs 19:28 = Isa 37:29 (see מֶתֶג ).
g. receiving kiss Prov 24:26 compare Song 4:11 and (in figurative) Song 7:10.
h. of divine speech, ׳ מִצְוַת שׂ Job 23:12, ׳ דְּבַר שׂ Ps 17:4.
2. language (see לָשׁוֺן ), Gen 11:1 ; Gen 11:6 ; Gen 11:7 (twice in verse); Gen 11:9; Ps 81:6 ׳ עַם עִמְקֵי שׂ Ezek 3:5; Ezek 3:6; Isa 33:19, ׳ לַעֲגֵי שׂ Isa 28:11 שְׂפַת כְּנַעַן Isa 19:18 (i.e. Hebrew).
3. edge: shore of sea Gen 22:17 ( J ) + 7 t.; bank of river Gen 41:3; Gen 41:17 ( E ) + 5 t., of wady (נַחַל) Deut 2:36 + 7 t.; lip (bank) of אָבֵל מְחוֺלָה Judg 7:22 compare GASmG 400; brim of vessel 1Kgs 7:23 (twice in verse) + 7 t.; edge of altar Ezek 43:13 of curtains (in pairs) Exod 26:4 (twice in verse) ( P ) + 6 t.; of חשֶׁן, toward ephod Exod 28:26 = Exod 39:19 ( P ); of round opening in robe (יִהְיֶה) לְפִיו ׳ שׂ Exod 28:32 = Exod 39:23 ( P ).
