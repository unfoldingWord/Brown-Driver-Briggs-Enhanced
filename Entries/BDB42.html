BDB41 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB43

אֲבִישַׁי proper name, masculine (my father is Jesse; LagBN 75 thinks from אֲבֶן (= בֶּן + ישׁי compare ᵐ5 Ἀβεσσα[ι] 1Sam 26:6) — ׳ א 1Sam 26:6 (twice in verse) + 17 t. 1 & 2 Samuel = אַבְשַׁי 2Sam 10:10 + 5 t. 1 Chronicles — grandson of Jesse; son of Zeruiah & brother of Joab 1Sam 26:6 (twice in verse); 1Sam 26:7; 1Sam 26:8; 1Sam 26:9; 2Sam 2:18; 2Sam 2:24 + 2Sam 20:6 (where read יואב ᵑ6 Th We Dr) + 12 t. 2 Sam + 2Sam 20:7 (where insert אבישׁי ᵐ5 We Dr) + 5 t. 1 Chron.
