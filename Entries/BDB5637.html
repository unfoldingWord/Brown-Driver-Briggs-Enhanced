BDB5637 [H5567]

Biblical Hebrew

סמן verb whence

Niph`al Participle נִסְמָן Isa 28:25 (si vera lectio) barley in an appointed place, or a determined portion (compare Ges Hi De and others), but ׳ נ lacking in ᵐ5 and plausibly taken as dittograph for כֻּסֶּמֶת by WeProl. 417; Geschichte. Israel i. 409 CheComm. and Hpt. Du; compare Brd GuKau.
