Biblical Hebrew

[קוֺף] noun [masculine] ape, so Vrss , 𝔊 πίθηκοι (omitted by 𝔊 B in Kings) (foreign word; probably = Sanskrit kapi , id.; Egyptian as loan-word gôfë WMMAs.u.Eur.95, gi'f ErmanZMG xlvi (1892), 121; Greek κῆβος, κῆπος is of Egyptian origin according to LewyFremdw. 6); — plural קֹפִים 1Kgs 10:22 = קוֺפִים 2Chr 9:21, brought to Solomon [from southeast] by Phoenician fleet; compare Arabic proverb 'donum regionis Jemen simiae sunt,' FreyProvv.iii.3160.
