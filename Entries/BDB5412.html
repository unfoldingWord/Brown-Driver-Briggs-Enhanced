II. [נָשָׁה] verb forget (Late Hebrew id.; Assyrian nišîtu perhaps forgottenness DlHWB 486; Aramaic נְשָׁא (especially Ithpe`el, Aph`el), forget; Arabic forget, neglect; compare Ethiopic ignoscere, condonare, indulgere Di633); —

Qal Perfect 1 singular וְנָשִׁיתִי אֶתְכֶם נָשׁאֹ Jer 23:39 I will forget you finally (but ᵐ5 ᵑ9 ᵑ6 Che Gie וְנָשָׂאתִי, followed by וְנָטַשְׁתִּי as Jer 23:33 b, see III. מַשָּׂא ); נָשִׁיתִי טוֺבָה Lam 3:17 I have forgotten prosperity; Imperfect 1 singular הַאֶשֶּׁה shall I forget ? i.e. overlook, read by We Mic 6:10 plausibly for ᵑ0 הַאִשׁ; Infinitive absolute א) נָשׁאֹ ׳ (ל Jer 23:39 (see above).
