II. נָחָשׁ proper name, masculine

1. king of Ammon 1Sam 11:1 (twice in verse); 1Sam 11:2; 1Sam 12:12; 2Sam 10:2; 1Chr 19:1; 1Chr 19:2 perhaps also 2Sam 17:27 𝔊 Ναας.
2. father of Abigail and Zeruiah 2Sam 17:25, si vera lectio: 𝔊 Ναας, but 𝔊 L Ιεσσαι (compare 1Chr 2:16); WeGeschichte. 2. 57 Anm. 1 Löhr defend נחשׁ; dubious WeSm HPS.
3. giving name to a city, ׳ עִיר נ 1Chr 4:12, 𝔊 πόλεως Ναας; 𝔊 L Ηρναας.
