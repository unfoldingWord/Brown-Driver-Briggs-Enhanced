拽址专职址肢执 proper name [of a location] conquered by Israel Amos 6:13 (so Gr We Now GASm, compare also 止 职指专 above; and most below 拽侄专侄 2); probably in Bashan, = 伪谓伪喂谓 1 Macc 5:43; 5:44, 伪谓喂慰谓 2 Macc 12:21; also in 壮 注址砖职转职旨专趾转 拽 Gen 14:5 (see III. 注址砖职转指旨专趾转 above).
