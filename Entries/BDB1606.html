Biblical Hebrew

גַּחֶ֫לֶת noun feminine Exod 1:13 coal (compare Assyrian gu—lu, a shining precious stone? COTGloss ZimBP 45) — absolute גַּחֶלֶת Isa 47:14 suffix גַּחַלְתִּי 2Sam 14:7 plural גֶּחָלִים Ps 18:9 + 6 t.; construct גַּחֲלֵי Lev 16:12 + 5 t. + Ps 18:14 (strike out 𝔊 De Che); suffix גֶּחָלָיו Isa 44:19, גֶּחָלֶיהָ Ezek 24:11 — coal, plural אֵשׁ ׳ ג coals of fire Lev 16:12 ( P ), 2Sam 22:13 = Ps 18:13 [also Ps 18:14 compare above] Ezek 10:2 (in vision); compare 2Sam 22:9 = Ps 18:9; Job 41:13, also Prov 6:28; Isa 44:19 in simile (of strife) Prov 26:21 (opposed to פֶּחָם black coal); Ezek 1:13 גַּחֲלֵי אֵשׁ (of the living creatures); figurative of hope in posterity 2Sam 14:7, of divine judgement, רתם ׳ ג coals of broom-plant Ps 120:4, compare Ps 140:11 , also (negative) Isa 47:14; Ezek 24:11 of kindness to enemy Prov 25:22.
