BDB4316 [H3839]

Biblical Hebrew

לִבְנֶה noun [masculine] poplar (apparently = Arabic ; Ethiopic styrax officinalis; but 𝔊 λεύκη, compare ᵑ6 and LöwNo.107); — Gen 30:37 לַח וְלוּז וְעַרְמוֺן ׳ מַקַּל ל as marking places of idolatrous incense-burning תַּחַת אַלּוֺן וְלִבְנֶה וְּאֵלָה Hos 4:13.

named from exuding milky (white) gum De Ges and others.
