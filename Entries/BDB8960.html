BDB8960 [H8203]

Biblical Hebrew

שְׁפַטְיָה(וּׅ proper name, masculine (׳ י judged, vindicated); — usu, Σαφατια(ς):

1. יָה-:
a. fifth son of David 2Sam 3:4 = 1Chr 3:3 (Σαβατεια, A Σαφαθια, 𝔊 L Σαφατιας).
b. contemporary of Jeremiah Jer 38:1 ( B Σαφανιας).
c. man of Judah Neh 11:4.
d. chiefs of families of restoration: (1) Ezra 2:4 = Neh 7:9; Ezra 8:8 (2) Ezra 2:57 = Neh 7:59.
e. Benjamite 1Chr 9:8.
2. יָהוּ-:
a. son of Jehoshaphat 2Chr 21:2.
b. hero of David 1Chr 12:5.
c. Simeonite 1Chr 27:16.
