II. חוּר proper name, masculine (meaning dubious; compare Nabataean proper name חורו Nö in Eutp. 45) —

1. ᵐ5 Ωρ, named with Aaron as companion of Moses Exod 17:10; Exod 17:12; Exod 24:14 (all E ).
2. ᵐ5 Ωρ, a Judahite Exod 31:2 = Exod 35:30, compare Exod 38:22 (all P ) 1Chr 2:19 ; 1Chr 2:20 ; 1Chr 2:50 (on divinity of verse see Be VB ); also 1Chr 4:1 ; 1Chr 4:4; 2Chr 1:5.
3. ᵐ5 Ουρ, a king of Midian Num 31:8; Josh 13:21 (both P ).
4. ᵐ5 L Εουρ ( B omitted) head of family of returned exiles Neh 3:9.
5. בֶּןחֿוּר officer of Solomon 1Kgs 4:8, see below בֵּן p. 122.
